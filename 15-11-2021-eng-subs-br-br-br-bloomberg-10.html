BREAKING NEWS.
Greek-Turkish relations.
GREECE. TURKEY. CYPRUS.
According to the exchange rate index of the Turkish lira against the US dollar, on the Bloomberg website, today is the worst day to date, after the exchange rate exceeded the barrier of 10 Turkish lira per dollar, and at the moment we are talking, at 15:30 Greek time, it is around 10.01, which is a historic low.
So now all the items imported to Turkey are very expensive and cant be get by the average Turkish citizen, who sees day by day, the purchasing power of the money in his pocket, constantly decreasing, since inflation has skyrocketed.
And what also made an impression today is that in his speech, Turkish President Tayyip Erdogan hinted, for the first time, that he might not be re-elected, thus meaning, that there would be a new government in the country after the next elections, something we do not yet know if it will boost the Turkish economy.
So far, however, the Turkish lira is literally "crumbling", and it will take much more, than a new government, to be able to return to where it was, several years ago.
