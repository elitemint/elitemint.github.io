RECIPES

Warm Chia Pudding
- 1 cup plant based milk
- 3 tbsp chia seeds
- 2 tbsp ground flax
- 1 apple diced (can also do frozen berries / banana…)
- Dash of cinnamon, cardamom (optional)

1. Heat milk in a small sauce pan
2. Add in all ingredients, stir
4. Allow to simmer for about 5 mins, stirring occasionally, until pudding consistency is formed
5. Add any toppings of choice (I like yogurt, figs & walnuts)

Fall Pasta Salad
- Veggies of choice (I did sweet potato, broccoli and brussel sprouts)
- 1 diced red onion
- 1 cup chickpea pasta (or pasta of choice)
- 1/2 cup dried cranberries
- 1/2 cup halved pecans
- Crumble of feta goat cheese

Maple Vinaigrette
- 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tsp dijon mustard
- Salt & pepper to taste
