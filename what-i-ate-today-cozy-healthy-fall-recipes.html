- 3 tbsp chia seeds
- 2 tbsp ground flax
- 1 apple diced (can also do frozen berries / banana‚Ä¶)
- Dash of cinnamon, cardamom (optional)

Fall Pasta Salad

- 1 diced red onion
- 1 cup chickpea pasta (or pasta of choice)

- 1/2 cup halved pecans
