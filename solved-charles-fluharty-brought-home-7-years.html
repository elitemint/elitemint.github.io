On this episode of Chaos Divers, we were humbled to bring Charles Fluharty home, ending the 7-year span of agony for his loved ones.

After an extensive two-day search with Adventures with Purpose, both teams moved to the next case in Pennsylvania. Requested to come back and remove a different vehicle by law enforcement, Chaos Divers took the opportunity to push farther on the cold case of Charles Fluharty.
Being able to locate and bring Charles home to his loved ones was an honor and a humbling experience that we will forever be grateful for and never forget.
