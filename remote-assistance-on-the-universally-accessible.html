One way to empower them is to make sure they make the most of the "windows to the world" that the computer means today. We have designed a computer usable whatever their sight or their age and a custom-tailored training and assistance so that they are not left alone in the learning curve.

With 300+ users in France, the proof of concept is over and the product,, available in 24 different languages, is ready for internationalization.
