Thousands of migrants are stranded in the cold in Belarus, at the border with Poland. As the EU discusses new sanctions, the Belarusian leader threatens to cut off the gas supply.
The situation in the border region has dramatically worsened during the past few days. The Belarusian leader Alexander Lukashenko is accused by the EU of having more and more people from crisis regions flown into the country and brought to the border. They are now stuck there. Large groups of migrants have tried in vain many times to break through the barbed wire fence system which Poland is using to try and keep them from crossing through into Poland and thereby the EU.
