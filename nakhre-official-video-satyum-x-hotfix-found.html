“Nakhre,” starring Satyum, Hotfix & Miloni Jhonsa, is a quintessential fresh, up-tempo party anthem portraying every millennial’s dream house party set to a catchy, alluring hook. The song concept revolves around a demanding girlfriend with the guy being head over heels for her and so he tries to keep up with her " Nakhre." The mood of the song is perfect for long drives, house parties, clubs and any festive occasion!

Satyum is a rising rapper and hip-hop artist raised in Mohali and Delhi. Being introduced to multi-lingual music genres, he developed a fluent style where he flows between Hindi, Punjabi and English and caters to emotions with his unique sonics. HotFix is an upcoming hip-hop artist from Pune and has been since almost a decade, HotFix is known for his versatile writing skills and witty bar fest which he creates on the mic.
