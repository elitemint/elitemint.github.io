It’s time for a BMW M3 vs Porsche 911 Carrera drag race!

These are two of the best performance cars you can buy for under £90,000. The question is, should you go all-out sports car with the 911, or pick the more family-friendly M3?

Starting with the 911, you’ll find a 3-litre flat-six engine under the bonnet that can deliver 385hp and 450Nm. It weighs in at 1,505kg and it’s RWD.

As for the M3, it’s powered by a 3-litre straight-six twin-turbo that puts down 510hp and 650Nm. So it’s up on power compared to the Porsche, but it’s also up on weight, tipping the scales at 1,730kg. And just like the 911, it’s RWD.

So what do you think - will the weight difference power the 911 to victory? There’s only one way to find out - LET’S RACE!
