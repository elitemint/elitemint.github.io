These students were expected to attend a series of tutorials by tuning in to the Youtube videos originally created by their teacher. The videos contained explanations and demonstrations of materials development and teaching techniques related to the curriculum in early childhood education. The instruments were questionnaire, open-ended questions and students‚Äô performance in assessment tasks.
