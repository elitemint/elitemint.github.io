Ayushmann Khurrana, who is busy breaking stereotypes in Bollywood, one film at a time, sat down for an honest conversation with PeepingMoon's Managing Editor Ankita Bhalla celebrating the success of Abhishek Kapoor's 'path-breaking' film 'Chandigarh Kare Aashiqui'. In the candid chat, the actor, who is known to contribute to the quality cinema with each and every film of his, opened up about taking 'baby steps' to highlight representation issues in India, the makers being slammed for casting cisgender actor Vaani Kapoor to play the role of a trans & being compared to Akshay Kumar in terms of initiating social concentrations through his movies!
