Ayushmann Khurrana, who is busy breaking stereotypes in Bollywood, one film at a time, sat down for an honest conversation with PeepingMoon's Managing Editor Ankita Bhalla celebrating the success of Abhishek Kapoor's 'path-breaking' film 'Chandigarh Kare Aashiqui'.
