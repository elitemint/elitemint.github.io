Supermarket.
Ripe, red tomatoes, sugary doughnuts, plump chickens, and boxes and boxes of cereals line the aisles at the supermarket. How do the stores keep everything fresh and tasty-looking? How on earth do stores keep track of all this stuff?Changing from season to season, adapting to people's needs over time, supermarkets are a big business and a big part of everyone's life.
