The show follows immigrants claiming UK welfare payments for their children, even though they live in their home countries. He heads to his home country Romania, to check how the renovation of his home is going. Evita gets benefits for her son living back in Poland, because the father of her child lives and works in Bristol. She owns her own restaurant so the benefits are used just for her son.
