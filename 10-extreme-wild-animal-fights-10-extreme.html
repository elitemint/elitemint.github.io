This is not for the weak-hearted people. One thing you need to know about crocodiles is that they are absolutely vicious animals. If some innocent animal does wander off there, it just might be their last day. In this video, a pack of wildebeest has found this river on a hot summer day. While drinking water, a crocodile attacks them out of nowhere and grabs one wildebeest by the throat. It is his last day on earth.
