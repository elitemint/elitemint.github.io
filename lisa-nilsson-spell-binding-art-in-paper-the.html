The Massachusetts Cultural Council (MCC) is celebrating "40 Years of Massachusetts Artist Fellowships" by sharing video interviews with some of the extraordinary artists who have received Artist Fellowships since the program's inception in 1975.

Creating intricate works using a traditional paper technique called quilling, Lisa Nilsson doesn't mind working slowly. But when her spell-binding art went viral, her career went through a swift transformation.
