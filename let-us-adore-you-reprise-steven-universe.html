Hello everyone! This week I sang Let Us Adore You from Steven Universe.

I’M BACK AND IT FEELS GOOD
January was a much-needed break for me and I did absolutely nothing other than play sims and catch up on some shows and books. It was fantastic. That being said, I’m feeling totally refreshed and more than ready to jump back into YouTube and making covers!
