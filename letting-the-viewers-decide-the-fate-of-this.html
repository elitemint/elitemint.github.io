The Corsa exhaust retails for $2000 new. I did not get any floor mats. Two keys and both work. All windows, locks and seats work. Rust free southern car so it would be a shame to subject it to salty winters but, hey, do what you want if you buy it. The engine mounts have been replaced already and I cannot confirm any internal engine work but someone has been there before.

All you will have to do is transfer the title into your name and register it. If you happen to be from Illinois, I will handle all that for you.
