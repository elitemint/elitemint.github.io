Hey Guys! Today we're going old school with a vanilla no mods/no DLC vortex campaign with Tyrion! This means no Free-LC, either! I wanted to get a version of the game with as much missing since we only truly appreciate some things when they're gone. Like unit variety! It should be a fun way to saw farewell to Total War: WARHAMMER II ahead of the games third instalment while revisiting the games oldest characters. An interesting challenge not being able to rely on certain units, too! There's plenty we'll have to do without, but our enemies have no such limitations :P

This LP might also prove useful for folks who've only just bought the game, but to them I'd recommend looking at the DLC page and also all the free content over on "Total War Access" since there's a pile of free content for this game that I'm not using here. Like seriously, there's another 9 free Legendary Lords just for Warhammer II, alone!
