If you relate to the following signs, you are mentally stronger than most people! Your resilience gets tested when life circumstances change abruptly for the worse, such as a diagnosis of a debilitating illness or disease, the loss of a job, or a relationship breakdown. Such hardships, however, allow you to rise above and become even tougher.

Mental strength is the capability to deal effectively with stressors, difficulties, and obstacles while performing to the best of your ability, regardless of the circumstances. Mentally strong people accept challenges and count their blessing instead of their burdens, which helps them keep life in perspective.

If life throws you a curveball, and you quickly get back up and adapt, then you are a mentally strong person. In fact, you are mentally stronger than most people!
