ProtaSteel allows you to perform automated steel connection design and produce high quality steel detail drawings in minutes. In this webinar, you will discover the new ProtaSteel and some of the great features;
• Macro Libraries
• Assembly Drawings
• Connection Calculation Reports
• IFC and Tekla Integration
• IntelliConnect for fully automating your steel connection design and detailing
• The Advantages of ProtaSteel
• The Future of Steel Design

Host: Syed Ateeq Ahmad, ProtaSteel Product Manager
