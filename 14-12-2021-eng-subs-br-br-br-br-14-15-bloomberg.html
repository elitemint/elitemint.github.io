Greek-Turkish relations.
GREECE. TURKEY. CYPRUS.
The Turkish economy is sinking into chaos, as the decisions taken by the Turkish government are pushing the exchange rate of the Turkish lira against the US dollar and the euro, to fall even today.
More specifically, at the time of writing this article, at 14:15, and always according to the Bloomberg website, one dollar is equal to 14,30 Turkish lira, which means a drop of 3.46% , from yesterday's closing price.
What is striking, however, is that so far no intervention has been made by the Turkish central bank, so that dollars can be sold and lira can be bought and an exchange rate improvement can take place.

Which means, of course, that there is no longer a brake on the Turkish lira.
Especially when today is Tuesday and Thursday, that is, the day after tomorrow, a further reduction of interest rates is expected to be announced, since the path chosen by Tayyip Erdogan is one-way.
And all this while winter is coming to Turkey and the citizens will need more money to stay alive from the cold.
