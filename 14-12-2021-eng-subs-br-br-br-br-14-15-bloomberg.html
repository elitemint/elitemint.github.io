Greek-Turkish relations.
GREECE. TURKEY. CYPRUS.
The Turkish economy is sinking into chaos, as the decisions taken by the Turkish government are pushing the exchange rate of the Turkish lira against the US dollar and the euro, to fall even today.

Which means, of course, that there is no longer a brake on the Turkish lira.
Especially when today is Tuesday and Thursday, that is, the day after tomorrow, a further reduction of interest rates is expected to be announced, since the path chosen by Tayyip Erdogan is one-way.
