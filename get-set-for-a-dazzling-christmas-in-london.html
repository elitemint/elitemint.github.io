Christmas has arrived in London! Shopping streets sparkle with seasonal decorations, magical Christmas venues open their doors and festive cheer takes over the capital.

Go ice skating in the stunning courtyard of Somerset House or embark on a spectacular festive adventure at Winter Wonderland.
