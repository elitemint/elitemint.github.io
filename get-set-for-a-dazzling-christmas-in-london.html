Christmas has arrived in London! Shopping streets sparkle with seasonal decorations, magical Christmas venues open their doors and festive cheer takes over the capital.

Share the joy of Christmas in London with family, friends or that special someone. Shop for gifts under the twinkling lights of Carnaby or browse the Christmas markets at Southbank Centre. Go ice skating in the stunning courtyard of Somerset House or embark on a spectacular festive adventure at Winter Wonderland. Stop for a delicious seasonal treat in Seven Dials or dine under the colourful lanterns of Chinatown.
