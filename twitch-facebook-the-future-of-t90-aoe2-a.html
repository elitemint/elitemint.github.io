I'm dedicated to the content on streams and videos going forward and want to continue to grow the game. Hope y'all benefit from listening in some capacity and are excited for the things to come. Whether it's on Youtube, Facebook, Instagram, or Myspace. I'll be working hard for y'all. Above all else, thanks!
