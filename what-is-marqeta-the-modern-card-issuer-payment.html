Marqeta is a modern card issuer and payment processing business founded in 2010 that is due to IPO in 2021, potentially listing with a valuation in excess of $15 billion. Jason Gardner founded the business 11 years ago to revolutionise the payments industry and bring credit card issuance, banking relationships, fraud prevention and deep insights all under one roof.
