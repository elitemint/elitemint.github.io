Skillta monitors demand for all employment skills around the world, making it easy for individuals and organisations to understand the changing dynamics of job markets. Our self-service insights platform and API provide rich insights to support key decisions.

Our aim is to enable individuals to make better career decisions, schools and universities to produce more employable graduates, recruitment specialists to achieve improved hiring and retention outcomes, and governments to focus their economic agenda on high potential areas.
