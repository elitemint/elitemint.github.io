Seldom definition (adverb) hardly ever

Seldom pronunciation: sel-duh m

Examples of Seldom in a sentence:

1. Taking cocaine is seldom credited with improving a person’s intelligence.

2. If you seldom wear an item of clothing, perhaps you should donate it to charity.

3. The police seldom cruise through the poorer neighborhoods in the city.

4. In the beach town, the temperature seldom falls below sixty-eight degrees.

5. According to legend, the Canadian Mounties seldom fail to get their men.
