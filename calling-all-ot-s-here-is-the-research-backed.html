Chances are if you're a school-based occupational therapist, you get referrals almost daily for messy writing. And if you're anything like me, you probably have tried every type of creative treatment out there to make messy writing better! This video goes over the EASIEST way to improve messy writing and it shows the research that backs it. It also touches a little bit on my journey as a pediatric occupational therapist, and how my treatment approach has completely shifted over the past few years. I hope it's helpful - enjoy!
