How to Start a Weight Loss Transformation!! Win Before You Begin!! This video pertains to week #14 of my 15-week Sexification 2021. I'm finally back to weighing under 300lbs which is very very exciting!! Lots of people have been messaging me via email, Facebook, and Instagram, asking what they can begin doing to lose weight, or how to start their own weight loss body transformation. I discuss the importance of first knowing why you want to lose weight and make small adjustments to your lifestyle and daily habits, why you must commit to achieving your goals no matter what, and how to win before you begin - train (exercise), strategize (nutrition), and dominate (believe in yourself that you can persevere)!! I hope some of you find this video helpful. Thanks for watching and all support!! Please ask any questions you have down in the comments!! Do you have any suggestions that could be added to this list that worked for you before starting your weight loss transformation??

0:00 - Introduction of How to Start a Weight Loss Transformation!! Win Before You Begin!!
1:25 - Know why you want to lose weight or accomplish whatever your physique goal is.
3:40 - Commit!! To succeed, you must be all-in and dedicated to achieving your weight loss goals!!
