How to Start a Weight Loss Transformation!! Win Before You Begin!! This video pertains to week #14 of my 15-week Sexification 2021. I'm finally back to weighing under 300lbs which is very very exciting!! Lots of people have been messaging me via email, Facebook, and Instagram, asking what they can begin doing to lose weight, or how to start their own weight loss body transformation.
