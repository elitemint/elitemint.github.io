How to Start a Weight Loss Transformation!! Win Before You Begin!! This video pertains to week #14 of my 15-week Sexification 2021. I'm finally back to weighing under 300lbs which is very very exciting!! Lots of people have been messaging me via email, Facebook, and Instagram, asking what they can begin doing to lose weight, or how to start their own weight loss body transformation. I discuss the importance of first knowing why you want to lose weight and make small adjustments to your lifestyle and daily habits, why you must commit to achieving your goals no matter what, and how to win before you begin - train (exercise), strategize (nutrition), and dominate (believe in yourself that you can persevere)!!
