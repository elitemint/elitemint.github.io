Those who tend to stay in their comfort zones tend to avoid life and Sadhguru says it is not wrong but it is against the nature of this creation.
Every creature on this planet is trying to be a full fledged life in its own way.
But we human beings are suffering the two best asset that we have ; our vivid sense of memory and imagination.
