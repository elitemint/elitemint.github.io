⭐The subsequent temporary application charge applies to certain temporary visas. This charge is payable by each person in your application and is based on their individual visa history. It is payable in addition to any other visa charges that apply to your visa application at the time of lodgement.

⭐In this video, our Australian Qualified Education Agent Counsellor Kristy (QEAC: Q238) will tell you more about how to calculate the subsequent temporary application charge.
