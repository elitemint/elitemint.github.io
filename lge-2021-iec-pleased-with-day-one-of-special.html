The IEC says it's happy with Day One of Special Voting. Just over 1.1 million people had applied to cast Special Votes. However, the IEC is concerned following a number of incidents since the voting process got under way on Saturday. Five people were shot and wounded in uMzinto in the KwaZulu-Natal south coast while at Camperdown 8 voting stations didn't open following a dispute over traditional leadership. But for more on this, we are joined by IEC Chief Electoral Officer, Sy Mamabolo.
