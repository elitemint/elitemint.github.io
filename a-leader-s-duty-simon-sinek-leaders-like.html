Leaders, like social hierarchies, have been around for tens of thousands of years. One thing has always been true: a strong relationship between a leader and their people is based on the trust that the leader will have their back.

Simon is the author of multiple best-selling books including Start With Why, Leaders Eat Last, Together is Better, and The Infinite Game.
