Amazingly the female was still able to feed during this 10 days with her mouth being "occupied" and full of eggs. She did however loose a bit of weight but still took bloodworms,mosquito larvae,earthworms,freshwater shrimp ,breadcrumbs and pieces of lettuce!

It would however not surprise me if she swallowed a few eggs or fry every now and again to keep her matbolism going...
