Session from @E2EVC Digital. In this session, you will learn an IT Services Methodology honed by over 20 years of industry experience and consulting. You'll learn how every project can be broken down into 4 iterative phases that everyone on your team can understand:
1) Understand
2) Plan
3) Change
4) Maintain
You'll walk thru a full lifecycle of a project that followed this methodology... and one that didn't. You decide which method you'd rather follow!
