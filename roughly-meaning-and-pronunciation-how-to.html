How to pronounce roughly?

This video provides examples of American English pronunciations of roughly by male and female speakers.

IPA Transcription of roughly is /rˈʌfli/.

Definition of roughly according to Wiktionary:
roughly is an adverb

As an adverb roughly can mean:
1. In a rough manner.
2. Unevenly.
3. Imprecise but close to in quantity or amount; approximately.
