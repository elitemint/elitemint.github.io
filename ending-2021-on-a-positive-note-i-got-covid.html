The numbers have stayed at aroud the 1000-2000 mark but these last few days (due to the Omicron variant) they have climbed to the 5000's. I was one of the unlucky ones who got it over the new year period! So I'll be home for that (And I'll be getting ready and having a little party by myself hehe)
