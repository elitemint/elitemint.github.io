- Requests will be selected amongst the highest rated comments and or by popular demand. Unfortunately these creations are very time consuming. This method allows me to meet demand more efficiently.

- Please consider the practicality of your suggestion to avoid any unnecessary disappointment. Anime characters and characters derivative of niche / stylised art styles are very hard to replicate well, to any degree.
