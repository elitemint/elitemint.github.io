Anadolu Efes Istanbul confirmed its good moment by downing AS Monaco 98-77 at Sinan Erdem Sports Hall on Friday. Both teams now have a 5-6 record in the Turkish Airlines EuroLeague Regular Season standings. Rodrigue Beaubois led the winners with 17 points. Shane Larkin and Adrien Moerman each added 14, Tibor Pleiss 12 and Filip Petrusev 10 for Efes. Donatas Motiejunas led Monaco with 21 points. Paris Lee added 18 and Dwayne Bacon 12.

One Team, Euroleague Basketball's corporate social responsibility (CSR) program , supported by Turkish Airlines as the One Team Founding Patron and with the collaboration of Special Olympics as Proud Partner, uses basketball to achieve real social impact in our communities.
