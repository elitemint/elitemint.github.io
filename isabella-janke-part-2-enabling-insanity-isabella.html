Welcome to part 2 of the Isabella Janke story and documentary. In part 1 we covered and focus primarily on Isabella Janke, her actions and her beliefs. Part 2 of the story explains how Sean and Gibi reacted to being spoon fed information from Bella and shows what they were both subsequently exposed for.
