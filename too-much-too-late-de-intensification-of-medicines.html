A University of Michigan and VA team published new results suggesting that more doctors and their older patients should be working together to scale back medications that can produce long-term benefits but may pose short-term risks in older people.

In this video, IHPI member Jeremy Sussman, M.D., M.Sc., assistant professor of internal medicine, explains the research.
