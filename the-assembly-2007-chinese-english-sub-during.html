When he was found by other PLA troops, he found nobody knows him or believes his story due to he was wearing the uniform of Nationalist reactionaries he stole while trapped behind enemy lines. After his recovery, Gu went back to the military with sense of guilt. He vows to prove the existence of his comrades-in-arms and to restore their honors.
