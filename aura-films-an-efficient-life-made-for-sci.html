Made for Sci Fi London 48 hour competition. 2015.
The competition is to make a film 3 -5 minutes long in 48 hours. You are given a title, line of dialog and prop/action you have to included in the film. Plus the rest of the crew DOP Jamie McMurry , Mark Mountford, Sound Marcel Glover ,Set Design Eileen Aldous , EditorTristan Syrett, Assistant EditorJenna Menzies , Sound Design Fraser Merrick, production assistants Vicky Ashford and Mandy Lo. Producer Roger Allen
