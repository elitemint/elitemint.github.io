It took more than a magic wand, but #Pakistani #university students transformed their 150-year-old campus into #Hogwarts this week as they kicked off a festival celebrating the fictional young wizard #HarryPotter. With its vast halls and soaring archways, Government College University in the eastern megacity of Lahore has long reminded #students of the magical school created by British author J.K. Rowling.
