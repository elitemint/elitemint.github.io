This little issue put a stop to my wanting to go any further before correcting this problem. I thought it would be an option in the View (from the top menu strip) but I couldn't find it there. I ran over the ask my best friend (aka Google) how the heck to get my design / source / split tabs back. I came across someone asking this on Stack Exchange but the answer was lacking.
