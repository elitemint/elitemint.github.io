How To Cook Garlic Buttered Shrimp

Ingredients:

▪️1 pound extra large / jumbo deveined shrimp with shells and tails
on
▪️Old bay seasoning
▪️Lemon pepper seasoning (Spain's Spices)
▪️Garlic and Herb seasoning (Spain's Spices)
•Garlic and Onion spice blend (you can find this @ Ross
Department Store)
•Parsley Flakes
▪️1 stick unsalted butter
▪️2 tablespoon minced garlic

Instructions:

1. In a bowl , season shrimp with all the spice seasoning.
2. In a skillet, heat stick of butter until it melts -make sure it doesn’t
brown
3. Add minced garlic and shrimp to the skillet and cook shrimp on
each side for 2-3 minutes- try not to over cook your shrimp
4. Remove from heat and serve this baby right on up! You will love
this recipe.
