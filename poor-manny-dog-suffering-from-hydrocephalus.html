Day1

Honestly, I was in a deep despair
because there was no specific medicine for this terrible disease.

Day7
Currently, Manny was condition was stable and was being treated according to a prescription.
However, the boy was completely uncoordinated because his head was showed many abnormalities.
To found out, the boy was taken for an MRI scan and the results: hydrocephalus - humoral fluid accounts for 60-70%.
Surgery was now impossible as Manny was body won not be able to take it.
he could sustain life for up to a year and Manny would be mentally and physically underdeveloped.
maybe when he grew up, those diseases would gradually be reversed?
day15
Manny was condition was not good these days, he kept convulsing

and then I lost Manny in my own little hands in the middle of the night.
This world did not deserve him anymore, I hoped he would be happy in the big heaven
