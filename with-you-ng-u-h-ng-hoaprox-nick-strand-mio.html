The original version (Ngau Hung) means a lot to me, especially because it opened up an opportunity for me to collaborate with Nick, Mio and the team to create a more completed version (With you). The MV is my journey to find a long lost treasure, during which I will travel through many picturesque locations (some have never been featured in MV/ filmes). Through this project, I wish to introduce these beautiful sceneries, the "hidden charms" of my country to the world.
