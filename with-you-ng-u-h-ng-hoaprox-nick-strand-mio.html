The original version (Ngau Hung) means a lot to me, especially because it opened up an opportunity for me to collaborate with Nick, Mio and the team to create a more completed version (With you). Through the melody, the lyrics and the MV, We want to focus on sending a positive message in this hard time, about a better future after the dark, as long as we unite and stick together.
The MV is my journey to find a long lost treasure, during which I will travel through many picturesque locations (some have never been featured in MV/ filmes). Through this project, I wish to introduce these beautiful sceneries, the "hidden charms" of my country to the world.
