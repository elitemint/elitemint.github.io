In a bid to reduce the unemployment rate in Nigeria, the German development agency, GIZ has sponsored eighty persons in hospitality service and is currently undergoing training at Amena Academy, Benin City.

Roseline Ebhoramen reports that the event featured the maiden inauguration of the Executive of Housekeeping and Stewardship Association of Nigeria in honour of Lady Amena Eghobamien of blessed memory.
