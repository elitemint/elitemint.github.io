This week, the top skaters headed to Torino for the 2021 Gran Premio d'Italia (aka relocated 2021 Cup of China). Anna Shcherbakova and Yuma Kagiyama completed impressive comebacks to win gold after rough short programs. There were also impressive performances from Papadakis and Cizeron, Sui and Han, Peng and Jin, Hubbell and Donohue, Stepanova and Bukin, Maya Khromykh, Loena Hendrickx, Mikhail Kolyada and Daniel Grassl. We also break down the latest injury news from Yuzuru Hanyu, Alexandra Trusova and Rika Kihira - and we debate what it means for the 2022 Olympic Games. Plus, the return of the Team Tutberidze Pyramid and Gabby Daleman and Madeline Schizas at Canadian Sectionals! It's another episode of 'This and That' you won't want to miss!
