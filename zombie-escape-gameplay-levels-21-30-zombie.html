Zombie Escape Pull Pin game

Zombie Escape is a fun, innovative and beautifully designed puzzle game where you select your hero and venture into the town that is battling a zombie apocalypse.

Pull the pins, move through elevators and break bricks as you try and eliminate the zombies one by one to solve the puzzles and uncover the path to goal. Each level poses unique challenges and is full of traps and objects that are fully interactive‚Äîpush the zombies into the fire, lure them into metal cutters, drop crates on their head and much more.

Every small aspect of the game is designed with great detail and quality. With beautiful effects for fire, water and splendidly rich and vibrant environments, the experience in Zombie Escape is one to behold!
