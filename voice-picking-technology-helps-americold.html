Americold is the global leader in temperature-controlled warehousing and logistics to the food industry, offering the most comprehensive warehousing, transportation, and logistics solutions in the world.

Implemented by Dematic, Voice picking has delivered increased accuracy and productivity for Americold’s Arndell Park DC, and transformed it into a paperless facility facilitating real-time, two-way data flow between workers on the distribution floor and the WMS. This allows Americold to easily evaluate business, quickly respond to customer volume fluctuations, and better manage labour levels.
