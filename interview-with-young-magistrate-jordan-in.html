In our interview with Jordan he covers a huge range of topics from what magistrates do, the attributes needed, how big the commitment is in terms of time, and how as a student he fits his studies around sitting on the bench.

Jordan is one of the growing number of young magistrates who sit in courts across the country. If you would like to find out more about being a magistrate you can go to https://magistrates.judiciary.uk/ where you will find additional information and links to current vacancies.
