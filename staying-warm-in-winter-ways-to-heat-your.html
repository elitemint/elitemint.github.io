For many people it is not a practical solution to use a propane heater or any type of gas heater in their vehicle. Especially if you are in a car or SUV- you don't have the clearance needed to keep things away from the heater. So what are some options?
Here are some alternatives to propane or gas. We will look at sources of heat such as: Electric blankets, 12V Blankets, Gas One Chafing Pan Warmers and Candle Lanterns.
Also, we will look at how to stay warm if you are not creating heat: Wool Blankets, Puffy Blankets, and Hot Water Bottles. We will be releasing an entire series this month on heat and how to stay warm in your vehicle! I hope you find these ideas helpful and that you find the best way for you to stay warm and cozy this winter!
