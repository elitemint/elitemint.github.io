In addition to the owners and Group Management, employees introduce the company and describe what makes EGGER special for them - from the first plant manager of the St. Johann plant Manfred Dittrich to mechanical engineering apprentice Alexander Dorfer. They talk about the multi-faceted history of our Group, past and current milestones and venture a glance into the future.
