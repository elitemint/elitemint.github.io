Beyond Ambient Light
A One-on-One Online Photography Workshop

The aim of this 1-HOUR long workshop is to develop your vision and style with the use of off camera flash.

I will discuss with you habits you should be developing before you actually click that shutter as well as the basics of off-camera flash photography, light manipulation and posing. Then finally, we will implement these learnings through what I call ‘DeConstruction’. I guarantee you it will be a fun way to learn and will be a critical step to improving your photography.
