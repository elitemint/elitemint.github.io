All travellers are required to submit the SG Arrival Card with Electronic Health Declaration within 3 days prior to their arrival in Singapore.

SG Arrival Card is an online system which replaces the paper Disembarkation/Embarkation Card, also known locally as the "White Card", which is no longer in use.

This instructional video guides you through the easy steps of filling up the SG Arrival Card. The step-by-step processes are slightly different across two subgroups:
1. Singapore Citizens, Permanent Residents and Long-Term Pass Holders
2. Foreign Visitors (Including In-Principle Approval (IPA) Holders
