The Drop of Perfume makes anyone within Victor's shriek radius Oblivious, meaning that they cannot hear the Killer's terror radius. Even if you increase the radius with Madeleine's Glove (+4m), this effect is quite trivial in most situations and is largely overshadowed by the much stronger Silencing Cloth add-on, which straight up makes Charlotte undetectable. That being said, if you send yourself to a good map and place Victor strategically... you can still get some interesting results. 😄
