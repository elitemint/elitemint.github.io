What’s up Vital MTBers, we’re going to walk you through our Shreddy-award-winning favorite e-mountain bikes as we head into 2022. While e-bike land is still a bit of the wild west in terms of technology and bike styles, we’ve chosen a heavy, full-sized ebike and a lightweight offering. Enjoy links to the full reviews below, so you can do more research to decide which e-bike might be best for you.
