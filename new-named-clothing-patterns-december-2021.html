I am so excited to bring a brand new sewing pattern range to Sew Essential - the Named Clothing range. This is an indie pattern company I have admired and lusted after for years and I am so excited we are now stocking them! There are so many super popular designs that I've seen so many people make and now we have them in stock I want to make them all. They are a Finnish sister duo who started the brand to make sewing and making clothes interesting, exciting and attainable for as many people as possible. In my opinion we can say they have succeeded. Their designs are super contemporary with clever design details and original ideas that make my sewist heart skip a beat. I've included my favourites in the video and paired them with dressmaking fabrics you could sew them up in, but there are more designs to check out on our site so do hop on and have a look. My picks are the Kielo wrap dress and jumpsuit, the Inari dress and T, the Reeta shirt dress, the Olivia wrap dress, the Helmi trench blouse and tunic dress and the Talvikki sweater. As always you can find links to everything I mention below.
