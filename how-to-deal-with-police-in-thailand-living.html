Living and working in Thailand as a tourist or expat, you’re bound to have a run in with the police. Whether it’s a motor bike accident, running foul of the law or even getting picked up after being drunk and disorderly. What is the first thing a foreigner should do? The Thaiger speaks to Ben Hart from Integrity Legal about the misconceptions and steps a foreigner should take if they get in trouble in a foreign country.
