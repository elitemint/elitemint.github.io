In the third part of the Holds Around The World Series, we continue our exploration into the history and current state of climbing in Denmark, this time with a look at how Urban bouldering played a big part in the climbing scenes of the 80's and how it is still relevant and practiced today.

Much in the way that climbers of the UK found alternative methods for training to go climbing in the mountains, the climbing scene of Denmark had to find similar ways to practice and train when climbing gyms had not become the normal practice that they have today...
