When it comes to gemstones, Pigeon blood ruby is the creme de la creme of the bunch. This ruby specimen gets its name from its resemblance to the blood tone of a freshly killed pigeon. They occur most primarily in the Mogok region of Myanmar and are hands-down the rarest and most expensive variation of ruby on the planet.

In this video, you'll come across some super incredible pigeon blood ruby high jewelry that has made its way to us from jewelers around the world.
