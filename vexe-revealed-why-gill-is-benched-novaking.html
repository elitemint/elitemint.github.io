Ahead the commencement of Battlegrounds Mobile India Series 2021, it has been revealed as per some sources that @GiLL also known as ‘ORGill’ might be benched by @OREsports .

@GiLL has been a part of @OREsports for a long time and the revelation came as a surprise to many fans who have been eagerly waiting for the upcoming BGMI India Series 2021.

As per the @Vexe recent youtube livestream who is also a part of the OR Esports @BATTLEGROUNDS MOBILE INDIA roster that Arshpreet Gill (OR Gill) has been benched by the organization.@Vexe has also commented that due to contract breaches, the organization has decided to bench Gill, although an official confirmation hasn’t been made yet.

There were various speculations among the fans that Gill might leave OR Esports and join Team SouL. Prior to this, Regaltos (Parv Singh) had revealed that he might have to look for other teams if he’s not getting to play with Team SouL. Team SouL’s consistently degrading performance during the recent tournaments had led to the rumours that there might be a change in the SouL lineup.
