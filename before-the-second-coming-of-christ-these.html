In verse 3 of Matthew 24, the disciples of Jesus Christ ask for the signs that they will see and know that Jesus is coming.

Matthew 24:9 Then shall they deliver you up to be afflicted, and shall kill you: and ye shall be hated of all nations for my name's sake. Many Christians have experienced persecution one way or the other because they acknowledge Christ as their Saviour. In their work places, homes, communities etc.The persecution varies from as much as murder to spreading of rumours, to hate all because they profess jesus as their Lord. These signs are visible in almost every part of the world.
Earthquakes and natural disasters are no longer news stories because we hear about them frequently. They appear more frequently than usual and even the countries of the world that have good emergency systems still record huge casualties.
In many countries of the world, there is internal conflicts, tussle for power, coups, unstable governments rebel gangs overthrowing the government, there are threats of civil wars and world wars.
