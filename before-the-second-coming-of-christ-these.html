The Second Coming of Jesus Christ is one that Christians all over the world have been expecting earnestly. The signs that were spoken about have already begun manifestation. The world has dipped into an unexpected low, economies are crashing,morals keep dipping.
The book of Matthew 24 speaks about the Second Coming of Jesus Christ. It tells all about the signs that when you see indicate the second coming of Jesus Christ.
In verse 3 of Matthew 24, the disciples of Jesus Christ ask for the signs that they will see and know that Jesus is coming.

Another sign that would happen before the second coming of Jesus Christ is persecution of the followers of Jesus. Matthew 24:9 Then shall they deliver you up to be afflicted, and shall kill you: and ye shall be hated of all nations for my name's sake. This verse of the Bible is one that has become a reality in our world today. Christians all over the world are being persecuted for their faith. Many Christians have experienced persecution one way or the other because they acknowledge Christ as their Saviour. In their work places, homes, communities etc.The persecution varies from as much as murder to spreading of rumours, to hate all because they profess jesus as their Lord. In Matthew 24:6 the bible says "And ye shall hear of wars and rumours of wars: see that ye be not troubled: for all these things must come to pass, but the end is not yet. 24:7 For nation shall rise against nation, and kingdom against kingdom: and there shall be famines, and pestilences, and earthquakes, in divers places" Hmm the bible here talks about wars, famines, earthquakes and pestilences. These signs are visible in almost every part of the world.
Earthquakes and natural disasters are no longer news stories because we hear about them frequently. They appear more frequently than usual and even the countries of the world that have good emergency systems still record huge casualties.
In many countries of the world, there is internal conflicts, tussle for power, coups, unstable governments rebel gangs overthrowing the government, there are threats of civil wars and world wars. All of these are enough to imprint fear in the hearts of people but 1 John 5:14 &15 say that "this is the confidence that we have in him, that, if we ask any thing according to his will, he heareth us: And if we know that he hear us, whatsoever we ask, we know that we have the petitions that we desired of him".
