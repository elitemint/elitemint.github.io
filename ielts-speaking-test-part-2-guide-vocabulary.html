This video is a part in a series that instructs the steps necessary to achieve a high score, between 7 to 9, on the IETLS speaking section question. This series will teach you the skills that will help you to be successful, confident and to reach success on these questions during the speaking interview. Follow the instructions carefully and make sure to practice. Use the subtitles as necessary to help you comprehend the information.
