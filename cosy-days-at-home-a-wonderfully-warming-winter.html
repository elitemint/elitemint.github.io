Individual chicken pies with winter roasted veg, WW RECIPE
5 smart points on my Personal Plan

Prep time: 20mins
Cook time: 55mins
Serves: 4

Using filo pastry instead of shortcrust makes this pie simple to prepare and reduces the SmartPoints too.

Ingredients (as per WW recipe)
Sweet potato(es), raw
400g, cut into 1cm pieces

Potato(es), Raw
200g, chopped into 1cm pieces

Parsnip(s), raw
150g, chopped into 1cm pieces

Thyme, Fresh
4sprig(s)

Rosemary, Fresh
4sprig(s)

Olive Oil
2teaspoons

Gravy granules, dry
40g

Chicken breast, skinless, raw
300g, cut into small pieces

Filo Pastry
120g

Instructions
Preheat the oven to 200Â°C, fan 180Â°C, gas mark 6. Put the potato, sweet potato, parsnips, thyme and rosemary in a large roasting tin. Drizzle with the oil and season with black pepper. Roast for 40 minutes or until tender and golden.
Towards the end of the cooking time, put the gravy in a large pan. Add the chicken, cover and simmer gently over a low heat for 6-8 minutes or until the chicken is cooked. Set aside.
Tip half the roasted vegetables into the chicken mixture. Use a potato masher to break them down, then stir through the remaining roasted veg. Spoon into 4 individual pie dishes, then top each with a scrunched-up piece of filo pastry. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the pastry is crisp and golden. Serve with steamed green veg such as broccoli or cabbage.
