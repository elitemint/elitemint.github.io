Toyota has released all prices of the new Aygo X. The playfully lined crossover in the A-segment is available from 15,995 euros. With Toyota Private Lease, consumers can start from 249 euros per month*. The new Toyota Aygo X will be available at the Toyota dealer from mid-April 2022.

Toyota previously announced that the new Aygo X would receive a starting price of under 16,000 euros and therefore keep its promise. Toyota supplies the basic version of the Aygo X for 15,995 euros. It stands on large 17-inch wheels and is equipped with LED daytime running lights as well as air conditioning and electrically operated windows at the front. For extra convenience, the rear seat can be folded down into two equal parts. The Aygo X has a maximum nose weight of 50 kg. The Aygo X is also available with a completely new S-CVT automatic transmission. It can be seen as a fully-fledged automatic transmission, something that is certainly not self-evident in the A-segment.

The second version in the line-up is the Aygo X Play, from 17,595 euros. In addition to the basic version, the buyer will receive a reversing camera and a 7-inch multimedia system with a USB connection, DAB +, and four speakers that also support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto. The exterior mirrors are in the color of the body and electrically adjustable and heated. The driver's seat is adjustable in height.

Toyota also supplies the Aygo X as 'First' for quick decision-makers. In addition to LED daytime running lights, this version is also equipped with LED headlamps and LED turn signals. Furthermore, fog lamps, automatic air conditioning, a rain sensor and privacy glass at the rear are also standard equipment. The prices of the Aygo X First start at 18,995 euros. The crossover line-up is completed by the Aygo X Envy (from 20,795 euros) and Aygo X Limited (from 22,595 euros), both optionally available with a convertible top (995 euros). Both versions are mounted on large 18-inch light-alloy wheels, with the 'Limited' always in the body color Cardemom Green with a black roof and orange design accents.
