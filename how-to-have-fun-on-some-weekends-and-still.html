How to "Have Fun" on Some Weekends and Still Lose Weight!! This is a check-in video regarding weeks #11 and #12 of my 15-week Sexification 2021!! I discuss how I went to Portland, Oregon to spend a weekend "having fun" with high school friends, and enjoyed myself while still staying in control of my calorie intake while eating and drinking higher calorie foods and beverages than I normally have while dieting. I then came back focused and motivated to work hard toward achieving my weight loss goals, dominating week #12. I sprinkle in a few mindset tips and questions I ask myself before events where I'll be eating or drinking higher calorie foods and beverages. I lost 73.4 lbs (33.36kg) during my 12-week Sexification 2020 program. Did I beat that number during the first 12 weeks of this year's weight loss journey? Thanks for watching this check-in video and have a great weekend!! There are a few more weeks remaining as part of this series, and I appreciate all support!! Let me know if you have a question you'd like me to discuss!!
