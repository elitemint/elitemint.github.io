How to "Have Fun" on Some Weekends and Still Lose Weight!! This is a check-in video regarding weeks #11 and #12 of my 15-week Sexification 2021!! I then came back focused and motivated to work hard toward achieving my weight loss goals, dominating week #12. I sprinkle in a few mindset tips and questions I ask myself before events where I'll be eating or drinking higher calorie foods and beverages.
