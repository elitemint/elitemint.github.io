Knit this fabulous oversized chunky knit sweater with me in this step-by-step video tutorial. I'll show you how exactly how to make this cozy bottom-up pullover in no time! No matter what your knitting level is, you can easily reference the chapters below to find out all of the information you need to knit this yourself.
