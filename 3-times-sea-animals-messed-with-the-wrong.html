Australia... A tropical paradise. But the oceans surrounding this island continent conceal sinister undercurrents. These pristine waters are home to some of the deadliest creatures known to man.
The Freshwater Crab duels with Tiger Leeches. It is a struggle to the death, with only one victor.
The Peacock Mantis Shrimp is a puny pugilist with a ferocious attitude. Unaware of the mantis shrimp’s presence, the crab strays close. Too close.
The Giant Triton is one of the ocean’s largest sea snails. The crown of thorns starfish seems to be one of its favorite foods. Giant tritons have developed a tolerance to the starfish’s toxins, and their saliva contains a paralyzing agent.
