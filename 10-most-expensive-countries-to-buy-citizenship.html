The Wealthiest people in the world tend to buy most expensive items such as fast cars, yachts, jewelry, art collection of paintings, historic artifacts, luxury villas and also passports. A second passport has become a very important insurance policy against global uncertainties or allowing visa-free travel to a greater number of countries and a must investment portfolio for the future of high net worth individuals. Currently, 46 countries run citizenship by investment programs or residency by investment programs also known as 'golden visas' as a backdoor to citizenship. In this video we will have a look at countries that provide residency through investment, which eventually leads to citizenship after residing in the country for a couple or more years. These are most expensive and time consuming passports to buy which could run from hundreds of thousands to millions of dollars.
