Currently, 46 countries run citizenship by investment programs or residency by investment programs also known as 'golden visas' as a backdoor to citizenship. In this video we will have a look at countries that provide residency through investment, which eventually leads to citizenship after residing in the country for a couple or more years. These are most expensive and time consuming passports to buy which could run from hundreds of thousands to millions of dollars.
