Hi guys! My name is Surina Taneja and I am a junior doctor in the UK. I have put together the highlights from my medical elective last year in Lautoka, Fiji. I spent my elective in the surgical department in Lautoka hospital which was an amazing experience and I learnt a lot about surgery. I explored Fiji thoroughly during my stay there and you can find out what I got up to in this video! Hope you enjoy the video and let me know if you use any of these recommendations!
