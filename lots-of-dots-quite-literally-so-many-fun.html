Grab a cup of tea and settle in, let's play with some random DOTS - I have five bags of Extra Dots Series 5 with cute kawaii printed round tiles and the box of Lots of Dots. Now I have too many options and a crippling creative block. But I just had a go at doodling with the DOTS and it was very cathartic - lovely for some restful mindfulness.
