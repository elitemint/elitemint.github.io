$100 STREET FOOD CHALLENGE IN MEXICO CITY

1. CHILAQUILES STAND
ADDRESS: Mercado de Xochimilco, Av. Nuevo León s/n, Santa Crucita, Xochimilco, 16070 Ciudad de México, CDMX, México

🇲🇽CHILAQUILES VERDE: Add chilaquiles in salsa verde in a styrofoam container. Add cream Add basket cheese. Add chicken. Add onions and serve

💸PRICE: 20 pesos / $1.00 USD
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
2. COCINA REBECA
ADDRESS: Mercado de Xochimilco, Av. Nuevo León s/n, Santa Crucita, Xochimilco, 16070 Ciudad de México, CDMX, México

🇲🇽HUAZONTLE: Dip amaranth in special whipped batter and deep-fry until golden brown. Slather in salsa verde and serve.

🇲🇽PANCITA: Place cow stomach in broth and leave it to boil for 20 minutes. Cut the stomach with a knife and then serve in a bowl.

💸PRICE: Huazontle - 90 pesos / $4.50 USD | Pancita - 100 pesos / $5.00 USD
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
3. LA HIJA DE CABRERA
ADDRESS: Mercado de Xochimilco, Av. Nuevo León s/n, Santa Crucita, Xochimilco, 16070 Ciudad de México, CDMX, México

🇲🇽GORDITA: Stuff chicharrón in corn dough and flatten. Place on grill and flip. Cut open and add lard. Add iceberg lettuce and cheese. Add chile pasilla. Fold and assemble into a sandwich. Serve.

💸PRICE: 25 pesos / $1.25 USD
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
4. ANTOJITOS GUERA
ADDRESS: Mercado de Xochimilco, Av. Nuevo León s/n, Santa Crucita, Xochimilco, 16070 Ciudad de México, CDMX, México

🇲🇽Huarache: Stuff black beans in corn masa dough and flatten in a mold. Grill. Flip on both sides and add lard. Add green sauce, red sauce and pasilla chile. Add lettuce, cheese, onions and grilled beef.

💸PRICE: 60 pesos / $3.00 USD
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
5. ANTOJITO XOCHIMILCO
ADDRESS: Mercado de Xochimilco, Av. Nuevo León s/n, Santa Crucita, Xochimilco, 16070 Ciudad de México, CDMX, México

🇲🇽PAMBAZO: Cut bread and dip in red sauce. Place on a grill and continuously flip it. Add lard. On the grill, add potatoes. Assemble the potatoes and on top of one side of the bread. Add lettuce and cheese. Lather cream on one side of bread and the other.

💸PRICE: 25 pesos / $1.25 USD
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
6. XOCHIMILCO STAND
ADDRESS: Mercado de Xochimilco, Av. Nuevo León s/n, Santa Crucita, Xochimilco, 16070 Ciudad de México, CDMX, México

🇲🇽HUITLACOCHE (CORN SMUT QUESADILLAS): Add blue corn dough to a mold and flatten. Add cheese on one side and corn smut on the other. Fold and grill for a bit longer then serve.

🇲🇽TLACOYO: Stuff cheese inside corn dough and flatten in mold. Place on grill and then add napole and cheese.

💸PRICE: 25 pesos / $1.25 USD each
