In 2020, Expanscience – a French independently-owned pharmaceutical and dermo-cosmetics laboratory –generated a turnover of €250.2 M, 73% of which out of France. Its solid reputation as an innovator and as a specialist both in in skin health and treatment of osteoarthritis is based on its leading brands – Piasclédine 300 and Mustela - in particular. Expanscience's positive impact on society and the environment is recognized by the international B Corp label.
