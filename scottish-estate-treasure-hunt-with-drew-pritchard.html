With the shop's dwindling inventory, Drew's search for one-off items takes him from posh to junk. With priority access to a sprawling Scottish estate, Drew hopes to uncover a bit of country house magic. An old-school scrapyard in Coventry is like heaven. Drew makes a stop in Brighton to sell a few of his wares. Back at the shop, Drew hears the restorer's verdict about his new rusty Morris Minor convertible.
