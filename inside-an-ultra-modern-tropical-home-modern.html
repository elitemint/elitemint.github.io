🌴 ABOUT THE PROPERTY
When we think of the top-of-range homes in Bali, this luxury mansion comes immediately to mind. Built on 950sqm of land, Shahzadi Villa is an enormous and outstanding property with 6-ensuite bedrooms, open plan living, modern Bali design, and even 2 infinity pools.

Entering the property, you will be greeted by an incredible water feature and amazing sculptures. The dining and living areas are all open and have exceptional views of the surrounding jungle, which is the perfect setting for guests to relax and unwind. Another highlight of this home tour is the sunken sofa under the glass ceiling from the two-story pool. The waterfall and the Bali sunshine create an idyllic atmosphere in what feels like an underwater living room. This luxury home is seriously impressive from the moment you enter.

The modern Bali interior design is inspired by natural materials and neutral colours, which have been chosen to bring more warmth to the spaces and give more prominence to the architecture and tropical scenery that surrounds the house.
