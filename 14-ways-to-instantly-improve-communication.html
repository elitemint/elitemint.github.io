It guides everything we do, whether we're communicating at work to meet deadlines and get things done, or interacting with friends, family, and partners to form meaningful bonds. Regrettably, unless we are fortunate enough to be surrounded by exceptional communicators, we are prone to developing negative habits. So, let’s get down to it.
