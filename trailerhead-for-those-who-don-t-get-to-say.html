Returning after a seven year hiatus, the evolving Epic Music composer collective known as Trailerhead bring you “Catharsis”. Produced by Immediate Music's co-founder Yoav Goren, the album is a thematic work featuring 12 brand new compositions from 7 leading composers, presenting their cinematic impressions of the pandemic that has reshaped our lives and changed our world in 2020/2021.

"For Those Who Don't Get To Say Goodbye" is track 6 on the album, composed by Aleksandar Dimitrijevic:

"A musical translation of thoughts and emotions I have when I think about all the people in this world who have lost loved ones during the pandemic and couldn't say one final goodbye; and about hope that we, as species, will come out of this disaster more united and better prepared for the next one."

Mixed and Mastered by Greg Townley
