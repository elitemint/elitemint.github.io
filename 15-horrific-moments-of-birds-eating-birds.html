Caracara Attacks

On the Falkland Islands, avian parents must remain vigilant. Caracaras are on the lookout, and an unattended nest can quickly turn into a buffet for these ferocious carnivores. The Johnny Rook is a bird that gets right in your face. These cunning animals, known as the 'flying devils' are the Caracara of the Falkland Islands, and they gather with their young to scare both large and tiny species. They are fearless birds.
