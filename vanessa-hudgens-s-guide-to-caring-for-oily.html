For actor Vanessa Hudgens, healthy skin starts with one rule: wash your face. “Even if it's midday and you're putting on your makeup to go out with your girlfriends, cleanse. Get everything off so you have a really clear face,” Hudgens advises. The tick, tick…Boom! star begins prep for a girl's night out with her Know Beauty cleanser and moisturizer—avoiding oils and serums completely because she has oily skin.
