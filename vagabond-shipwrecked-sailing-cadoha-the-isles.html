El Vagabond shipwrecked after the storm Evert smashed into the Isles of Scilly, 2021. The RNLI lifeboat teams were called out a total of 22 times to the islands, alone that night.

These incredibly brave men and women put their lives on the line to rescue those in trouble and so we want to dedicate this video and ALL proceeds from it to help support this magnificent charity that we're all so lucky to have.
