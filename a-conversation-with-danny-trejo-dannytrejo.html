The Midnighters are honored to host a LIVE conversation with actor Danny Trejo.

He has a prolific career in the entertainment industry, yet his road to success has been hard earned and anything but typical; beginning with imprisonment and culiminating with helping young people battle drug addiction; alongside acting and producing. Trejo has starred in numerous films, including Desperado, Heat, the From Dusk Till Dawn franchise, Con Air, Once Upon A Time In Mexico, Grindhouse, Machete, Machete Kills, Dead In Tombstone, and Dora the Explorer to mention but a few.

Together, we will be discussing his many memorable roles, and everything else from his life before acting, to achieving mainstream fame.
