What is Language?
a language is system of communication which consists a set of sounds and written symbols, which is used by a perticular country or region in order to communicate.
Language is common and meaningful human communication method which is is consist of words that are used in structured and conventional way, and conveyed by speech Writing or gestures.
Human Language is vocal, arbitrary and meaningful way of communication.

How Language is Arbitrary?
In Linguistics, Arbitrariness is the absence of any natural connection between a word's sound and thing to which it refers.

Characteristics of Language:
1) Language is Arbitrary.
2) Language is Social.
3) Language is Systematic
4) Language is Vocal.
5) Language is Non-instinctive.
6) Language is Creative and Productive.
