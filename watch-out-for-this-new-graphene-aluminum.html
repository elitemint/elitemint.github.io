Yes, that's right, there is a new battery on the block, and it's the graphene aluminum battery produced by clean technology firm Graphene Manufacturing Group (GMG). Since the time Tesla exploded onto the EV scene, the electric car industry has come a really long way, especially where batteries are concerned. Musk also says there is plenty of lithium in Nevada and will use the surroundings of his super factory, the Giga Nevada, to manage the process.
But amidst all this comes GMG's fantastic breakthrough, the graphene aluminum battery that is now about to give Tesla and so many other battery manufacturers a run for their money.
