Yes, that's right, there is a new battery on the block, and it's the graphene aluminum battery produced by clean technology firm Graphene Manufacturing Group (GMG). The new battery will feature a chemistry more powerful than Li-ion batteries.
Since the time Tesla exploded onto the EV scene, the electric car industry has come a really long way, especially where batteries are concerned. Musk also says there is plenty of lithium in Nevada and will use the surroundings of his super factory, the Giga Nevada, to manage the process.
