Police are searching for the occupants of a vehicle, which could be linked to the suspicious disappearance of a man in South West Queensland.

The car, stolen from the Sunshine Coast, has been filmed in fuel drive-offs and was seen close to where 27-year-old Jeremiah Rivers went missing.

"We're interested in any person who was out there. That includes transport drivers, tourists." - Detective Acting Superintendent Stephen Blanchfield, Queensland Police.
