This song BHAYAPADABEDA birthed out of my quiet time when i was going through the lowest point and was living in the fear of the unknown.
"Fear not" is the most repeated command in the bible.
Fear is the enemies most popular weapons used against God's children.
I was a victim of fear but God spoke to me through Isaiah 41.10 ..we might feel afraid but we have a God who is with us.
We might not be in control of our circumstances but can trust and put our faith in the one who can.
As the culture around us seems saturated and plunged into state of fear by the pandemic
Im hoping this song will encourage and remind you everyday to choose faith over fear and trust that there is a higher power at work than depression, poverty, setbacks sickness, death and God will help us in every step to conquer fear.
May you be ministered as you hear this song.
