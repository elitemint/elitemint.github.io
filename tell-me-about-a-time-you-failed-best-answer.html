TELL ME ABOUT A TIME YOU FAILED (How do you answer Tell me about a time you failed?)
INTERVIEW TIP #1. Use the STAR technique…
Situation
Task
Action
Result

INTERVIEW TIP #2. Give a specific example that includes:
1. The REASON why you failed. (Don’t blame others!)
2. What YOU DID to put things right.
3. What you LEARNT from the experience.

INTERVIEW TIP #3. Be HONEST and take responsibility for what happened.
