Christmas on Temple Square launches this year with a musical tribute: “Witnesses of Christ.” Join a virtual broadcast on Sunday, November 28, at 6:00 p.m. MST. In this 50-minute tribute to the birth of Jesus Christ, you’ll hear peace-filled messages and new arrangements of favorite hymns and carols performed by the Truman Brothers, the Utah State University Chamber Singers, Daniel Beck, and many others. The event will be hosted by David Butler.

Feel peace and joy this Christmas as you hear witnesses of Christ’s divinity and feel His love. This concert will be available to view on demand during the Christmas season.
