​We want children to be proud of what makes them different, to embrace it, to find their strength in what makes them unique. We are honouring the odd fish, the strange birds, the introverts and the extroverts.

Every piece that is available in our Black Odd Ball, Navy Odd Ball or Black Slinky Stripe prints features a shape or colour in a position that is exclusive to that piece. No Bean in these prints will be the same making each piece special. We hope your children have as much fun wearing these playful and comfy designs as we did making them!
