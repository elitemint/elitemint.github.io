We can’t believe another year is over and 2021 has certainly been an interesting one. Here is a compilation of our favourite and some never before seen outtakes, bloopers and funny moments from making our 2021 videos. We always wanted our channel to be “real life” in a real machining workshop and this compilation certainly shows that, from Kurtis's way with words, to the many different interruptions, to the lost tools and issues with the welder. Thank you for all the support this year, we wish all our subscribers & viewers a Happy New Year and here’s hoping 2022 is better for us all.
Cheers!
