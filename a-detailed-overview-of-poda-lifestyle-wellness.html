PODA Lifestyle & Wellness is a company that just recently began trading on the CSE under the stock symbol $PODA. They're coming to market with a patented Heat Not Burn reduced risk smoking alternative that aims to have widespread adoption overseas as many smokers are looking to move away from combustible cigarettes, but are not drawn towards vaping as a means of quitting.

We sit down with their CEO Ryan Selby as he explains why this method of smoking has so much room for rapid growth among current smokers as they gear up for their first products to be on shelves in the summer of 2021.
