After serving many years in the Australian Army, Andre Obradovic became an athlete – competing in and coaching for many events around the world, proving that you can actually become faster, stronger, leaner and healthier as you age.

Andre now coaches his clients through life’s many seasons, achieving sustainable results in both mental and physical well-being.
