I define self-respect as ‘having admiration, pride and confidence in your own self.’ I also believe that you’ve gotta respect yourself in order to gain respect from others. When you respect yourself you are consciously concerned with what you say and how you behave. And I’m sure you would have noticed that others tend to treat you the way you treat yourself. Right?

In today’s episode of Preet TV – Self-Respect: How To Own It – I’ll be sharing 3 simple tips on how to own your self-respect AND get the respect of others. If you aren’t already doing so, learn how to respect your efforts, respect yourself. For self-respect leads to self-discipline, and when you have both firmly under your belt, you have real power.
