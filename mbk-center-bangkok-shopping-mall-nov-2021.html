MBK CENTER is a large shopping mall in Bangkok.
It is a popular shopping mall, especially among tourists.
It is popular for its low-cost food court and Shopping Street where you can buy various souvenirs.
It also used to be famous for its mobile sales area, the largest in Bangkok.
Currently, many areas are still being renovated.
I believe that more and more stores will reopen in the future.
