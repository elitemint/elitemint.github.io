Have you ever wondered if foxes eat chickens? They do, indeed. Foxes pose a significant threat even in urban areas, as they are both strong and merciless. Taking precautions to protect chickens from predators is critical no matter where you live. This fox jumps on a hen and kills it instantly, or so he thinks. The hen begins to run wild with feathers flying all over the place, but it never returns and neither does the fox. However, for someone creative, there are enough feathers on the floor to make a nice, fluffy pillow. This fox has a field day when he breaks into this chicken coop. He attacks every chicken in sight, kills a few, and creates havoc in the coop.

Fox VS Dog

This fox harasses a chubby dog, and the dog doesn’t appreciate it. The dog barks at the fox, but the fox doesn’t back down and even tries to bite the dog’s legs. The fox is stubborn and even if the dog continues barking, the fox doesn’t care and stands his ground. This fox is not very big, but boy, does he have a lot of energy. I’m not sure if these two are playfighting or if they’re involved in a real fight, but the dog looks like he’s trying to bite the fox’s abdomen. It’s a tough fight, but not entirely convinced they hate each other. However, at some point, I almost feel like the dog will cut the fox’s stomach open.
