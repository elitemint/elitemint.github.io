Have you ever wondered if foxes eat chickens? They do, indeed. Foxes pose a significant threat even in urban areas, as they are both strong and merciless. Taking precautions to protect chickens from predators is critical no matter where you live. He attacks every chicken in sight, kills a few, and creates havoc in the coop.

This fox harasses a chubby dog, and the dog doesn’t appreciate it. The dog barks at the fox, but the fox doesn’t back down and even tries to bite the dog’s legs. The fox is stubborn and even if the dog continues barking, the fox doesn’t care and stands his ground. This fox is not very big, but boy, does he have a lot of energy.
