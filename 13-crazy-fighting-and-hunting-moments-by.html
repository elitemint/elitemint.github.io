This fox jumps on a hen and kills it instantly, or so he thinks. The hen begins to run wild with feathers flying all over the place, but it never returns and neither does the fox. He attacks every chicken in sight, kills a few, and creates havoc in the coop.

Fox VS Dog

This fox harasses a chubby dog, and the dog doesn’t appreciate it. The dog barks at the fox, but the fox doesn’t back down and even tries to bite the dog’s legs.
