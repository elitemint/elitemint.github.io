This fox harasses a chubby dog, and the dog doesn’t appreciate it. The dog barks at the fox, but the fox doesn’t back down and even tries to bite the dog’s legs. I’m not sure if these two are playfighting or if they’re involved in a real fight, but the dog looks like he’s trying to bite the fox’s abdomen.
