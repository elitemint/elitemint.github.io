I want you to understand that the ways of God is totally different from human comprehension but when he finally chooses to speak, every knee bows and tongues confess that he is Lord.

I want you to know that you will live to see the reward of the wicked, you will know that it is good to serve God. All those that have treated you will come back and fall at your feet, they will be covered in shame because God will not allow them win over you.
