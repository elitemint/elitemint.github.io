Iskandar Malaysia Social Hero Awards (IMSHA) 2019 is inviting you to nominate a hero from your community for our 6th annual IMSHA Gala Night where a total of 20 organisations and individuals will receive and be awarded based on the following categories;

1. Arts, Culture & Heritage Preservation
2. Animal & Wildlife Preservation
3. Disaster Relief
4. Education Advancement
5. Environmental Preservation/Protection
6. Health Services
7. Human Empowerment
8. Poverty & Hunger Relief
9. Public Safety
10. Social Innovators
11. CSR Awards (Organisation Category only)

Nominate your hero by clicking this link: https://goo.gl/forms/GzWMFijJGFSUdYEu2

Nominations for IMSHA 2019 are being accepted till July 31st 2019 and the award winners will be announced during our IMSHA 2019 Gala Night this coming October.
