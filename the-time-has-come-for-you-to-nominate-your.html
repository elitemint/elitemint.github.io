1. Arts, Culture & Heritage Preservation
2. Animal & Wildlife Preservation
3. Disaster Relief
4. Education Advancement
5. Environmental Preservation/Protection
6. Health Services
7. Human Empowerment
8. Poverty & Hunger Relief

10. Social Innovators
