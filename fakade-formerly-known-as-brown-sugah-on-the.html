He describes himself as a Neo-soul, Jazz, and Funk musician and is on the comeback trail after a four-year sabbatical.
The seasoned singer, songwriter, composer and performer, used to be known as Brown Sugah, but has reinvented himself and is now known as Fakade.
Fakade, along with his full band, will perform at his comeback concert at The One Room in Gqeberha on December 16 and he joins us now to tell us more about the event.
