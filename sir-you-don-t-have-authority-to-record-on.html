Today we take a look at Toyota Motor Manufacturing UK, Derby DE1 9TA.

Parking was a problem so I decided to park onsite this time, a decision which stung me later.

Security didn't like me filming and ask me to leave but at this point I wasn't convinced that they had that power as the road is used by more than one business.

When the security boss arrived we spoke about the private property issue and eventually the police arrive.

The Police Constable and the PCSO where very professional but they did spot my vehicle.

At the end we take the drone over and see the Security Staff and Police still in conversation.
