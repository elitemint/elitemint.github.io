Alisa Ueno & Vigiletti - Is That It
OUT 09/14/2018 HERE: http://smarturl.it/Is-That-It

Creative Director of Tokyo fashion brand Fig & Viper Alisa Ueno and Fashion guru Edward Vigiletti have teamed up to create a stunner of a release, primed to elevate the two as mainstays within the DJ circuit.

Alisa Ueno, renowned for her collaboration with EDM superstar duo Sofi Tukker on their chart staple ‘Best Friend’, ‘Is That It’, whilst written and produced by NERVO, ensures a new signature sound for the pair - pop laden harmonies, combined with a strike of technical flair and first rate production qualities.

Fresh from his debut five track EP ‘Xsc8pism’, the contribution of underground DJ Edward Vigiletti offers different dimensions to this upcoming release, an all-encompassing track bound to create airwaves around clubs across the globe.
