Ivo Dimchev is a choreographer and performer from Bulgaria, known internationally for his provocative and often controversial works of performance art. His work is an extreme and colorful mixture of performance art, dance, theatre, music, drawings, and photography.

Dimchev is an author of more than 30 performances. He has received numerous international awards for dance and theatre and has presented his work across Europe, South America, and North America. He is well knows for his collaboration with the famous Austrian sculpture artist Franz West, with the sculptures of whom Ivo has created 2 stage productions presented in prestigious European Festivals. Ivo Dimchev is knows also as visual artist and very often along his performances and concerts festivals organise his exhibitions of paintings, photography and sculptures.
Ivo Dimchev has presented his work in London, Moscow, Berlin, Frankfurt, New York, Los Angeles, San Francisco, Rio de Janeiro, Sao Paulo, Paris, Brussels, Athens, Roma, Warsaw, Budapest, Oslo, Helsinki, Seul, Miami, Belgrade, Madrid, Barcelona, Istanbul, Montreal and many others.
