Swedish band ABBA has released their album Voyage - it will be their first album in 40 years and their last ever album.

The band members Agnetha Faltskog, Bjorn Ulvaeus, Benny Andersson and Anni-Frid Lyngstad - all now in their 70s, ended up recording the 10-track collection after reuniting to record two songs.
