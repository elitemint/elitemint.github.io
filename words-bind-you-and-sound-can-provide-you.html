Words Bind You And Sound Can Provide You The Doorway Into Creation! A Powerful Talk by Sadhguru

Shakespeare said, what's in a name, that which you call a rose, by any other name, would smell as sweet. Shakespeare had a deep insight when he said that. Even if you call rose a smelly flower, it would not make any difference to its sweet smell, nor would it make any difference to its beauty. So it shows the power of the words to hypnotize you. When you attach a word to something, it makes you feel that you really know it. But the truth is you've covered up the mystery with a label. You attach a word rose to a sweet smelling beautiful flower, and you feel you know this flower. But the truth is that you know nothing about it. That is the magic spell, you can say, the words cast on us all. For more, kindly watch my video!

About Sadhguru:
Sadhguru, a yogi and profound mystic of our times, is a visionary humanitarian and a prominent spiritual leader. At home in loincloth as much as he is in blue jeans, barefoot through the mighty Himalayas, or straddling a BMW motorcycle on the expressway, Sadhguru is the most unusual mystic that one can encounter. Marking a clear departure from mere customs and rituals, Sadhguru's scientific methods for self-transformation are both direct and powerful. Belonging to no particular tradition, Sadhguru incorporates and presents what is most valid for the contemporary life from the yogic sciences.
