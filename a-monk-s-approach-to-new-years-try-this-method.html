A Monk's Approach to New Years | Try This Method

In this video, I wanted to give you a different approach to looking at the New Years - how do we celebrate and prepare for this occasion in the monastery? As Buddhist monks, we try to be mindful of our thoughts, speech and actions so New Years is another occasion that we get to practice ourselves. I hope this new approach is helpful and feel free to use this strategy to improve yourself if you find it helpful. I hope you enjoy! #monks #buddhism #newyears
