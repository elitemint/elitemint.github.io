We get to the lake and the kids run as fast as they can to the beach. Adley and Niko do get caught by pirates and trapped in jail, but luckily I was able to find the keys to free them.

We head home to see Jenny, who has some amazing baby updates and we end the vlog playing with marbles in the ultimate toy room!!
