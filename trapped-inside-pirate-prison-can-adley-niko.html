I know I've said this many times before, but I love having breakfast with my kiddos. Espcially when we're all three having cereal and laughing and starting our day and morning routine off right. We get to the lake and the kids run as fast as they can to the beach. We head home to see Jenny, who has some amazing baby updates and we end the vlog playing with marbles in the ultimate toy room!!
