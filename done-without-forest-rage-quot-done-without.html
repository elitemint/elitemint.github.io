"Done Without" is an underground one-man band from Zhytomyr, Ukraine.

Track " Forest Rage " dedicated to all bastards engaged in deforestation of Polissya, Carpathians and other Ukraine's forests and all over the world.

Track recording began from at home improvisation.
All samples including sound of voices,chainsaw,twigs breaking and birdsongs are original.
Chainsaw was recorded at home.
Birdsongs were recorded during riding my bicycle in the Polissya forest.
Twigs breaking sound is a record of manually broken twigs.
