Asmongold designs a better WoW Patch 9.2 in 15 minutes than Blizzard could with all their time and money since World of Warcraft Shadowlands came out. Asmongold easily finds ways to make flying fun to get, reimagine WoW's outdoor combat with other players and bring back the true feel of an MMORPG to World of Warcraft, essentially designing a better 9.2 Patch than what Blizzard was able to deliver in over a year.
