A business usually exists to solve a business problem. But, when you’re starting one for the first time- how can you make sure that the problem you’re trying to solve is actually valid? 🧐

This is where market research is important. The research will help you validate your business problem and allow you to figure out if your price points and business processes make financial sense 🙌🏽

However, running your own market research for the first or the fifth time won’t be easy if you haven’t mastered the right process. So, how can you master this? 👀
