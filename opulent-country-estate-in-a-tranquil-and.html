Treasured Te Hihi Estate is a highly sought after, uniquely opulent property for guests who desire the ultimate in luxurious, high-end accommodation. Offering 92 acres of manicured grounds, the estate provides a tranquil and delightfully secluded setting.


The exquisite Te Hihi Estate boasts six beautifully designed bedrooms and four deluxe bathrooms. The Estate has also been well frequented by those looking for a unique venue to hold special events or corporate retreats, perfect for groups or families seeking luxury lodgings with total privacy.

You will enjoy the luxurious daily accommodation service, full cooked breakfast, and a selection of bar drinks. Situated only thirty minutes from Auckland Airport, Te Hihi Estate is easily accessible for both international and domestic guests. This property is also suitable for exclusive functions such as charity events, corporate events, weddings, and family ceremonies.
