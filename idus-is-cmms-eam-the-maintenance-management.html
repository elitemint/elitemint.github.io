Idus IS is a complete and flexible maintenance system for industrial firms, helping you retain complete control of your maintenance. Idus IS features:
Navigate Your System Easily with Idus's Point-&-Click Interface: Upload images of your facility, find your equipment easily, get information quickly and issue work orders fast.
Preventative and Condition-Based Maintenance: Create and execute maintenance schedules or respond to problems as they arise.
Work Order Generation: Create work orders based on data linked from existing systems and keep a record of work orders.
Integrate with Any System: Connect with any system easily. Specifically, Idus is integrated with the screen objects and iFIX tags so that during runtime and development, you are a 'right mouse click' away from the IDUS system.
