Jonny visits new Bristol brewery Lost and Grounded, a brewery that had hype before they even brewed a beer. Avoiding the IPAs we're all obsessed with, they're taking inspiration from Belgium and Germany with a range of lagers and yeast-forward beers.

It's a beautiful brewery, with beautiful beers and people...but DAMN is it loud in there.
