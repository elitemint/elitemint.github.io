Actor, screenwriter, and hit podcast host Casey Wilson traces the evolution of her career—from Bride Wars to Bitch Sesh to upcoming Apple TV+ dark comedy The Shrink Next Door. While unpacking her Chanel bag, she reveals her favorite sketch from SNL, what it’s like working with Kathryn Hahn, and why Susan Sarandon is a very forgiving woman. She also shares how Bitch Sesh came to be and why she photoshopped her husband out of the cover for her book, The Wreckage of My Presence. Plus, she spills on what happened when the Happy Endings cast went to Vegas.
