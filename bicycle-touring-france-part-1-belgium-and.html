In the Summer of 2018 I rode my bike from my hometown Goirle in the Netherlands to the South Western corner of France. It took me 9 days to cover a total distance of 1350 km.

For the bigger part I followed the French part of the route to Santiago de Compostella. Also known as the "Camino". GPX can be found here (dutch): https://www.europafietsers.nl/fietsroutes/st-jacobs-fietsroute

This part 1 covers the first 3 days where I rode thru Belgium and Northern France, right up to the outskirts of Paris.

• Riding solo
• Bike: 2015 Specialized AWOL
• Camping yes, cooking no.
