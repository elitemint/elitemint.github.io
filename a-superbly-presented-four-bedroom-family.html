Less than 1/2 a mile from the local shop and primary school and set in the ever popular area of Reffley is this wonderfully presented four bedroom semi-detached house.

An ideal family home the property offers an excellent level of accommodation with gas central heating, ample off-road parking, a single garage and fully enclosed rear garden.

The entrance hall welcomes you into the home, it provides somewhere to leave coats and shoes and leads on to the first floor and the living room, flooded with natural light the living room is an ideal space to relax and unwind with friends or family, double doors lead on to the dining room meaning very easily this space can be opened up for when you entertain. The dining room and kitchen flow seamlessly into each other and the rear garden making this a 'go to' space when you have guests, the dining room offers sliding doors into the conservatory and a separate door into the rear garden. The kitchen offers a fantastic level of storage thanks to the fitted units along with a gas hob, electric oven and views of the rear garden.

Upstairs you will find the four well proportioned bedrooms, the family bathroom and a separate W.C, the main and second bedroom are both generous doubles that boast fitted wardrobe space, the third and fourth bedrooms are both comfortable singles. There is a separate W.C and bathroom with the bathroom offering a suite including bath with shower over and wash basin.

Outside the gravel driveway provides an abundance of off-road parking along with access to the garage. The rear garden offers a good degree of privacy along with a patio for al-fresco dining and a generous lawn ideal for enjoying a balmy summer's day with friends and family.
