Hyperemesis Gravidarum (HG) is at the extreme end of the pregnancy sickness spectrum. It affects 1-2% of pregnant women every year (around 3 Million globally).

Without medical intervention women with HG will suffer tremendously. Whilst the physical aspects of this condition are often overlooked and "brushed off" as morning sickness and women are often misdiagnosed, the mental affects of this disease are just as forgotten.

This film will look in to one of them.....Perinatal Anxiety.

Presented by Brushed Off Films Producer and HG Campaigner Charlotte Howden. Charlotte suffered with HG in 2015 with her son Henry.
