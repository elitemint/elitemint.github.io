In this video we answer the most common questions about an International Driver’s Permit (IDP). If you are traveling to a foreign country, an IDP is one of the documents you might need to carry. Some travelers claim they have never needed one while others have had issues with authorities for not having one. But what is the truth? We separate the Facts from Fiction in this video by answering questions like:

- What is an International Drivers Permit?
- Do I really need one?
- Isn’t your driver’s license enough?
- How long is an IDP valid?
- Which countries require an IDP?
- What’s the IDP application process?
- What are the IDP requirements?
- How long does it take to get an International driver’s license?

You may have heard different opinions about whether or not an IDP is necessary so we’ve put together a list of everything you need to know before you travel. Many travelers have run into trouble because they didn’t know what to expect. It is our goal to save you from any hassle and ensure your travels go smoothly.

We’ll separate all the facts from the fiction so you don’t have to do any more research. By the end of this video, you’ll know exactly what to do the next time you travel.
