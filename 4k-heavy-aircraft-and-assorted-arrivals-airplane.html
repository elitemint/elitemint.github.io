Wishing you a Happy New Year 2020 and a Happy New Decade! It's been a while since my last post but I finally snagged some spare time to run up to my hometown Chicago O'Hare
to catch some of the latest action. In this video I finally get a decent video of the Austrian Airlines 60th Anniversary Livery and some clips of aircraft in the new United livery as well.
Sad to see the KLM 747 finish service to O'Hare after many years of proud service but we welcome the Boeing 787 Dreamliner in all of its increasingly common varieties.

It is shaping up to be a promising new year with some new arrivals planned for this year later on including El Al coming back March 22nd and Qantas starting their inaugural to Brisbane on April 15th I will try to get both of these two the day of their first arrival, but if not, rest assured I will try to get several good videos of them in the days that follow.

Thanks for your patience putting up with my busy schedule... I do try to post new video as often as I can and I realize that's not been very often lately.

A B!G THANX to ALL 60,000+ subscribers!!!
I'd still make videos even if nobody watched but you all make it all the more worthwhile.
