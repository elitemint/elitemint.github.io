Having a deep understanding of and connection to your desires for your future ensures you are moving forward in the direction of those desires. Because your mind moves you towards what is familiar and comfortable, it is helpful to have consistent repetition when visualizing what you want for your life. Consider making this practice a part of your bedtime routine for the month or until you have reached your goal. Additionally, tapping into the emotions you will have when you do experience what you want will support the manifestation process.
