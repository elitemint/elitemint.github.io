Learning platforms permit efficient complete total control of automisation, administration, and communication with users, trainers and teachers, and of course content conduct. They permit effective management of registrations, and origination of courses and groups. The e-learning platforms are functional learning spaces manufactured to ease distance education, both for educational centers and for companies.

The most essential advantages of online platforms for students:
1. Online Learning fits everyone’s demands;
2. Rapid delivery of lessons;
3. Lectures can be picked anytime;
4. Scalability;
5. Offers arrival to updated content;
6. Decreased Costs;
7. Effectiveness;
8. Easy arrival to data;
9. Saving money and time;
10. Effective management;
11. Immediate and up to date content;
12. Improved reporting;
13. Personalisation;
14. Multimedia learning;
15. Commercialisation and Sales;
16. and Developed communication.
