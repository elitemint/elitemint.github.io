I like sampling different types of food whenever l go to an eatery, but l must confess l am obsessed with the Sizzler chicken and Stir fry served at Sultan Restaurant in Hargeisa Somaliland.

If you are in Hargeisa, Jigjigayar precisely, make sure you pop in Sultan Restaurant and enjoy their dishes…and great ambiance of course.
