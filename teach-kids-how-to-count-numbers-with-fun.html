Teach Kids how to Count Numbers with Fun Popsicle Number Toys and 1 to 20 Counting Puzzle! Kids will love to learn counting with fun preschool popsicle ice cream number toys. These colorful ice cream toys come with five possible flavors. Kids count the dots on each preschool toy in order to find out which number popsicle flavor goes on the ice cream. Once we've learned how to count to ten, cute kid, Genevieve, will help teach kids how to count to twenty with a fun 1 to 20 number puzzle. As your kid watches and learns, help them retain the information by repeating the numbers with the video.
