Interesting straight forward production by Gon Haziri & Sonik, who like to produce a more orientalic-like vibe, but not in the traditional manner I'd say. Very techy & snappy groove combined with the floaty oriental sounds, that (put together) create a weird harmony, that is quite interesting to listen to - or how about you, how do you feel about it?
I admire different styles and this is definitely a different, weird & fun track! So go have some fun!
