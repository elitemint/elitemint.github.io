Are you suffering from Imposter Syndrome? Do you feel too young to be taken seriously? In this video I share my career advice for young professionals. Young professionals in the first years of their career often face many career challenges. In this video I talk about how to overcome imposter syndrome. I talk about how to deal imposter syndrome, how to build confidence, how to speak up and participate in meetings and share career advice and tips for young professionals.
