Hello curious creatures! Welcome back, once again, in my kitchen.

This pandemic still has us stuck at home and believe it or not, but I am well and truly craving a trip Britain.You may or may not know, but I a a bit of an anglophile. I have been since I can remember.

So what do we do when we can't go and get our afternoon tea in London? We make it ourselves. In this vlog I am taking matters in my own hands and I'm organising a traditionally British afternoon tea. So come along on a day ALL about AFTERNOON TEA filled with plenty of baking! My favourite!
