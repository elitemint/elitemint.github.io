NCD market vendors association today staged another protest at the Gordon Market in the nation’s capital following yesterday’s protest at the city hall.

The vendors are against the no jab, no entry policy imposed by the City Hall at market facilities.

NCD Governor Powes Parkop was there to hear their grievances and responded to the rowdy crowd.
