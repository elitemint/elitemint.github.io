Hello, guys :) In this video, you will see the best 3D pen crafts that you can make in no time! Most of these DIYs are really simple, and we recommend that you repeat them together with your kids! If your child loves playing with hair, create some new hairpins with a 3D pen to surprise them ;) We will also show you how to make adorable rings for your kids.
