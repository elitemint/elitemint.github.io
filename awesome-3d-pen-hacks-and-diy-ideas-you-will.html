Hello, guys :) In this video, you will see the best 3D pen crafts that you can make in no time! Most of these DIYs are really simple, and we recommend that you repeat them together with your kids! Boost your creativity and crafting skills with 5-Minute Crafts! Did you know you can make a small house for your cute little pet by just using a 3D pen? We will show the tutorial in the video ;) If you love making jewelry, you can easily make earrings, just fill a Pop It fidget’s holes with a 3D pen! If your child loves playing with hair, create some new hairpins with a 3D pen to surprise them ;) We will also show you how to make adorable rings for your kids.
