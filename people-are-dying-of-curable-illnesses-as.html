More and more Americans are feeling the strain on our country’s health system as unvaccinated COVID infected individuals are packing hospital beds and clogging the system. Although this is how the system has been set up, it’s worth questioning whether or not the unvaccinated should be prioritized when there are vaccinated individuals dying of non-COVID illnesses.
