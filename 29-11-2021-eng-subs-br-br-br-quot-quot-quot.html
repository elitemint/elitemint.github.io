Greek-Turkish relations.
GREECE. TURKEY. USA.
Video with the heavy work, which we have for a few days now in the port of Alexandroupolis, where there is literally a "frenzy", after it has been flooded by US military vehicles and helicopters, was released a few hours ago.
All this is happening, as the US cargo ship, Arc Independence, has docked at the port pier, unloading huge amounts of military equipment, that shows the enormous importance given by the US forces in Alexandroupolis, which has now acquired a large strategic footprint throughout the southeastern Mediterranean region.
Of course, that bothers Turkey unbelievably and causes to it huge dissatisfaction, which it expresses in every way, where ever and as it can, but mainly with articles in the Turkish press, which show the fear of the Turks, for the upgrading of Hellenic-US relations.
