This weeks project is called "the other half" as its the other half of a piece of wood I used to turn "The lost log", which is a piece of wood I found on the floor while out walking.

Originally I wanted to keep the bark on, however too much came off during turning so the entire lot was removed, followed by a re-sand of the back.

Finished with several coats of Danish Oil only. Plenty of spalting and burl down the one side.
