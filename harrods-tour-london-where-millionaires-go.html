It attracts millions of customers from across the world with its unrivalled product selection, world-class service, innovative retail theatre and historic setting.
There are 28 restaurants in-store; comprising of Galvin Demoiselle, Bentley's Sea Grill, and the institution that is The Georgian. Customers can enjoy a wealth of exclusive services, such as By Appointment Personal Shopping and The Penthouse, a private suite dedicated to unsurpassed bespoke service in a beautiful environment.
