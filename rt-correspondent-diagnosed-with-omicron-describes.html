RT’s Paula Slier has recently been diagnosed with the Omicron COVID variant, and has been describing her symptoms. She says, ‘In the beginning, I didn’t even know I had it,’ and describes her symptoms as ‘very similar to those of a common flu’: headache, sore throat, tiredness and feeling generally run-down.
