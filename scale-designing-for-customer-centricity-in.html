Designing for Customer Centricity in the New Possible. This session included panelist from JetBlue Technology Ventures, Hyatt Hotels & Resorts, Rappi and McKinsey Design. The panel shared insights on:

Innovations and examples of customer centric design
Impact of COVID-19 consumer behaviors in the new normal
Opportunities for design to reimagine the new possible
