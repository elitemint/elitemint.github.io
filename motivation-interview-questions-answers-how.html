MOTIVATION INTERVIEW QUESTION #1. What Motivates You? 00:27
TIP: When answering this motivational interview question, talk about things that motivate you both at work, and outside of work.

MOTIVATION INTERVIEW QUESTION #2. What Are Your Long-Term Goals? 01:22
TIP: Sometimes, this question is posed as, where do you see yourself in 10 years’ time? When giving your answer, focus entirely on where you want to be from a work perspective.

MOTIVATION INTERVIEW QUESTION #3. What Are You Passionate About? 02:01
TIP: Most people answer this interview question be just telling the interviewer what they are passionate about outside of work. Whilst it is good to talk about your external passions, don’t forget to mention the things you are passionate about within the work environment!

MOTIVATION INTERVIEW QUESTION #4. How Do You Adapt To Change? 03:01
TIP: Always remember, change is absolutely vital for an organization to keep meeting the needs of its customers. Therefore, in your answer, you need to show the hiring manager that you always embrace change with a positive attitude.

MOTIVATION INTERVIEW QUESTION #5. How Do You Define Success? 03:49
TIP: Don’t say you define success as having money or prosperity! Instead, giving a mature and considered answer that shows the interviewer you will be the perfect fit for their team!
