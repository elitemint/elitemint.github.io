If you want your money or keys to be in a safe place while you’re at the beach, we have a cool hiding trick for you. Put all your belongings in a small food container and cover it with sand. Don’t forget to put a ball or something else in this exact spot so you can find your stuff easily. If you have a huge sweet tooth, here is a new ice cream recipe! Get a pack of your favorite cookies and break them inside the package. Then fill it with milk and put a wooden stick in it. Let the mix freeze and enjoy your treat with a unique taste ;)
