Hey everyone! Today we will share with you cool hacks for hot weather ;) If your child loves swimming, you can easily make a mini pool for them! You will need to tie together 4 tables and put a tarp inside. Fill the reservoir with water and put some toys inside ;) It’s a super safe way for your kids to play in the water. You will see a clothing hack to help you turn your stretched out swim bottoms into the ones that will fit you perfectly. If you want your money or keys to be in a safe place while you’re at the beach, we have a cool hiding trick for you. Put all your belongings in a small food container and cover it with sand. Don’t forget to put a ball or something else in this exact spot so you can find your stuff easily. If you have a huge sweet tooth, here is a new ice cream recipe! Get a pack of your favorite cookies and break them inside the package. Then fill it with milk and put a wooden stick in it. Let the mix freeze and enjoy your treat with a unique taste ;)
