The details are updated for heavy rainfall, widespread winds and significant mountain snowfall. This occurs Tuesday December 14. Snow level rapidly drop Tuesday afternoon and reach low elevations Tuesday night. The heavy rainfall is expected for 2 to 6 hours, while light to moderate rain will occur for 12 to 24 hours. There is potential for lightning and small hail for all areas.
