The details are updated for heavy rainfall, widespread winds and significant mountain snowfall. This occurs Tuesday December 14. Total rainfall of 1 to 3 inches is expected but amounts around 6 inches for the San Bernardino Mountain coastal slopes. Rainfall rates at their peak will reach 0.25 to 0.75 inches per hour. There is potential for lightning and small hail for all areas.
