When a worker is injured at work, it doesn’t always mean they need time off work. Recent research has shown that work has important health and wellbeing benefits.

Being off work for long periods of time can reduce the likelihood of a worker ever returning to work. It is a crucial step in recovering from a work-related injury.

WorkCover Queensland will discuss why it is important to return to work and the benefits, as well as guide you through how to support your worker and define suitable duties.
