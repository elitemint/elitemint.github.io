Decision making is the most powerful tool in a person's life, in order to come to a decision one must have real need which has to do with his survival. A decision for him is to create his path to life without being restricted. Decision means action and creation. Since our decisions create the conditions for our survival, some people choose just to survive while others survive by living their lives to the fullest.
