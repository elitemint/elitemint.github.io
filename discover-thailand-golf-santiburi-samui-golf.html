Looking for the Ultimate Thailand Golf Guide? Well you found it! Searching golf courses in Thailand but can't decide where to play? Golf with Bang is a series on my Channel that will tour you through Thailand's top golf courses, but in a fun way! Bang is my best Thai friend and we've been buddies for over 25 years. Somehow, this man from Koh Samui learned to play golf very well...unfortunately I didn't. That's ok, have a laugh at my expense as we play a round at Santiburi on the beautiful island of Koh Samui. This is truly a bucket list golf course and one you should play!
