WHO knew that the Loch Ness monster had a cousin called Champ? Champ, who is said to live in the waters of Lake Champlain, has been ‘seen over 300 times’. Beast Hunter Nash Hoover talks to local Cristine who claims to have seen Champ. The Unexplained team check out the Champlain sea cave to try and discover clues about the mysterious sea monster.
