Home Affairs Minister K Shanmugam has said the pandemic has made terrorists more adept at going online to recruit and radicalise people. This is as COVID-19 has hindered travel between countries. In an exclusive interview, he said a key plank of Singapore's fight against terrorism is ensuring everyone has a place in society, regardless of race and religion.
