The Jaquar Orientation Centre allows you to experience Jaquar's complete bathroom solutions and complete lighting solutions. At the Jaquar Orientation Centre, you will get a complete see, look and feel of all bathroom products and lighting products.

When you visit the Jaquar Orientation Centre, you will receive expert guidance on products, selection and installation.
