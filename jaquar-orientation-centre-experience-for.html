The Jaquar Orientation Centre allows you to experience Jaquar's complete bathroom solutions and complete lighting solutions. Once you walk into a Jaquar Experience Centre, you will see a wide array of bathroom products and lighting products. At the Jaquar Orientation Centre, you will get a complete see, look and feel of all bathroom products and lighting products.
