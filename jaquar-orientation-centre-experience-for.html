The Jaquar Orientation Centre allows you to experience Jaquar's complete bathroom solutions and complete lighting solutions. Once you walk into a Jaquar Experience Centre, you will see a wide array of bathroom products and lighting products. These bathroom designs will bring life to any space and the lighting designs will help in bringing a touch of elegance to any room setting. At the Jaquar Orientation Centre, you will get a complete see, look and feel of all bathroom products and lighting products.

When you visit the Jaquar Orientation Centre, you will receive expert guidance on products, selection and installation. Our experts can guide on light planning for free with an estimate on the entire project. If you aren't comfortable heading to the Jaquar Orientation Centre, you can look to avail an online consultation.
