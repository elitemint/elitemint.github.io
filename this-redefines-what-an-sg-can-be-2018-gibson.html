📚 Description: I documented an early SG Elegant a few years back and since then I've wanted to check out "version two" - the "Tiger" finishes. I've seen Grey Tiger, Red Tiger, Green Tiger and Blue Tiger so far - even though red would be my least favorite, it was available and maintains the usual look of an SG very well for SG fans. Let's check out this red headstock, flame maple top, abalone inlaid SG!
