Well, Tibetan Cancer Society was founded in the end of year 2014, formally got registered in 8th May 2015 at Karnataka. They also help many cancer patients with treatment irrespective of nationality, caste, creed and religion.

Meals for invisible is one of their initiation to help feed Disadvantaged homeless people with proper nutritious meals to help them combat hunger and survive.Each individual sponsor can feed a person by donating Rs 100 per person which include Rice, Dal, Vegetables, Egg, Banana, and a bottle of water. Further it also includes food packaging stuffs,kitchen expenses, Transportation and volunteers expenses.
You can donate them in celebration of your birthday, special occasion,remembrance of loved one who passed away with homeless by providing proper nutritious meal.
