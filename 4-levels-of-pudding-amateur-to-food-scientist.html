We challenged chefs of three different skill levels - amateur Onika, home cook Beth, and professional chef Frank Proto from The Institute of Culinary Education - to come through with their best pudding recipe. Once each level of chef had presented their creation, we asked expert food scientist Rose to explain the choices they made along the way - both good and bad.
