For the first time, SpaceX has teamed up with researchers from NASA and several other US institutions to publicly discuss how it plans to use Starship to build Mars Base Alpha.

SpaceX has released a whitepaper PDF submitted for the National Academies’ next Planetary Science and Astrobiology Decadal Survey about their plans to settle on Mars

The whitepaper marks the first time that SpaceX has properly fleshed out the basics of its first crewed and uncrewed Starship missions to Mars and confirms a great deal of well-informed speculation.
