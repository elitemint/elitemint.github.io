Cedar Mountain Chapel (CMC) International is a thriving branch of the Assemblies of God, Ghana, founded on the 3rd of October 2010. We are a local church with a global mandate. Our mandate is to reach the international community in Ghana and all around the globe while disciplining all within our reach. We are committed to representing God well by being a Model Church who reflect the excellence of God. We promise to shepherd you well by empowering you to fulfill your dreams and aspirations and make you a candidate of heaven.


Mission
To become the cutting edge 21st century and a new Testament model Church excelling in Worship, Raising Leaders and Disciples as well as providing Leadership in all things.

Vision
To disciple the nation through every excellent and innovative means: thereby equipping the individual to lead, live a fulfilling life on earth, become relevant in societal development and qualify to live in eternity.
