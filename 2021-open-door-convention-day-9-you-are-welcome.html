Cedar Mountain Chapel (CMC) International is a thriving branch of the Assemblies of God, Ghana, founded on the 3rd of October 2010. We are a local church with a global mandate. We are committed to representing God well by being a Model Church who reflect the excellence of God. Mission
To become the cutting edge 21st century and a new Testament model Church excelling in Worship, Raising Leaders and Disciples as well as providing Leadership in all things.

Vision
To disciple the nation through every excellent and innovative means: thereby equipping the individual to lead, live a fulfilling life on earth, become relevant in societal development and qualify to live in eternity.
