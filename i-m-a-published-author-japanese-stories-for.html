Hi Booktube! This video is about my book, Japanese Stories for Language Learners, published by Tuttle. I talk about my experience translating 5 Japanese stories from Japanese to English:
Yuki Onna (Snow Woman), Urashima Taro, Kumo no Ito (The Spider's Thread) by Akutagawa Ryunosuke, Oborekaketa Kyoudai (The Siblings Who Almost Drowned) by Arishima Takeo, and Gauche the Cellist by Miyazawa Kenji. Please like and subscribe if you enjoyed :)
