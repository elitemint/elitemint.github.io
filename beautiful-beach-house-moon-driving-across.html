WHAT A BEAUTIFUL NIGHT AT OUR BEACH HOUSE IN CATEEL, DAVAO ORIENTAL!

After enjoying some moments with the full moon. I woke up and headed down the coast towards Mati. Driving across the Province of Davao Oriental is always relaxing, and well, full of beauty. This video takes a little look at some of the coastal towns that I pass through driving across the Province. We check out one of the oldest churches in Mindanao found in Caraga. Stop for some food and take a look at the river in Manay, then climb up a beautiful mountain side ridge in Tarragona.

The Province of Davao Oriental is wonderful for exploring, driving, living and well...

LIFE.

From our incredible beach house in Davao, to all the small towns along the coast. The province life is beautiful!
