PwC has developed a new tool which facilitates the process of applying for a refund of fixed costs. The Excel template can help your company to easily:

- Calculate fixed costs and turnover for the period December 2019 to February 2020 and the period April 2019 to June 2019, respectively.
- Document expectations of fixed costs and turnover in the period 9 March 2020 – 8 June 2020.

In this video guide we walk you through the Excel template and we show you which numbers go where.

The Excel template is available here: https://www.pwc.dk/da/artikler/2020/04/fa-hjaelp-til-nemt-at-soge-refusion-for-faste-omkostninger.html
