.An ideal recipe to celebrate birthdays! Hearty snacks using just 5 ingredients


Ingredients:
pastry dough - 600 g (21.16 oz)
minced meat - 500 g (17.64 oz)
onion - 1 piece
salt - 5 g (0.18 oz)
black pepper - 5 g (0.18 oz)
water - 20 ml (0.7 fl oz)
mozzarella - 100 g (3.5 oz)
eggs - 1 piece
IN THE OVEN 180 °C (356 °F)/40 minutes
Tray size 35 cm x 30 cm (14 in x 12 in)
