It‘s no question that TheWeeknd’s „Blinding Lights“ is an absolute smash. Loi was well aware of that when she covered the song in a short snippet on social media in april. It was so well received that the sound snippet sparked a trend in Brazil on TikTok which expanded globally. Right now the sound has over 200.000 video creations with a total of 330 million views.
The positive feedback and the request for more led Loi to releasing a full version.
