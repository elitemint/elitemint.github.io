ECONOMIC USE OF ENERGY, HIGH PRODUCTIVITY, SIMPLE INSTALLATION, EXTREMELY QUIET OPERATION, EASY AND MINIMAL MAINTENANCE are the most important features of the industrial shredder ROBUST SD 60.

ROBUST SD 60 is a shredder that is suitable for small and medium-sized companies that handle waste such as diverse wood, particle boards, veneer, MDF boards, cardboard boxes, and other waste.

SD 60 is a well-designed shredder that is equipped with a horizontal pusher. The hydraulic unit saves space and is built into the machine housing. There it is protected from dust and dirt. In order to protect the shredder from possible damage to the cutting unit, the SD 60 has an automatic detection system that switches off at the right moment so that there is no damage. The automatic reversing function, which prevents overloading the motor, is also a very important part of the SD 60.

The tested system guarantees reliable shredding of the material up to 4 m3 / h.
