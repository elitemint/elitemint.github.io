This is the longest road trip in America! We drove from the Southern Most point in the US to the furthest north you can drive in the US. My whole family spent 6 weeks driving 10,400 miles (16,737 km) We drove from Key West Florida to Deadhorse Alaska to Prudhoe Bay on the Arctic Ocean. We explored, camped, fished, hunted fossils, noodled catfish, dug crystals, jumped off waterfalls, kayaked and much much more. We did the entire trip in a rental car and had an amazing family adventure.
