Every child is unique and cannot be judged by the same standards.

We can only facilitate by their side and provide the necessary things to help them grow their own twigs and leaves.

/////////////////////////////
With the hope to inspire more people to think about education for the future, Y2 proudly presents our two-part video series, The Meaning of Education.

The videos showcase two Montessori schools located in Taiwan.  Part one: To Help Children Develop into What They Are Meant to Be displays the period from pre-school to elementary school; and Part two: To Help Adolescents Find Their Own Place exhibits the period from elementary through secondary school.

It is our hope that these visual presentations will offer the general public a glance of Montessori education in practice and its profound meaning.
