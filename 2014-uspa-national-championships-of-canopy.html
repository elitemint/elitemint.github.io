With 76 competitiors (49 open and 27 advanced) including 24 guests, the 2014 USPA National Championships of Canopy Piloting was held at Skydive City Zephyrhills in Florida May 20-23. The competition consisted of three events - speed, distance and zone accuracy - and resulted in the selection of the U.S. Team and multiple state, national, continental and world records.
