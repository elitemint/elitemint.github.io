Welcome to The Founder's Diary

This is our journey from how we went from the council estate to becoming medical doctors. 4% of doctors come from working class backgrounds, we face greater challenges and obstacles so when we make it, it means more.

We share our journey to let you know we can all do whatever we can imagine - this is a series to not only share our journey from the start but also going forwards as we are now on a journey to build a platform to put education and opportunity into the hands of everyone.
