KAHLIL Doucette, 27, from Canada, lost over 200lbs after being overweight her whole life. Her highest weight was 420lbs and she decided to have VSG after her mum cried when she came home for the holidays. Kahlil told Truly: "I was told I wasn't going to live past 40." She had to drastically change her lifestyle. The content creator took control of her life and changed her eating habits and decided to join a gym. She is now thriving in life and she documents her progress on social media.
