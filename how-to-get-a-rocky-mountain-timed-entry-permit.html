You need an extra reservation if you'll be visiting Rocky Mountain National Park in 2021!

I'm here to walk you through the process of getting a Rocky Mountain Timed Entry Permit Reservation.

You'll need this reservation when to enter any part of the park, unless you have a reservation at one of the in-park campgrounds.

Watch me live as I try to get a Timed Entry Permit reservation using Recreation.gov. You can see first-hand how stressful this process can be!
