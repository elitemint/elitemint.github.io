Scientists Discovered NEW Planet Better Than Earth!
Recently, a group of researchers has found not just one but twenty-four planets that could house a civilization just like ours. In fact, they even said that these planets are better than Earth.

The research was done by a team at Washington State University. They located 24 planets outside of our solar system that are not only Earth-like but actually better than Earth in some major ways. It’s important to remember that the researchers weren’t actually looking for life itself but rather planets that could be a good home for it.

The list of planets includes some that are larger than ours, warmer, or even wetter. Another way to get on the list is to be a planet that orbits around a star that has a longer lifespan than our Sun. As you can see, all of these things added up together create a planet that any human would be happy to call home.

To create this list, researchers created a slew of features that they were looking for to consider a planet “super-habitable”. A super-habitable planet is one that would be able to foster a civilization like ours and might even do a better job than Earth.

So, what makes a planet super-habitable? What are the conditions that make life most possible?
