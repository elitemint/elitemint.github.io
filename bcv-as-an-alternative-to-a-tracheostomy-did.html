Did you know BCV, in many instances, can be used instead of a tracheostomy?

BCV is often a safe and viable alternative to other forms of ventilation.

A tracheostomy is associated with many negative side effects, such as:
- Injury to vocal cords
- Damage to the esophagus
- Lung infection
- Bleeding and infection around the tube
- And, many other complications

Biphasic Cuirass Ventilation (BCV) has none of the side effects associated with forms of positive pressure ventilation, like mask ventilation and a tracheostomy.
