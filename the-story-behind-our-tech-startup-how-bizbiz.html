So... what is BizBiz Share? And how did it all start? Well here is the story behind our humble beginnings and a small overview of our revolutionary idea!

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

BizBiz Share is Canada’s largest business resource marketplace, with over 1000 companies using our platform and an offering of over $100,000,000 in available equipment, machinery and real estate for rent.

We are a platform that allows companies to work together and gain access to the resources they need and optimize the efficiency of what they own. This synergy benefits our economy, businesses and our environment.
