‚≠êÔ∏è The activities in " The Easiest Way To Learn English | Learn Vocabulary | Do Exercises" are specially designed to make use of important learning conditions. ‚≠êÔ∏è Illustrations for each target word are provided to help learners visualize the word as it is being used in the example sentence. This series has simply focused on the word in the form that it is most likely to be expressed.
