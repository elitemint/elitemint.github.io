This ginger lemon drink is a perfect home remedy for sore throat, cold, cough and flu. This is a great cure for the common cold and flu during the winter season. This ginger lemon drink is a quick and easy remedy for sore throats, colds, Flu and immunity booster. How to make a ginger lemon drink the easy way for your family. It is also a powerful drink to lose weight. Ginger has so many health benefits. e.g Reduce inflammation, lower cholesterol, headaches, period pain, Nausea and morning sickness, sore muscles Arthritis, blood sugar levels etc. This refreshing ginger lemon drink is great to make as juice for friends and family.

200g Sugar (optional)
