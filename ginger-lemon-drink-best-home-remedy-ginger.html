This ginger lemon drink is a perfect home remedy for sore throat, cold, cough and flu. This is a great cure for the common cold and flu during the winter season. This ginger lemon drink is a quick and easy remedy for sore throats, colds, Flu and immunity booster. How to make a ginger lemon drink the easy way for your family. It is also a powerful drink to lose weight.
