With businesses all wanting their information 'connected', the teams delivering the capability often still do this in an unconnected way, so here is the 3rd of 3 short videos that show how delivery teams can playback requirements with relationships and traceability between the Requirements, the developed Tech Solutions, the Business Process and related end user
training / methods material, whilst simplifying the ability for the client to understand the relationships, risks and impacts and visualize what they are getting?
Whilst this video was aimed at PLM and Digital Engineering, it should be applicable to any delivery team.
Note: Blueworks live is the modelling tool for process in this video, but any Process Modelling (not mapping) application should be capable.
