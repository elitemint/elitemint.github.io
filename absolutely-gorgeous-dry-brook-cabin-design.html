About this space
The Dry Brook Cabin design is inspired by the Scandinavian simplicity and functionality. Our goal was to create the space which gives you the comfort of modern leaving yet not to keep you inside but to be a part of the surrounding landscape. The space
The house is a new construction, completed back in the spring of 2020. It still has the scent of pinewood. Has been designed by ourselves and was built by the local contractors. You will have enough privacy to feel comfortable.
