If you are a people-pleasing yes-man, and you are tired of spreading yourself thin, then it's time to say "no."

Saying no doesn't mean you're going against society. Rather by not saying yes to everything, you're showing others that you command respect. That they can't just use you for their needs.

There's obviously a fine line. You shouldn't say no to everything. In this video, we will explore how to say no without feeling guilty.
