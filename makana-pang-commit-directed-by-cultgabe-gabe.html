Directed by cultgabe (Gabe Caswall) and Makana Pang, "Commit" tells the story of growing up as a surfer on the North Shore. Dive into Makana's generational family roots on Oahu, his friend group, and what it was like going to school across the street from one of the best waves in the world, Pipeline. The North Shore has bred countless ocean oriented talents, and it is clear Makana is determined to turn his potential into reality.
