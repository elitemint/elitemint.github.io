You may be the best candidate for a role, but if you can’t convey your potential, you’re unlikely to land the job. Communication is the keystone skill valued by employers that will empower you to put forward your other great qualifications.

Tune in for this virtual workshop with LatimerNext to learn a repeatable process to use in your job search today, and as you advocate for yourself throughout your career.

Learn how to:
- Assess your audience before you begin to speak or write
- Devise a unified message that runs through all the steps of your applications
- Control the way you are perceived through strategic documents and refined delivery skills
