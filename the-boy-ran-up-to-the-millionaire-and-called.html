The years were flying by quickly. Louis began to think that it would be a good idea for him to have a wife and children. That day, Louis arrived at the orphanage with gifts, as usual. The children were walking outside, many of them recognized the man and immediately started waving happily. Louis smiled warmly at the children. Suddenly, a boy of about five years old came out of the group of little kids. He rushed to the man, threw his arms around his legs and cried out loud. The children had never before reacted so keenly to his presence, and the boy's words struck the man to his heart.
