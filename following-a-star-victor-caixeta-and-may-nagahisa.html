Waiting for the #WorldBalletDay we are glad to present the long-awaited "Following a Star" video with a wonderful ballet duo — May Nagahisa and Victor Caixeta! Both of them moved to Russia from abroad to join the Mariinsky in 2017 and both have already earned sincere love of the Russian audience. "Romeo and Juliet" became a very special ballet for Victor and May — but let them tell you about it.

On October 19, 17:00 msk (UTC + 3), Victor and May will take part in the public talk with young dancers "Ballet future" which will be live-streamed on our channel as part of the Mariinsky #WorldBalletDay program — don't miss!

Watch also our extended interview with Victor published in March: https://youtu.be/VOE5vkxHiI0
