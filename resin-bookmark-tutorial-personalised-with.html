Learn how to make these Elegant Ombre Resin Bookmarks for the bookworms in your life!

A couple things:
- I didn't seal the vinyl because it wasn't needed, however you can definitely seal them.
- The gold paint I used dries super fast (2 seconds!!) Be aware that other brands do not and can smudge your finish :(
- Bookmarks should cure hard and not bend. If there is a bend, consider starting again and make sure your 1:1 ratio is as close as possible.
