Since element animation was introduced to Canva users, have been asking for the ability to adjust the timing. Here is a hack or workaround that will help you adjust the timing of elements. Animate Individual Multiple Elements in Canva https://youtu.be/T4K-GhQwb9A

Do not add a transition between frames, as this causes a jitter. It might look strange in Canva, but it all works fine without the transition when exported.

Starting with a video template will allow you to have more control over when your text or elements are displayed on the screen.
