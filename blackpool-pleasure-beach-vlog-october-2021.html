Join me as I return to Blackpool Pleasure Beach on a quiet October weekday to experience all of the different rides at the park! In this vlog I share a mix of on and off ride footage from throughout Pleasure Beach including POV's from all 4 dark rides, a lap round on the Pleasure Beach Express and much more. Along with that I get back on The Big One, ICON, Revolution and many more Pleasure Beach classics along with taking a look at the Halloween decorations throughout the park!
