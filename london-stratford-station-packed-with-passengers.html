Passengers have been waiting at a busy Stratford tube station after London Underground (LU) workers walked out in a dispute over rosters.

Five Tube lines will be severely affected this morning by strike action.

Transport for London (TfL) said the Central, Jubilee, Northern, Piccadilly and Victoria lines will be severely disrupted for 24 hours from 4.30am on Friday.
