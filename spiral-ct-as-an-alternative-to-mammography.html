Interview between Prof. Dr. med. Andreas Boss from the University Hospital Zurich and Dr. Anna Erat MD/PhD.



In this interview, Professor Boss presents the first and only spiral CT in use to examine the female breast. As an alternative to mammography, it is intended to lower the inhibition threshold of women to perform a breast screening.

The device has many advantages over mammography and has the potential to be a real alternative.
