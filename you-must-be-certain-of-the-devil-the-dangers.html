Seems especially pertinent *right now*, as Diamanda Galas (inadvertently?) points out in the soundtrack. The lyrics are *everything*. The editing is remastered (original film got deleted, but amazingly, I recently found the footage from which it was made), and is a tad clunky, but hopefully it gets the points across. x
