After Kyle Kuzma knocked down the go-ahead basket with 3.3 seconds remaining in regulation, DeMar DeRozan knocked down a corner three as time expired to lift the Chicago Bulls over the Washington Wizards, 120-119, marking Chicago’s 7th consecutive victory. This is DeRozan’s 2nd consecutive game-winning basket at the final buzzer. Zach LaVine (35 PTS, 5 REB) and DeMar DeRozan (28 PTS, 9 REB, 5 AST) led the way for the Bulls, while Bradley Beal tallied 27 PTS and a career-high 17 AST for the Wizards in the losing effort. The Bulls improve to 24-10 on the season, while the Wizards fall to 18-18.
