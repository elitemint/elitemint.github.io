There's probably been smaller days with a similar onshore, but these were by far the worst conditions that we've filmed in 2021.

The westerly onshore wind kicked in early, but there were still some pretty fun waves to be had at the Sandbar and Rivermouth in Canggu. Having a bunch good surfers out there probably made it look better than it was, but any time you have a section or two to hit is always better than sitting at home on the couch.
