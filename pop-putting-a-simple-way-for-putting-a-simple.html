A simple way to putt: "pop" putting.
Rather than following through with your putter, simply "pop" the ball.
This method has worked great for me, leaving balls with the right paste, no more passing the hole or leaving the ball short.
It takes a bit of practice, but once you get used to it, it works wonders.
Give it a try and let me know.
