Libertine Holdings PLC CEO Sam Cockerill tells Proactive the group is pursuing a listing on the AIM market in London to help advance their linear power systems technology.

Cockerill says Libertine's technology solution for powertrain OEMs enable efficient and clean power generation from renewable fuels. The company also provides engineering services and prototype hardware to support OEM customer evaluation of its technology, and incorporation of this technology into customer-led Linear Generator development programs.
