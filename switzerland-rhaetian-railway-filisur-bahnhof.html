Swiss Trains in the Snow!
Rhaetian Railway / Rhätische Bahn trains at Filisur Bahnhof - approximately half way between Chur & St Moritz.

Taken during a stay at the Hotel Grischuna, located at the end of the platform, during February 2020, just before Covid started to ruin things.

Some changes in the intervening time - Capricorn EMUs have since taken over the shuttle between Filisur and Davos Platz and the Ge 6/6 locos have been retired.
