BYO Musicians created this video to support Music Meets Medicine, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that donates instruments and teaching time to teenagers being treated in children’s hospitals.. Please donate today at https://byomusicians.com/Donate (This page will take you to several different links).

Dallas based BYO Musicians is an organization of musicians who play together, do business together and help the community through charity outreach. Because of the pandemic, we didn't have a way this year to play together and have our usual charity auction,m. This video is a way for us to help our favorite charity, Music Meets Medicine. Note that we have a donor who is willing to match the first $5,000 donated.

More can be found about Music Meets Medicine here: http://musicmeetsmedicine.org
