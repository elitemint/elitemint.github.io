Snakes have the unique niche role of being both the predator and the prey in the wild. On one hand, they help control pest populations. On the other hand, snakes make for a healthy snack for some animals, even poisonous ones. They do experience other unexpected challenges from all sorts of unexpected creatures, like spiders, badgers, birds, and even people.
