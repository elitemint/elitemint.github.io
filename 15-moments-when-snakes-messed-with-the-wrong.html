A mamba bite, on the other hand, has the potential to kill fifteen people. Consider how a five-pound mongoose would react to such a situation. Although deadly, the mamba is not particularly aggressive. Her venom enables her to subdue and immobilize her prey. Toxins in snake venom bind to chemical receptors in victims' bodies, interfering with nerve and muscle communication.
