Our life can suddenly change because of some unexpected event or disaster. This happens to EVERYONE, even the rich and famous. You may not realize it, but the more powerful the event is, the more powerfully you could be launched into a life you never expected... and probably would never have ended up in if it didn't happen. Power is energy. Might as well do something constructive with all that energy.
