Since 2019 Pim Reijerink and Mickey van den Borst started Zerostock B.V. With many years of experience at different companies in the stocklotbranche, we combined forces and decided to start our own company. We are specialized in all sorts of goods:

-Stocklots
-B-choice goods
-Shop returns
-Furniture

-Toys
