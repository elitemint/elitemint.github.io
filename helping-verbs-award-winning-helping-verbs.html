Students are encouraged not only to find and use helping verbs, but to clearly understand how helping verbs change the context of a sentence. Understanding Helping Verbs sets the framework for Advanced Helping Verbs in which the same steps are used, but adverbs and other parts of speech must be ruled out as helping verbs are located.
