I go Pike fishing in a big carp water. GP could not resist the urge any more. The weather is mild, the breeze out of the South West, and no rain for some time. Surely ideal conditions for catching a British Pike? But he decides to try somewhere different. A venue noted for huge Carp in excess of 40lbs...but will he be able to track down a Pike in this type of fishery? It's at Sandhurst Lake so watch the film to find out...Also he asks which is the anglers favourite knife for fishing? and closes out the episode with some mixed fishing madness, dedicated to the "All-round" anglers that chase different species, from freshwater to sea...Sit back and enjoy...it's almost as good as going fishing yourself!
