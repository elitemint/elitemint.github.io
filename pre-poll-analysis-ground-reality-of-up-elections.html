In this episode of Beyond Headlines, Senior Journalist and Founder of The Public India Anand Vardhan Singh talks to another Senior Journalist and Founder of HW News Network Sujit Nair and they both discuss the ground reality of the upcoming Uttar Pradesh Assembly Elections in 2022. They discuss in detail the raids that took place on Samajwadi Party Leaders and close aides and SP President Akhilesh Yadav and how is it going to affect the sentiment of voters in UP and whether central agencies like Income Tax department, Enforcement Department, or Central Bureau of Investigation are being misused by Narendra Modi Government. It has also become new normal opposition parties being raided by central agencies just before elections.
They also talk about how beneficial would be Shivpal Yadav for Akhilesh Yadav as he has made a Ghar Wapsi after being thrown out of SP and floated his own party Pragatisheel Samajwadi Party (Lohia). They further discuss the Prime Minister’s frequent visit to UP and the effect of the inauguration of renovated Kashi Vishwanath Temple in Varanasi.

Finally, they talk about the actual situation of UP Polls 2022 and who has the advantage over others as of now?
