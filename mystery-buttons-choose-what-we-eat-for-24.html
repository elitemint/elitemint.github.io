Today we are not deciding what we will eat for 24 hours, but mystery buttons will choose what we eat. Mom surprised the Krew with a fun challenge, she set up buttons on a table and a label above it. The labels read breakfast, snack, lunch, and dinner. Each member of the Krew had to press one button of each label to decide what they will eat for 24 hours. There were four buttons under each category. Only one of the buttons in each category was a yummy food item. For breakfast, Cilla didn’t really get what she wanted. Cilla had to eat bananas for breakfast, which she hates. Liam had to eat an acai bowl, which he had no clue what that was. Maddy had to add orange juice to her cereal and Anthony had the best breakfast. Cinnamon Buns! Maddy is a very picky eater, and this challenge wasn’t that easy for her. Do you think she pressed on a button that had good food items, or did she have bad luck? You must watch and see until the end to see what everyone was eating for 24 hours. We hope you enjoy this video as much as we enjoyed filming it.
