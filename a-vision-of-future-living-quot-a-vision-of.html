"A vision of Future Living" is a chapter derived from the project of 'FANTASTICA, Extraordinary Everywhere with Formica®'. It is an experimental interior design concepts showcasing elemental design creations and innovative function for the evolving demands of modern lifestyle.

Covid-19 has changed the world in numerous ways.

With working from home and online learning being the new norm, it made us question what really matters - new home essentials that will help you live a more fulfilled life.

With living spaces being more and more restrictive, it is vital that interior design surpasses being aesthetically pleasing and becomes multifunctional.
How do we play into this post-pandemic world union with extensive versatility in terms of design application and features?

Fantastica celebrates bold design and form-defying creations to inspire new uses for HPL surfaces. From tech-novations to visual expressions, our goal is to make laminates a commodity of creativity — a catalyst that speaks remarkable possibilities.

Together, we imagine a new way of life through great design.
