PolyWave is an Excellent Pad synthesizer for iOS and in this episode of haQ attaQ, I want to show you why that is. This thing has so much on offer and one of the most interesting features is the waveform sequencer. PolyWave was made by Fingerlab in collaboration with Jordan Rudess and have been inspired by the KORG Wavestation synth.
