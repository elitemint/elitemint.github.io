It’s the brand new Mitsubishi Outlander, and we’ve driven it extensively in town and country! It looks far more sophisticated outside, the interior is a huge upgrade, and it’s pretty affordable. Designed to compete with everything from the Toyota RAV4 to the Mazda CX-5 and even CX-8, the seven-seat Outlander is now based on the next-gen Nissan X-Trail CMF-CD platform, and uses the Nissan 2.5-litre naturally aspirated petrol four-cylinder.
