Release Info:
Our French brother Rafael Cerato returns to Eleatics with the smashing 'Further' release with the beauteous leading voice of Liu Bei.

The title track is the emotional and contemporaneously lyric grounded song, which will find it's place for peak time moments.

German duo Hidden Empire (Stil Vor Talent, Octopus, Senso Sounds) are rolling out the original through the hypnotic extent of harmonies and Liu's voice.

'Elixir' starts with the firm bass of Rafael: progressive shaded, but while groove filled at the same time.

Arpeggios including 'Nuclea' is closing the pack, forming another great release by Rafael on our list, so be sure to get your attention on it.

We set no boundaries and will never do.
