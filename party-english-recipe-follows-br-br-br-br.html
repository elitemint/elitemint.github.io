Spaghetti bolognese with tomatoes with rice cooker

Ingredients:
Frozen boneless pork chop 4 slices
Spaghetti 250g
Tomato ketchup 1 bottle
Cheese 4 slices
Tomatoes 4 Nos.

Preparation:
1. Pork chop, with plastics, defrost in tap water.
2. Heat up a wok of water at high flame.
3. Put spaghetti, cover up the wok. Heat up at high flame. Turn to low flame. Boil for 5 minutes.
4. Pork chop has been defrosted, pour away water.
5. Pork chop, cut into small pieces. Put it into blender. Add seasoning:
a. Pepper little
b. Oyster sauce 1 tbsp
c. Tapioca starch 0.5 tbsp
d. Light soya sauce 0.5 tsp
e. Oil 0.5 tbsp
Blend well.
6. Tomatoes, rinse with tap water. Dice in cubes.
7. Spaghetti, get it drained. Put into rice cooker. Add in:
a. A thick slice of butter, mix well.
b. Tomato ketchup 2/3 bottle, mix well.
c. Sugar 1 tbsp, mix well.
8. Use "super quick cooking" mode to cook the spaghetti.

Steps:
1. Low flame, melt a thin slice of butter in wok.
2. Put minced pork meat in wok. Fry well at high flame.
3. Turn to low flame, add in 1/3 bottle of ketchup and 300ml of water in wok.
4. There are still 12 minutes left in the spaghetti cooking. Open the cooker and check it.
5. Add in cheese slice into the spaghetti. Mix well and continue cooking.
6. Bolognese, add in 0.5 tbsp of sugar.
7. Add in tomatoes.
8. Spaghetti has been cooked well, put on plate.
9. Bolognese, add in little tapioca sauce. Heat it up.
10. Bolognese, is to be put on top of spaghetti.
11. Tomatoes, just put next to the spaghetti.
12. Complete. Serve.
