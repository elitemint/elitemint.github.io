Roseville Police just cleared the scene of a high speed Corvette crash along Foothills Blvd in Roseville.

The vehicle split in half on impact and send debris across the roadway.

According to radio traffic, no one was injured in the crash.

Traffic along Foothills Blvd was impacted while crews cleaned up the scene, from Denio Loop to Vineyard Rd.
