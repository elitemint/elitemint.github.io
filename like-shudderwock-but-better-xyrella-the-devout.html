We do this by playing deathrattle minions like Zombie Chow, Hench-Clan Shadequill, and Corrupted Healbot throughout the game. Then, on the combo turn we play Embrace the Shadow and Xyrella the Devout for the OTK! Super fun meme deck! Good luck!
