HOLLY loves tattoos so much, before the Covid19 pandemic began, she would get a new tattoo once a month. Describing her style as androgynous and tomboy, Holly has tattoos on the side of her head, while her neck and forearms are inked entirely black and she wears her hair short and bleached. Holly got her first tattoo when she was 18 but started getting more heavily tattooed from age 21 and is aiming for 80% coverage. Holly says she has been a tomboy “from the womb” and she typically dresses in oversized tees and shorts or ripped jeans from unisex or men’s brands, including her own clothing line. Holly wore trousers at school and was head girl. Everyone expected her to wear a suit at prom so she wore a black and blue corset with a black skirt. However, In her current wardrobe she has no skirts or dresses and has never worn heels. What then will she make of a girly glam transformation with a dress straight out of prom?
