Tuberous begonias are a big deal plant for those who have shady gardens because begonias flower non-stop from summer to fall! Begonia tubers are planted in the spring and can be started indoors or outdoors. Shirley Bovshow shows you easy steps for starting begonia tubers indoors how to plant them directly in the ground outdoors.

✅ Learn about different varieties of flowering tuberous begonias; there are cascading begonias for hanging baskets, mounding begonias, upright begonias for window boxes all filled with flowers for months! There are few plants that flower as prolifically in the shade garden.

✅ You will learn:
How to pre sprout begonia tubers (bulbs)
How to start tuberous begonias indoors
How to plant tuberous begonias directly in the ground
When to plant begonia tubers
How to care for tuberous begonias
