Exclusive electronic renewal of all categories of residence permits for third country nationals
The new service of the Ministry of Migration and Asylum concerns approximately 700,000 beneficiaries.
The application will be available from November 16, 2021, through the electronic services of the Ministry at the electronic address: https://applications.migration.gov.gr/metanasteusi/.
The process is simple and safe. It requires the identification of the user via mobile phone and email. The communication with the applicant will be done only electronically. The submission of the application by an attorney-at-law will take place through the electronic platform of portal.olomeleia.gr.
