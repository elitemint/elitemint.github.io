In this video tutorial, I teach you how to watch satellite tv channels offline on your PC/Laptop and Cellphone without internet connection.

First I introduce the hardware and software requirements.
Then explain how the hardware must be connected in order to receive satellite signal from Dish/LNB/Switch/Coaxial cable and finally the receiver itself.

I introduce Technisat SkyStar S2 PCI card to receive and watch satellite channels.
Then I walk you through the software requirements like: SkyStar S2 Setup 4 PC as the PCI card’s driver and official software designed to be installed and used by windows and Prog DVB software as the satellite TV channel scanner and player.

I also teach you how to watch it on Android phone by downloading, installing and configuring Prog TV Android App.

So unleash the power of satellite sharing at your place by watching and learning my video tutorial.
