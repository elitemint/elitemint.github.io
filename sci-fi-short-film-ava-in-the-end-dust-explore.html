More About "Ava In The End":
A white room, an annoying computer, dark bacon chocolate. Ava has just died and while she awaits for her consciousness to be downloaded into a new body, she must deal with "computer" who is...difficult to say the least. But as the wait continues and the tensions grow, Ava realizes that things aren't as they seem. Something sinister? Maybe -- or maybe the her best friend Rebecca is messing with her. At least, please let that be true. Ava in the End is a black-comedy that asks the question: what if at the end of your life the only company you had was an annoying artificial intelligence who won't shut the &*$# up.
