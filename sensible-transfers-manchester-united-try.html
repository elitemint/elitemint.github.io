In this Sensible Transfers series, In this series, we will analyse teams, identify problem areas, and suggest solutions in the form of incoming players.

Manchester United are at the beginning of a Rangnick revolution. Famed for his high energy high pressing Red Bull teams Rangnick needs players that can cope with this type of game. Whilst he may have players ready for this system, United have holes in their squad that need to be filled with a Rangnick type of player.
