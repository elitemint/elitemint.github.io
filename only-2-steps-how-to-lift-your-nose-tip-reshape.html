How to Lift your nose tip naturally.

The shape of the tip of the nose has a tremendous influence on the overall appearance of the nose.
A tip that is too wide, can diminish the overall beauty of the face.

When you have a higher nose tip will help make the large nose wing look smaller and prominent nose.
Helps the face look attractive and confident.

Today we have a massage method to lift the nose tip up naturally with techniques from Japan.

Let's get started.

Step 1
Put your hands together, put the little fingers into the nostrils.
Pull down and lift them up, hold for 1 minute.
Helps to lift the nose tip naturally.


Step 2
Place your little finger on the slit of your upper lip (Philtrum).
Hold the tip of the nose up with another finger and hold for 1 minute.
Helps the tip of the nose to be raised from the base of the nose, the wings of the nose appear smaller.

Finished.

Do it once a day regularly for 2 consecutive months and check the results.
