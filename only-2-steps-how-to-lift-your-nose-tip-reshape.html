Today we have a massage method to lift the nose tip up naturally with techniques from Japan.

Let's get started.

Step 1

Pull down and lift them up, hold for 1 minute.
Helps to lift the nose tip naturally.


Step 2
Place your little finger on the slit of your upper lip (Philtrum).
Hold the tip of the nose up with another finger and hold for 1 minute.
Helps the tip of the nose to be raised from the base of the nose, the wings of the nose appear smaller.
