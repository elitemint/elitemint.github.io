Donald Trump is no stranger to anti-semitic comments and viewpoints and he was at it again during a recent interview with journalist Barak Ravid. During the interview, Trump claimed that American Jewish people don’t care about the state of Israel, Congress was under "absolute power" of Israel until the Obama Administration, and that the New York Times is run by Jewish people that also hate Israel. And they’re Jewish people that run The New York Times. I mean, the Sulzberger family.”"
