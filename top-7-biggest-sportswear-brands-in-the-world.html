Sports brands or, more precisely, sportswear brands have been booming for quite some time now. More and more people all over the world are joining in on the trend for more exercise and sports in their daily lives. An unsurprising development considering an active lifestyle has proven to be beneficiary to your health and life expectancy. And yet, there is still a lot of potential to grow for these sports brands since there are still too many people who do not get the recommended amount of exercise. In order to exercise you need the right gear that will support you during your hopefully regular dose of sporting activity and make you feel as comfortable as possible.

Take all these facts together and you won’t find it surprising that the global sports brand have steadily grown over the last few decades and are now generating more than $100 billion in revenues put together.
