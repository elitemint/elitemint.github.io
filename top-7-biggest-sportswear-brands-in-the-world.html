Sports brands or, more precisely, sportswear brands have been booming for quite some time now. And yet, there is still a lot of potential to grow for these sports brands since there are still too many people who do not get the recommended amount of exercise. Take all these facts together and you won’t find it surprising that the global sports brand have steadily grown over the last few decades and are now generating more than $100 billion in revenues put together.
