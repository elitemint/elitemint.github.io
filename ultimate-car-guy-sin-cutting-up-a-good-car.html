Everyone seems to be losing their minds on this 1956 sedan delivery. "save it" "cut up the nomad" "cut up the 4 door wagon". I've never seen so much passion about a ratty old parts car.

Unfortunately the better the car the better it is as a parts car. I'm not a fan of sedan deliveries and judging by how long it was up for sale, I'm not alone.

It will live on and help build other cars. 1 car on the road is better than 2 cars in a field.
