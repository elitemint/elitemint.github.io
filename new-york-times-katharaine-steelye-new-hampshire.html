UNACCEPTABLE I TELL YOU! COMPLETELY UNACCEPTABLE!!

The solution? Forced ethnic integration - oh, and I am certain it's not going to matter if the targets cater to EXTREME CULTURAL DIFFERENCES - so long as New Hampshire is....'diverse'.

New policies that incentivize businesses and locals. Many ideas are still being floated around to FORCE this diversity - whether it's beneficial or not, an individual's color seems to be the force focal-point of this movement.

This silent movement to continue eradicating White people is obviously happening here. It's an eradication of a culture, and - just like doing this to any other ethnic group - it's a disgusting act.

Here's my view regarding the reason why flooding New Hampshire (or any state in the United States) with multi-culture/multi-ethnic/multi-diversity/whatever needs to be organic, and not forced. Otherwise, we'd bring in a lot of people who refuse to acclimate and segregate themselves to cause more rift and chaos eventually.
