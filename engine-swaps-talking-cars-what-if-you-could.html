There have been a thousand instances where you have wondered if a particular car came with a certain engine. While manufacturers have their reasons to not do it, there is nothing stopping us from imagining your own hotrod versions of everyday cars. As an enthusiast you are always looking for a fun car that's very affordable and there is nothing like plonking a big engine into a small car.

We list out some of the fantasy builds we would do if we could swap an engine for another. These are not legal but if you have access to a race track, these are the most fun you can have on a budget.
