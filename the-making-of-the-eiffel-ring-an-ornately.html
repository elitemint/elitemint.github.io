Paris... the city of love and romance... where poets write, artists paint and dreamers dream...

Perhaps you met your loved one in this magical city, or perhaps this is the city your loved one has always wanted to visit and where you intend to propose...

I can just see it now... a warm evening in Paris, the moon is out, the stars are twinkling and the Eiffel Tower is in the background... this is it! the perfect moment for the perfect ring!

Well, this ring was designed with this moment in mind, inspired by the iconic Eiffel Tower, in order to capture the essence of this magical city and treasure this special moment forever.

In this video you will see the creation of this ring from start to finish as it is carved from a block of wax, cast , carved, set and polished!

This video is not a tutorial and is intended with the sole purpose of sharing my making process and watching the magic of transforming base materials into a beautiful piece of jewellery.

If this video inspires you to want to learn how to make jewellery I would recommend checking out the wonderful array of classes at Vanilla Ink Jewellery School (vanillainkstudios.com) where I am both a Tutor and the Jeweller in Residence.

I hope you enjoy being at my side, watching me work away at this beautiful engagement ring.

If you do enjoy this video and haven't already, then don't forget to subscribe to my YouTube Channel and click the notifications button which keeps you up to date to any new videos coming out!

Also, If you are looking for a beautiful bespoke engagement ring to be made just for you, then feel free to contact me directly through my social media or my website.
