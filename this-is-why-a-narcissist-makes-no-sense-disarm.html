If you have lived with or married or loved a narcissist, you know that the gaslighting phrases they use can be mentally and emotionally debilitating. However, when you DETACH and OBSERVE the workings of their mind, you step out of the toxic emotional soup and you are far more able to see the ridiculousness of their thinking.

While it is never wise to battle, argue or fight with a narcissist, learning to disarm their mental power over you is critical. Learning to disengage from a narcissist allows you the space you'll need to start your recovery journey.
