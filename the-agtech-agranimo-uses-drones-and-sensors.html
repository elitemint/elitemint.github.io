In this interview, we talk to Nikita Gulin, CEO & Co-founder (https://www.linkedin.com/in/nikitagulin/) of the startup Agranimo (formerly known as Intelag (https://www.intelag.net/)). Agranimo helps farmers and fruit farmers to increase their output with the same resources with what is called “precision agriculture”. They collect data with microclimate sensors for soil, weather and so on. Based on this data they provide an actionable recommendation to help the farmers grow more fruits. Startups like Agranimo, who help in agriculture are commonly called agtechs. To learn more about this amazing agtech in our interview.
