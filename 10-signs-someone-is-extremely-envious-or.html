Here are some tell-tale signs that someone is extremely envious or jealous of you! If you’re wondering how to know if someone is jealous of you, chances are that you’re already aware of some of the actions of a jealous person. Generally, jealous people suffer from an inferiority complex and have a strong desire for the things others have – be it talents, skills, personality traits, material things, relationships, or achievements.

Some signs of envy are more subtle, but there are certain signs someone is jealous of you that are hard to miss. And while we all experience this emotion from time to time, some individuals simply can’t move past it. Unfortunately, envious people are toxic people who just drain your energy and delight in seeing you struggle and deflate.

So it’s important to learn to recognize the signs of jealousy in friendship or in a relationship early on.
