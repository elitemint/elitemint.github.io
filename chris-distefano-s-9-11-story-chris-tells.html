Chris Distefano shares on September 11th, 2019 his experience of where he was when the Twin Towers fell on 9/11/2001. He was in high school at the time at an all-boys catholic school experimenting with Winstrol. The emotions really got to him when he thought his mother had died in the attack as she worked in the second Twin Tower. She survived and was okay but as the city was mourning, Chris was facing expulsion. WATCH THE WHOLE CLIP AND HEAR HIS TRUE STORY!!
