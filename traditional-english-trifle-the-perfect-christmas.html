In a small pan boil the sugar and water for about 5 minutes to make a syrup. Add the sherry (if using) to the syrup and pour over the sponge.

Bring the 1 pint of heavy cream to a boil in a large saucepan. Whilst it is heating, mix the egg yolks, sugar, vanilla and cornstarch together in a small bowl. Pour the boiling cream over the egg mix and return it to the pan. Stir over a low heat until it just starts to thicken. Don‚Äôt over cook.
