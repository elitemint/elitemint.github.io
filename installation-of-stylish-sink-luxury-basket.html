ST-03 Luxury Stainless Steel Sink Basket Strainer

Upgrade your kitchen or laundry room sink with this sleek removable deep basket strainer by STYLISH® designed to add functionality and style.

This functional kitchen sink strainer will add a Stylish touch to your kitchen. This premium sink drainer comes with a luxury basket strainer. They are deeper and much more functional for catching food scraps than the regular strainers.
These sink strainers will perfectly fit any sink with a 3.5 Inch opening.
Made of quality T-304 stainless steel, this strainer seamlessly fits with most stainless steel kitchen sinks and it is also a great accent piece for ceramic or stone composite sinks.

**PREMIUM MATERIAL: SUS 304 Stainless Steel with an optimized 18% Chromium and 10% Nickel content, this sink drainer is superbly crafted to provide excellent strength and hardness. The metal threads allow higher torque to guarantee optimal sealing and prevent leaks.

**REMOVABLE BASKET STRAINER: The basket strainer can be lifted out of the drain opening, allowing you to easily clear any trapped matter
