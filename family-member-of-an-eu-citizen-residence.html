In this video, we teach you everything you need to know about the residence permit in Spain as a Family Member of an EU Citizen!

Do you have a Spanish relative? Is your partner or spouse a citizen of the European Union? If so, you can obtain your right to live and work in Spain legally with complete ease.

All you have to do is apply for the residence permit as a family member of an EU Citizen. And that is something really simple: you will only need to constitute yourself in marriage or common-law couple and the procedure will go smoothly.
