Raheem Sterling scored the only goal of the game - his 100th in the Premier League - as City beat a well-organised Wolves side to extend our lead at the top of the table.

It was a day of significant milestones at the Etihad, with Ederson's 100th clean sheet for City adding to Sterling's 100th Premier League goal.
