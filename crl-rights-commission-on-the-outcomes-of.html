CRL Rights Commission will be having an urgent media briefing regarding the outcomes of meetings it had this week with the Eastern Cape Provincial leadership, COGTA, and the Provincial House of Traditional Leaders. The purpose of the meeting was to address the escalating deaths of approximately 32 initiates who have died at the initiation schools in that province as well as those who are currently hospitalised.
