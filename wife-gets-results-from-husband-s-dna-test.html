Claire knew that she was in love with Mike from the moment they met. But Mike would only put the moves on her once they both turned 18.

After that, the two lived a happy love-drunk life together. But there was something lurking in the shadows that they couldn’t have prepared for.

But two years down the line everything would change and they wouldn’t see it coming.
