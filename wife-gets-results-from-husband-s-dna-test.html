Wife Gets Results From Husband’s DNA Test, Realizes What She’s Done


Claire felt her hands shake as she looked down and read the document. How could this be true? Was it some kind of joke? She felt her stomach tighten into a knot as she stumbled to the other side of the room.

She came to realize that it was no joke and that the words she was reading on the paper were indeed real. Who could a single piece of paper bring her whole world crashing down?

Claire knew that she was in love with Mike from the moment they met. But Mike would only put the moves on her once they both turned 18.

After that, the two lived a happy love-drunk life together. But there was something lurking in the shadows that they couldn’t have prepared for.


On their 10th wedding anniversary, the two received the news that Claire was pregnant. They both cried tears of joy and were ready to have the laughter of a child in their home.

But two years down the line everything would change and they wouldn’t see it coming.



Mike was celebrating his 30th birthday and Claire wanted to do something that he would never forget. She decided to get him a beautiful cake, lots of balloons, and invite his closest friends. But she also bought him a present – a DNA kit.
