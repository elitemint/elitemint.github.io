Penny Hunt and Fill #42 - Can We Make Progress?
I've decided to start a new series and see how far we can get at attempting to fill up a Dansco Penny Folder. There are 234 slots in this Folder but we don't expect to fill them all through coin roll hunting. Instead, we want to see how long it will take to fill the book with everything but the Key Dates, Semi-Key Dates and Steel Cents. Obviously, we'll be praying for some of those along the way!
This is part 42 of the series (Boxes 70 and 71) and I do plan on updating it from each penny box I hunt from this point forward until the penny coin collection is nearly completed! I will say in advance that once we get to a point where it is nearly impossible to add any more wheat cents to the album, we will then halt the series and try to fill the rest of the slots with existing finds or coins in my duplicates collection.
Keep hunting and good luck!
They're still out there for us to find!
