A FORMER TEACHER says there is no chance she would ever go back to teaching after landing a lucrative new job as an OnlyFans model. When she made the career switch and started posting in suggestive snaps online - she saw her income skyrocketing to $100,000 a month. The mum-of-two says the financial security she has been able to give her family proves she made the right choice - even if not all family and friends agree with her decision. "OnlyFans has improved our life in so many ways," Courtney told Truly. "Having the financial freedom to say yes to our kids is incredible."
