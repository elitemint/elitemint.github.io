In this episode of the Women in Maintenance Podcast, we have Maureen Gribble, Director of Marketing for UE Systems, on the show! Tune in to hear Maureen discuss the importance of ultrasound technology in our industry, what's evolved since the start of her career, and how powerful it is to forge strategic partnerships.
