This general effect driven show by Randall Standridge explores the elements of art. Standridge's work Vanishing Point serves as inspiration for the opening segment "Lines." Additional sound FX and narration provide multiple opportunities for staging, props, and creativity to provide a "high end" show that is still audience friendly.
