ABOUT THE VIDEO:
In this video, Jordan Peterson talks about certain traits a man needs to possess so that they can be deemed desirable. He starts off the video by describing how sexual selection is not a joke. The video has also been edited into three parts. The first part talks about the two traits; Dominance and Good health. Here is a quick recap. Jordan Peterson describes that females tend to choose males who are above the dominance hierarchy. He does better explaining in the video, so go find out more. The second trait he discusses is usefulness. It may sound simplistic but a study he conducted clearly shows that women choose more useful men regardless of their wealth status. Finally, He gives quick advice on how to become a desirable man. I hope you enjoy the video.
