ABOUT THE VIDEO:
In this video, Jordan Peterson talks about certain traits a man needs to possess so that they can be deemed desirable. He starts off the video by describing how sexual selection is not a joke. The video has also been edited into three parts. The first part talks about the two traits; Dominance and Good health. Here is a quick recap. Jordan Peterson describes that females tend to choose males who are above the dominance hierarchy. He does better explaining in the video, so go find out more.
