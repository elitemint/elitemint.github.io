It appears Victorian Premier Daniel Andrews will "stop at nothing to cozy up" to the CCP, according to Sky News host Peta Credlin.

Six months after the federal government tore up Victoria's Belt and Road deal with Beijing, the Andrews government is using taxpayer funds to purchase a stall at the China International Import Expo.

Ms Credlin discussed the issue with Sky News host Rowan Dean, who said this is "unbelievable".

"Here we go again," Mr Dean said.
