ROBINSONS FUENTE. Located at the heart of the city at Fuente Osmeña Avenue, this 4-level shopping mall boasts of an exciting mix of dining, retail and service establishments that serves the needs of the Cebuanos.

ROBINSONS CYBERGATE CEBU. Sets the stage for a very unique and exciting tenant mix as popular food chains, exquisite dining outlets, specialty shops and services, offices and medical clinics all gather under one roof. Robinsons Cybergate Cebu is also the province's first wellness and lifestyle center. This 4-level commercial establishment has paved the way in providing variety and convenient malling experience not only to its tenant business communities and medical practitioners but also to the surrounding hospitals, schools and commercial establishments.

ROBINSONS GALLERIA CEBU. Strategically located along General Maxilom Avenue, Robinsons Galleria Cebu is the newest shopping destination in the Queen City of the South showcasing a world-class architecture and vibrant indoor landscapes. This six-storey commercial complex houses more than 200 tenants offering an eclectic mix of local, national and international brands that will complement major anchor.

AYALA CENTER CEBU. A large shopping mall owned by Ayala Malls at the Cebu Business Park in Cebu City, Philippines. It is the first Ayala mall located outside of Metro Manila. It opened in November 1994. It has "The Terraces" which converted the mall's lagoon area into a food, restaurant and beverage mall extension.

AYALA MALLS CENTRAL BLOC. Large shopping mall in Cebu IT Park developed by Cebu Holdings Incorporated, becoming the second Ayala Mall in Cebu City, Philippines in 25 years since the opening of Ayala Center Cebu and its fifth regional mall in Visayas and Mindanao. It has a gross leasable area (GLA) of 45,000 square meters.The mall opened last December 6, 2019.

SM CITY CEBU. Also known locally as SM Cebu or sometimes SM Mabolo, is a large shopping mall located in Cebu City, Philippines. It is the 4th shopping mall owned and developed by SM Prime Holdings, the country's largest shopping mall owner and developer.

SM SEASIDE CITY CEBU. Also known locally as SM Seaside or just Seaside, is a large shopping mall owned and developed by SM Prime Holdings in Cebu City, Philippines that opened last 27 November 2015, exactly 22 years after SM City Cebu, which opened in 1993. It is the centerpiece of the SM - Ayala Land Joint Venture Estate called South Coast City, a 30-hectare mixed-use development at the South Road Properties.
