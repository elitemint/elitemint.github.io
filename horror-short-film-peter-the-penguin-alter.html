More About "Peter the Penguin":
Starting off as a very British comedy of social embarrassment PETER THE PENGUIN veers off into something very weird and dark indeed. Grotesque, grimly funny, utterly absurd, yet genuinely nightmarish and really rather horrible, this is one you won't forget any time soon. And you will never look at a stuffed toy penguin in the same way again.
