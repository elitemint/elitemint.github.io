Up this week, we'll be taking a look at the Portuguese national operator, Comboios de Portugal (CP) and their Intercidades (Intercity) trains. They use two types of coaches and, as you'll see, it's a bit of a lottery as to which type of coach you'll get, as the level of comfort varies drastically between the two!

Date of Travel: June 2021
Class of Travel: 1st Class
Rolling Stock: Class 5600 with Corail and Sorefame Modernizada Coaches
Type of Service: Intercidades
Train Number: IC720/IC572
Origin: Porto-Campanhã, Portugal
Destination: Lisboa Oriente, Lisbon, Portugal
Cost of Ticket: €45 (£38.10, $52)
