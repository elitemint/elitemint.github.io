On November 16, 2021, another natural disaster struck Italy. In the city of Catania, the region of Sicily, a huge destructive tornado has formed in the sea and on land. The natural disaster was accompanied by strong hurricane winds, rains and devastating hail. It is known about at least two such tornadoes that took place in Trapani, in Marinella di Selinunte and in Catania. At the moment it is difficult to say whether they caused any material damage, information about the victims has not yet been reported. The inhabitants of the region were greatly frightened, and shipping was temporarily suspended. Many cars were damaged as a result of the hail. Also on the night before, thousands of lightning strikes were seen in the sky over the region, which prevented the inhabitants from sleeping.
In 2021, natural disasters have become more frequent. We are seeing climate change in many countries around the world. It is not known how 2021 will end.
