Joining in Metallica’s 40th anniversary celebrations, the boiz paid tribute to their mighty Bay Area brethren with an Xmas setlist that spread holiday cheerz and beerz everywhere.

Forget It's A Wonderful Life, the Electric Happy Hour Xmas Spectacular is THE essential viewing for the holiday season. So put on your Xmas sweater, grab some eggnog and rum and join the boyz as they deliver one of the hardest rockin' Happy Hours to date.

As well as the expected Machine Head bangerz like "Old", "Imperium" and "Aesthetics of Hate", Robb and Jared delivered an awesome tribute to Metallica, with a career spanning covers set including "No Remorse", "Damage Inc.", "Enter Sandman" and "Hardwired”.
