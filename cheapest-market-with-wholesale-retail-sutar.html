पत्ता:
सुतार चाळ, झवेरी बाजार, मुंबई 400002
जवळील स्टेशन: (CR) CST & (WR) MARINE LINES

If your want to buy anything for festival items, diwali, new business or for any wholesale or retail shopping, ‘Sutar Chawl’ is a Best Option😊👍🏻.
it’s a most unexplored market in Mumbai. There you will get diya, kandil, rangoli, lighting, gifts, birthday party items, wedding decoration, return gifts, imitation Jewelery, hair accessories, cutlery, stationary like papers, paper boxes etc. In very affordable rates but as it’s a wholesale market you have to buy it in 6 pieces, in dozen or in quantity. ITS A ONE OF THE MOST UNEXPLORED WHOLESALE MARKET.
