Become a WSA Academy Member and get in touch with fellow composers or music industry professionals!

If you are a professional composer for screen or another professional active in the film music industry, we invite you to become a member of the World Soundtrack Academy.
Membership is open to professional screen composers and other film music industry professionals, such as music executives, music supervisors, music editors, agents, managers, producers and publicists.
Membership provides invaluable support for the film music activities and events of the World Soundtrack Academy, organised for the benefit of the international film music industry.

More info on www.worldsoundtrackawards.com
