‘Kena Check’ is a new brand song produced and performed by Joe Flizzow, Hullera and Bunga in collaboration with realme Malaysia’s ‘Dare To Be You’ campaign. In line with our Dare to Leap spirit, we dare all our fans to stay true to themselves and be YOU. Let's be crazy! Be different! Be real! Be the change! Most of all, be YOU!

Joe Flizzow - I understand everything in our lives happens for a reason and we need to strive for excellence because we only live once.

Hullera - I am really happy to work with realme Malaysia again as the spirit of ‘Dare to Be You’ really touches me and I feel like everyone should be comfortable and proud to be themselves in this modern era.

Bunga - This is one of my dreams to work on a track with Joe Flizzow and Hullera. It proves to me that if I ever wanted to give up, my journey in this industry would be a waste.

Through realme’s Dare to Leap and Dare to Be You spirit, another disruptive device was born to take the leap forward in this 5G era, the realme GT Master Edition. The realme GT Master Edition will be available from 9th September onwards. Check out realme official website: https://www.realme.com/my/ for more info.
