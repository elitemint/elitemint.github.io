In today's vlog we head to Walt Disney's Magic Kingdom for a Very Merriest After Hours Event! We hope this vlog will help you plan your night at the party! We started our night a couple of hours before the event started and we took a look at some holiday merch and tried to find some food to eat. It was hard to find during the fireworks and before the special after hours event started. But we tried the Mr. Toad’s Brat Burger and it wasn’t as bad as people were saying. Then we tried some of the free hot chocolate and cookies before heading over to get our spot for the Christmas parade. It was so nice to finally have a full parade!! We also got to see Minnie’s Wonderful Christmastime Fireworks Show, which was so so good! We ended our night by watching the Mickey & Minnie’s Very Merry Memories stage show. We had so much fun at the party and it really felt like we were getting so much closer to having the regular Mickey’s Very Merry Christmas Party back soon! We hope you enjoyed hanging out with us at Disney’s Magic Kingdom tonight! Thank you for watching and we’ll see you tomorrow with a new vid!
