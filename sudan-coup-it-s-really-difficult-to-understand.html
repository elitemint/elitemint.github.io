Soldiers arrested most of the members of #Sudan's cabinet on Monday and a #military officer dissolved the transitional government, while opponents of the takeover took to the streets where #gunfire and injuries were reported. Ahmed Soliman, Research Fellow at the Chatham House, talks to FRANCE 24.
