We were blessed at this IWJG watch show in Miami, buying and selling millions in luxury watches and getting to interact with other well known grey market dealers in the process...that's always fun!

Interacting with Roman Sharf was also a lot of fun. His rare AP is pretty sick. We also made some deals with the guys from Timepiece Trading, and got to check out some of the great inventory from Timepiece Gentleman, while chatting with Alfred and Marco about some pieces.

The only problem with this show is that we got way too much footage, so based on suggestions from fans in the past, we broke the footage up into two episodes. This is the first one. The next episode will be released this weekend!
