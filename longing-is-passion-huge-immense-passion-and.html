Longing is passion huge, immense passion and yet it is but abstraction,
I understand it is an illustration of deep togetherness, mutual penetration.

Wherever u play a role, whatever role, u are human being in entirety,
U come through other’s profound togetherness, through full fertility,
The juncture u cross over or come across where u drown in emotion.

U have but to swim in the sea of reality touching its waves and storms.
U are capable though to float above, storms evolve in u like sweet thorns.
U conglomerate all those, breed new meanings & haunt for translation.
