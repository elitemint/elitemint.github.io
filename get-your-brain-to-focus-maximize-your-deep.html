Focus is becoming increasingly difficult in the digital age we are living through. There is an increasing number of adults being diagnosed with A.D.D., A.D.H.D, anxiety and more. With an overwhelming number of options at any given moment and an onslaught of information and entertainment available at your fingertips, it’s not surprising that so many people struggle with focus. Becoming a successful entrepreneur, however, and pursuing any worthwhile and meaningful goals is going to require you to focus and do deep work effectively and consistently. This is where we offer you 6 ways you can start maximizing your focus right away. Starting with deliberate practice that will take you to the edge of your competence. Being able to push through the edge of your abilities with sustained concentration is a superpower that many successful entrepreneurs and elite performers possess.
