Today's transferees, according to Carey, are increasingly opting to rent, either short-term or long-term.

Sue Carey is nationally known in relocation circles, and is a frequent speaker at industry conferences. She recently paid a visit to Brook Furniture Rental's downtown showroom for a chat about relocation with Brook's Amanda Eirich and YoChicago's Joe Zekas.
