VAL ANDRE Golf Club - Course Vlog - FOURBALLS - Part 1

Val Andre is in the Côtes-d'Armor department of Brittany in northwestern France.

Just a short Ferry adventure from Portsmouth England, to St Malo France and then 1 hour west and we find ourselves in the stunning area of Val Andre.

Myself (Dan Hendriksen), Lee Whittaker, Coach James Urquhart and Old Fold Manor Golf Operations Manager Nathan Gilpin, along with more than.just a cameraman Jordan are making our way around this beautiful 18 hole golf course right on the coast of Normandy with some spectacular views and stunning golf holes.

In today's match, it will be a 3 part series, 6 holes in each part, where Myself and Lee will go against James and Nathan for 6 holes of Fourballs, 6 holes of Scramble and finally 6 holes of Greensomes. I hope you enjoy the longer parts, and that you find changing up the formats refreshing.

Book your own trip to Val Andre:

With exceptional views along the coast, Pléneuf-Val-André is an outstanding seaside course. A mix of clifftop links and heathland, it boasts some truly memorable holes that combine undulating fairways, thick rough, and well-protected greens. Holes 9 to 12 are particularly worthy of mention and can make or break your round, but the rest of the course is impressive - including some corking short holes. Regularly ranked among the top 10 courses in France, green fees are a fraction of the price you'd pay for an equivalent course in the UK.
