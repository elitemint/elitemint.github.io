In this video, RØDE Product Specialist Ryan Burke takes you through the features and specifications of the Lavalier II premium lavalier microphone.

Key features of the Lavalier II:

- Premium lavalier microphone with a revolutionary low-profile design
- Broadcast-grade sound quality
- Very flat frequency response, low noise, and omnidirectional polar pattern
- 3.5mm TRS output with locking connector
- Includes premium accessory kit with pop shield, mini furry windshield, compact mic clip, coloured ID rings and zip case
- The perfect companion for the Wireless GO II and AI-Micro
