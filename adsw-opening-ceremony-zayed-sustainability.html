We are pleased to announce a collaboration between #Expo2020Dubai and Abu Dhabi Sustainability Week (#ADSW). Hosted by #Masdar, the ADSW Opening Ceremony and the #ZayedSustainabilityPrize Awards Ceremony will take place at the Dubai Exhibition Centre on Monday 17 January 2022.

Joining forces with #Expo2020 Dubai demonstrates the #UAE’s leading role in driving action on climate change, at home, in the region, and throughout the world.
