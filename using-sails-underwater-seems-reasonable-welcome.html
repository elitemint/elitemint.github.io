Trailmakers is about building very awesome vehicles and machines, but you don‚Äôt need an engineering degree to get started. The intuitive builder will get you going in no time. Expedition Mode is the challenging campaign mode of Trailmakers. You are competing in an off-world rally expedition with only a few building blocks to get you started.
