Using state of the art cameras, lenses, and long exposure time-lapse techniques, we are bringing to you these majestic views of our Galaxy, the Milky Way.

The Milky Way spans 100,000 light years across. Its bright core emits light that takes 26,000 years to reach Earth. The core is also home to the supermassive black hole called Sagittarius A*.

We spent over 2 years capturing about 500,000 raw long exposure photos, then rendered into this ultra HD resolution timelapse film.
