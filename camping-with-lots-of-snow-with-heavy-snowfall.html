Thanks to our Mr. Heater Buddy Heater we were able to warm our tent and get a comfortable night's sleep. We did not know our surroundings until the morning. Upon waking up we explored the beauty of our location. Winter camping is the best! The snow is stunning. We always enjoy camping in the snow.
