I was met by Matthew Conlon, the city clerk, who can be reached at Phone: 609-989-3187. Detective Sergeant Torres, Badge number 79, decided to rip the phone from my hands and grabbed me, and shoved me out of the building. He claims it is not a crime because he returned my cell phone to me, he had no right to take my phone and delete the video (had to retrieve it later on) and force me out of a public building and a public lobby. 2C:13-1 Kidnapping.
