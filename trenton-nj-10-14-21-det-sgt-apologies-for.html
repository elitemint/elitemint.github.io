I was met by Matthew Conlon, the city clerk, who can be reached at Phone: 609-989-3187. Flowers as Herb Flowers is a cop in Trenton. An Internal Affairs complaint has been signed. The Internal Affairs officer agreed that anyone can record in the public building, in a public lobby.

He claims it is not a crime because he returned my cell phone to me, he had no right to take my phone and delete the video (had to retrieve it later on) and force me out of a public building and a public lobby. 2C:13-1 Kidnapping.

I shall return.
