ROBLOX is an online virtual playground and workshop, where kids of all ages can safely interact, create, have fun, and learn. There is no cost for this first plot of virtual land. By participating and by building cool stuff, ROBLOX members can earn specialty badges as well as ROBLOX dollars (“Robux”).
