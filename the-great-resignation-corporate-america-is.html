This trend is being called “The Great Resignation” and it arrived in the wake of the lockdowns and the pandemic, sky high inflation, and social unrest the likes of which we haven’t seen for decades. Many people have been pointing to the fact that unemployment rates are falling, but despite that, less people are actually working.
