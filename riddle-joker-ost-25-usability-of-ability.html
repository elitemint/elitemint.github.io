Merry Christmas Everybody!

Riddle Joker from YuzuSoft English Version released on December 18th 2020.

Honestly It is YuzuSoft, but still I was suprised about how good this one was.

The Protagonist, his Sister and many other Characters have Abilitys called Astrals. Supernatural Powers with which they try to make Everyones Life easier and Safer.
But that isn't the Case for all Of em, and there are some who Abuse their Powers to commit Crimes, Rob Money, etc.
That's where our Main Character comes into Place.
He and his Sister work for the SFD, a secret Organisation to Hunt Down such Criminals.

In his newest Mission he Attends now is a Infiltration Mission in the School this VN takes place in 90% of the time.


There are 5 Routes in Total:
Ayase, Nanami, Mayu, Hazuki and Chisaki.

My Personal Ranking would be:
1: Chisaki
2: Ayase and Nanami
3: Mayu
4: Hazuki

It doesn't mean Hazuki's and Mayu's Routes are bad, I just like the other one's a lot more.

I Recommend you do Ayase first, because there they reveal a lot of stuff, which feels sort of like a True Route.

Comedy is as always Top Notch, I laughed out loud a lot, which basicaly only ever happened once, and was in Noble Works.

The Romance, omg the Romance is seriously the best aspect of this one.

I haven't seen a VN in a very long time, where the Main Girls are this aware of their feelings towards the Main Protagonist.
And they did act pretty quickly too on confessing, which is Very rare in most Visual Novels unfortunately.

And just overall did the Routes feel really Good.

The Music is Very Good too, with no Track that stands out of being bad.

And unlike most other Yuzusoft VN's, I could record finaly screenshots via Fraps, which in earlier Yuzusoft VN's never worked, so, Probs there too!

Honestly, I don't have a single bad thing to say about this one.

The Steam version is Censored yes, but it's not anywhere as bad as other censored Titles like Nora to Oujo to Noraneko Heart.
The Jokes, Dirty lines stay all in there, it is purely the +18 Scenes that where cut out, which is fine in my Opinion.

And even more baffling, this one did cost me just 26 €, which is not much for such a great and decently long VN.

So what would my Score be?

Honestly, I was very Happy and Satisfied with this one and couldn't find a single bad thing.

So it is gonna be Legendary 10/10

YES it is really really Good, Trust me.

Those are my Words to Riddle Joker.

See you in the next Year!
