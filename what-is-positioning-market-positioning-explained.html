POD vs. POP

Point of Parity (POP)

Point of Parity (POP) refers to the aspects of the product that are not unique and are shared by other brands. These are industry standards that are compulsory if the brand wants to be a legitimate competitor in the chosen field.

Point of Difference (POD)

Point of Difference (POD) refers to the benefits that are associated with a brand by the consumers. It provides a competitive edge by making the brand look unique and different from the rival brands.
