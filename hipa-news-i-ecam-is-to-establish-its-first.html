As a global player in every major area of glass manufacturing, the Turkish Şişecam will establish its first European glass-packaging facility in Kaposvár. This greenfield investment of EUR 220 million is to have an annual net production capacity of 330,000 tons. With two individual furnaces, the new facility is expected to begin its operations in 2023, reaching full capacity by 2025.
