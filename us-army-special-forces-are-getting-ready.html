Army special-operations units are particularly concerned, as they make up most of the US Special Operations Command’s (SOCOM) numbers.

The Army special-operations community is composed of several units with distinct capabilities.

Generally, when people refer to Army special-operations forces (ARSOF), they refer to the Green Berets of Army Special Forces, the 75th Ranger Regiment, Civil Affairs, Psychological Operations, and the Night Stalkers of the 160th Special Operations Aviation Regiment.
