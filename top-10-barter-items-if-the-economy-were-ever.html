If the economy were ever to fail, money would become useless. In a world without money one of the safest ways to procure much-needed items would be to use the bartering system. However, each trade must be well-thought out to ensure you get the most value.

In this video, Jon and Chris share their top 10 bartering items and why they chose them. Many of these might surprise you, but as you'll learn, the reasons for considering them make good sense.
