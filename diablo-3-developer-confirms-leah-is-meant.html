Diablo III Developer Chris Metzen CONFIRMS Leah is Meant to Return, possibly in Diablo 4?

During the latest Diablo lore exploration shorts,
I had highlighted the reasoning behind Leah becoming the latest incarnation of the lord of terror
Which, like her design, had recieved mixed reviews from the community.

However you feel about Leahs ulitmate portrayal in the series, most fans felt a little
shortchanged when the prime evil evaporated & unlike Albrecht or the wanderer, her body
was not present thereafter. It seems less is more though
as it was hinting at a possible return,
It's one of the last few plot threads left from the original D3 that weren't answered in Reaper of Souls,
but now more likely Diablo 4 in whatever form a fair maiden takes after being lost in the abyss

Interestingly, in the behind the scenes of Diablo 3 we learn of Leahs humble beginnings
as a side character , her rise as new side kick to Deckard Cain & ultimate fall as the new
lord of terror.
