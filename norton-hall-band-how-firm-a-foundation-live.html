The Norton Hall Band exists to strategically promote and model the foundational goal of the Department of Biblical Worship at Southern Seminary: “To train pastors who lead worship.” Each of the Norton Hall Band members has a pastor’s heart and desire to promote Christ-centered, biblically-guided worship.
