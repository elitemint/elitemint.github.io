We follow every astonishing step of the 36-hour delivery of salmon from the Norwegian arctic circle to sushi diners in Japan, via Finnair’s state-of-the-art cargo cool facility. The gigantic air freighter, the Antonov 124, delivers 30 tons of life-saving pharmaceutical manufacturing equipment. And a trailblazing airport boss goes on one of her infamous airport walkarounds.

Mega Air dives deep behind the scenes of the incredible 6 trillion-dollar air freight industry. Meeting the people, gasping at the extraordinary processes, rejoicing in the peculiar and celebrating some of the more mind-boggling items, transported in aircraft that beggar belief.
