Bunny talks us through the highs and lows of both external and internal garden boundaries. She guides us through how to design them for purpose - whether that be concealing eyesores or enhancing views and separate areas of the garden.
Bunny explains how to achieve height at minimal cost and effort and how to use varying boundary heights within a garden for maximum impact. She also talks us through see through boundaries and gates as well as using very low boundaries where appropriate.
