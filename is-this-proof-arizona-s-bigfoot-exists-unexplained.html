Luckily you won’t have to try to track this giant down yourself as beast hunter Nash Hoover and his team have done the hard work for you. Heading to Arizona himself to speak to locals and one of them had this to say: “Lots of stories from the colonial times to today of bigfoot type creatures, there hasn’t really been a huge population of grizzly bears in Arizona in 100 years.”
