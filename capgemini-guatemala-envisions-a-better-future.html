In the final stage of its "Building a Better Future" project, Capgemini Guatemala has built a fourth school for the Ciudad Quetzal Community. This amazing initiative has provided three equipped classrooms and a third mobile computer laboratory, giving 396 children in Guatemala access to an education they normally wouldn’t have been able to receive.
