✈ Ground services: check-in (concept), baggage drop-off, queues, boarding efficiency

✈ Cabin crew: presence, proactivity, friendliness, attentiveness, professionality
✈ Cabin: design, cleanliness, up to date configuration, lavatory, general condition (e.g. worn out)
✈ Seat: facilities (power outlets, USB-ports, device holder, headrest, recline), legroom, comfort, genaral condition (e.g. worn out)

✈ Service: menus, availability of different options, amenities (kit, blankets, pillow), food & drink quality, amount of food and drinks served, how many times is served, general service concept, costs
