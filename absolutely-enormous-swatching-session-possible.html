I've decided to do something different in this one and I've broken up the swatching with views of our garden, and later on, you can see the house and garden at dusk as I head out for a walk before it gets dark. This video is perfect to put on in the background while you work. Make yourself a hot drink (Green & Black's Hot Chocolate or Clipper Organic Tea with milk and one sugar for me!) and sit back, relax, and enjoy these beautiful colours.
