Yes folks, it's a long one! Nearly an hour and a half of calm swatching over the course of two autumnal days at the end of October. This video is perfect to put on in the background while you work. Make yourself a hot drink (Green & Black's Hot Chocolate or Clipper Organic Tea with milk and one sugar for me!) and sit back, relax, and enjoy these beautiful colours.
