Actor Vicky Kaushal, filmmaker Shoojit Sircar & producer Ronnie Lahiri are our guests in this latest edition of ThePrint's Off the Cuff.


In a conversation with ThePrint's Editor-in-Chief Shekhar Gupta about their upcoming film 'Sardar Udham', they talk about the challenges and dilemmas of an actor and a director. They also talk about the changing phase of Indian cinema and what goes on behind creating a historical film.
