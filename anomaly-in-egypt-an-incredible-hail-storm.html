Vacationers in Egypt were not expecting such gifts for the New Year.
Nature decided to play a joke on them.
On Saturday, January 1, severe hail fell in the city of Hurghada, located in eastern Egypt.
The hail fell in a thick veil literally in the morning, covering the beaches, streets, and hotel areas with ice.
The thickness of such a cover in some places reaches several centimeters.
Palms found themselves in a snowy landscape, unusual for these places.
Tourists who arrived in Hurghada, soak up the sun and go diving in the New Year, were forced to lock themselves in hotel rooms.
Videos from the resort are being published on social media.
Users claim to have seen hailstones the size of a quail's egg.
Streams of water flow through the streets of Hurghada, and cars have to make tracks, as if this is not happening in Africa, but somewhere in central Russia.
The hail is such that it knocks you off your feet, and the door to the room cannot be closed.
Nobody dares to approach the pool in the hotel.
This is more like a New Year's bombing.
The hail ended as suddenly as it began.
In Hurghada, the damage caused by the elements is being assessed.
And they wonder what to expect from 2022, which sends such signs in the first hours.
