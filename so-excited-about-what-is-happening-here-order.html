You know, riding with my sons over the years has always felt like an investment in our futures. Before the ride, in the moment of riding, and afterwards can be a lot of work. Sometimes as a dad you find yourself asking "why am I doing all this", or "what is the point here?"

Even though it might not seem like a huge deal to any of you watching, by the time we got back to the truck from this ride, I felt like those hours we've spent in the past are starting to pay off. Look, we just to this for fun. We aren't fast, and we aren't the best. We aren't lining up for races, because I don't care about that stuff. We are spending time together, slowly developing talents, and enjoying ourselves. What more could we ask for?
