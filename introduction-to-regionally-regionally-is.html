Regionally is an innovative investment service, restructuring and semi-automating traditional investment processes to deliver the quality, diligence and discipline of an exchange with the ease and convenience of an online environment.

Founded by Justin Urquhart Stewart and Jim Odell, Regionally aims to facilitate investments into the local economy by exclusively connecting private investors with investment ready regional scale-ups.

We facilitate investment into companies seeking between £500,000 to £15,000,000+ of growth capital. To find out more, visit our website (www.regionally.com) or email us on info@regionally.com.
