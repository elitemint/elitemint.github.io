At his press conference Jurgen Klopp addresses his past criticism of Diego Simeone who he faces when Liverpool take on Atletico Madrid in the Champions League.

Liverpool boss Klopp, speaking before his squad fly out to Madrid for Tuesday's match, also addresses Mohamed Salah's chances of winning the Ballon d'Or, and whether the forward's fantastic form helps or hinder the club in contract negotiations.

Klopp also acknowledges the rest of the squad's quality and it's importance against teams like Atletico Madrid.
