This Sunday, Pamplona in Spain and its region, like other parts of Navarra, were covered with heavy snowfall. Also, airports in this region were temporarily closed, but special ones cleared two runways at the international airport and departures, albeit with a long delay, resumed. No information about the victims has been reported.
