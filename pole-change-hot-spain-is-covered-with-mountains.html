This Sunday, Pamplona in Spain and its region, like other parts of Navarra, were covered with heavy snowfall. Up to 100 centimeters of snow fell, there were a lot of traffic accidents on the roads, resulting in huge traffic jams, many citizens got stuck in their cars, special teams cleared 500 roads and saved more than 1,500 people. Also, airports in this region were temporarily closed, but special ones cleared two runways at the international airport and departures, albeit with a long delay, resumed. No information about the victims has been reported.
