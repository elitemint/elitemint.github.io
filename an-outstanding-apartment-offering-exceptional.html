An outstanding two-bedroom, two-bathroom first floor apartment offering exceptional accommodation inside and private and secluded space outside, located 1.17 miles from Beaconsfield town and station.

Glenock Place was built in 2016, offering just 7 apartments, all finished to an exceptional standard to include beautiful, landscaped grounds and plenty of secure vehicle parking space, which is rare to find. It is the perfect space for all ages, from those looking to downsize, to professional’s wishing to live within easy reach of London, but in a rural setting.



4 Glenock Place comprises an excellent sized entrance hallway, open plan kitchen/dining/sitting room, separate drawing room, two en suite bedrooms and a guest cloakroom. The apartment enjoys floods of natural light through the four sets of bi-folding doors from each room, creating a superb open space. The open plan kitchen/dining/sitting room is complemented by the bi-folding doors opening the private balcony overlooking the grounds and the further fields. The kitchen area is fitted with a range of high-end appliances, including Miele oven, microwave, dishwasher and instant hot tap, and has an adjoining utility room fitted with all laundry appliances. This opens into the formal drawing room where there is an additional set of bi-folding doors.

Both bedrooms are of excellent size and both enjoy built in wardrobes and en suite facilities, as well as direct access onto the private balcony. The apartment includes underfloor heating and Sonos sound system throughout and there is lift access to each floor.



Gardens and Grounds,

The apartment enjoys two separate private balconies, both of good size to enjoy the Summer months and Al Fresco dining. The communal gardens are of excellent size, landscaped to a superb standard to include flower and shrub
beds throughout and a large lawned area. The apartment includes two private secure parking spaces.
