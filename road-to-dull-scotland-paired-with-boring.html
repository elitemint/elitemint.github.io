Tour Scotland video of the road to, and through Dull, Perthshire, Scotland. This small community is paired with Boring in Clackamas County, Oregon, USA. Dull consists of a single street of houses on the north side of the valley of the River Tay. Boring in Oregon was named after William H. Boring, an early resident of the area. Boring was a Union veteran who had moved out to Oregon after the Civil War. Dull is on the site of an early Christian monastery founded by St Adomnán, Abbot of Iona who died in 704
