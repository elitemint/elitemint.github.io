Put an egg into a pepper and you will be amazed! Quick Stuffed peppers recipe for all your family. If you want to try simple and delicious recipe to amaze your friends, then you should definitely try to make this meal. This easy stuffed peppers recipe is a meatless dish, but not only for vegans. Сut the top and empty the insides

Salt
Basil

Black pepper
Cheese

Oven 180C/356F for 50 minutes
