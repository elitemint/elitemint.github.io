Put an egg into a pepper and you will be amazed! Quick Stuffed peppers recipe for all your family. If you want to try simple and delicious recipe to amaze your friends, then you should definitely try to make this meal. This easy stuffed peppers recipe is a meatless dish, but not only for vegans. Every one will love it! You can use different ingredients from your fridge in order to stuff it well. Just use your imagination, my friends.

❗️ The video has subtitles in your language, please turn them on and enjoy!

Delicious stuffed peppers recipe ingredients:

3 small Paprika
Сut the top and empty the insides
Mozzarella
Salt
Basil
3 Eggs
Black pepper
Cheese
Olive oil
Oven 180C/356F for 50 minutes
Spring onions
