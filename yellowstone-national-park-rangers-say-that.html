Yellowstone National Park rangers say that something huge has just been detected under Yellowstone. Today, we take a look at what this Yellowstone National Park ranger said is happening in Yellowstone right now.

Over 50 earthquakes have hit Yellowstone National Park in the last week, with park officials noting that one of those was the biggest of the year. It was a 3.3 magnitude earthquake, and interestingly this is happening very close to where the uplift has been detected. These Earthquake clusters have been hitting Yellowstone hard in the last few years, with a team of researchers noting that recent activity has been hitting the park deep under Yellowstone Lake.
