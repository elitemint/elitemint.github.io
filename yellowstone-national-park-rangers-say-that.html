It was a 3.3 magnitude earthquake, and interestingly this is happening very close to where the uplift has been detected. These Earthquake clusters have been hitting Yellowstone hard in the last few years, with a team of researchers noting that recent activity has been hitting the park deep under Yellowstone Lake.
