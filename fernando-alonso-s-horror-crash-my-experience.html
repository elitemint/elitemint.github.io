At the 2016 Australian Grand Prix, we would see both Ferrari's overtake the two Mercedes drivers into turn 1, a young Carlos Sainz and Max Verstappen tangle, the debut of Haas, and one incident that made my heart stop. Fernando Alonso's horror crash at turn 3. I was in attendance that day, and i was terrified after seeing Fernando's crumpled McLaren Honda up against the barrier. Thankfully Fernando Alonso and Esteban Gutierrez were okay, but that instant shift in mood at the track, is something that was so eerie to experience. Another reminder that this great sport has some scary moments.
