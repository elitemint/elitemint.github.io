TRUE - DAY 11 - SOFTEN

Day 11, Yoga Heaven! The day we officially stop pushing and forcing and learn to soften.

This quick 17 min practice is both gentle and invigorating. Take advantage of soft easy movement to explore and unlock parts of the body that need tending to. Let go of rigidity and get empowered to move in a way that feels supportive of your unique body and depth of breath.
