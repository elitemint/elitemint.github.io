Acetate cards provide a sneak peek to the inside of DIY cards with a creative element. Todays video shares three cards all using acetate in unique ways. 

We'll start off simple with flat panel card then step things up with a window otter card. Finally I'll teach you how to make a flat panel shaker card using the brand new Hello Ladybug Bundle. Regardless of which one you decide to try, these window cards will make beautiful handmade cards!
