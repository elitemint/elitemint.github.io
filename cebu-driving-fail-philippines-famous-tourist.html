I HAD A DRIVING FAIL WHILE IN CEBU! But hey, all is good, and we both really enjoyed spending time at a resort on Mactan Island in Cebu!

Mactan is famous in Cebu for its many resorts. It was great to be able to spend some time with my girlfriend here, and was wonderful to see the resorts come back to life with local tourists. Yup, being a local tourist in Cebu is always wonderful... except when you go driving around Lapu-Lapu and make a wrong turn!

Yup, I had a driving fail in Cebu.

But we had an amazing resort experience at Crimson in Mactan... A famous Philippines tourist spot!
