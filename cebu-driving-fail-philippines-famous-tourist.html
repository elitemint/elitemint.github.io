Yup, being a local tourist in Cebu is always wonderful... except when you go driving around Lapu-Lapu and make a wrong turn!

Yup, I had a driving fail in Cebu.

But we had an amazing resort experience at Crimson in Mactan... A famous Philippines tourist spot!
