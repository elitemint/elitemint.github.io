The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) and the Centers for Disease Control (CDC) have approved the mixing and matching of COVID-19 vaccine boosters. That means you don’t have to pick the same brand for your booster shot as you got for your first round of COVID-19 vaccination. Learn more about mixing and matching in this video with Cindy Williams, Vice President of Pharmacy with Riverside Health System.
