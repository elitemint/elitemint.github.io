Last season, Vipers ruled the roost thanks to the brilliance of Farouk Miya. The Cranes attacker left Vipers in the January transfer window to join Belgian outfit Standard Liège. Miya is one of eleven players training with the national football team ahead of a friendly match against Zimbabwe next Tuesday. We caught up with him and picked his mind about how he is bedding in at Standard Liège.
