The River Thames can be like a giant fleamarket or carboot sale sometimes - with all sorts of odd bits and pieces left out at low tide to find. These are things that you wouldn't look twice at in a curio shop - but somehow the act of finding them makes them precious and special!

Today I find a varied collection of objects - and then I meet up with Anna Borzello and Monika Buttling-Smith for lark in the sludgy mud.

You can follow Anna on instagram @foreshoreseashore and you can follow Monika @mudika.thames. You can follow me on twitter and instagram @tidelineart

You need a permit to mudlark on the River Thames. Details can be found on the Port of London Authority website.

Thank you for watching. See you soon.
