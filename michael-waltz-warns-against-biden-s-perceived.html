MICHAEL WALTZ: Well, we have to appreciate that we're on the verge of possibly seeing the largest land invasion in Europe since World War Two. Russia has hundreds of thousands of its soldiers massed on the Ukrainian border. Cyber attacks are increasing on their electrical grid. They don't want to antagonize the Russians. They don't want to provoke, but instead that perceived weakness and inaction and indecision is doing the exact opposite.
