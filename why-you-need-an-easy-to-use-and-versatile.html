Even if you don't need to travel as remote or as rugged as Jase from All 4 Adventure, you do need to have a brake controller on board to keep your trailer, caravan or camper under complete control.

The Tow-Pro Elite is an electric brake controller designed to suit most common trailer braking applications whilst requiring minimal dash space and is simple to install and operate.

REDARC have manufactured Electric Trailer Brake Controllers in Australia for more than 35 years.
