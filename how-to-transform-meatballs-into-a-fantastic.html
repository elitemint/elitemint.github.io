boiled potatoes - 800 g (28.2 oz)
soft butter - 100 g (3.5 oz)
black pepper - 3 g (0.11 oz)
salt - 10 g (0.35 oz)
Sauce:
tomato sauce - 300 ml (10.14 fl oz)
oil - 20 ml (0.7 fl oz)
water - 50 ml (⅕ cup)
black pepper - 3 g (0.11 oz)
salt - 5 g (0.18 oz)
basil - 3 g (0.11 oz)
Meatballs:
minced meat - 300 g (10.6 oz)
eggs - 1 piece

pressed cheese - 150 g (5.3 oz)
