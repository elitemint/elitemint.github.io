For the main feature on Episode 13 of Carp Angle we spend 48 hours with one of the biggest characters in the world of carp fishing, Frank Warwick.
Frank has had a fascinating life, he’s like a human encyclopaedia and his stories are simply second to none… aside from that he’s also an extremely successful carp angler with a wealth of experience and knowledge and so for us it was an absolute pleasure to spend a few days with him.

Joe Bloggs is back continuing his spring camping on the mighty St Ives complex and you can also expect a variety of stories from the likes of Alan Blair, Ash Bradbury & Mark Jackson.
