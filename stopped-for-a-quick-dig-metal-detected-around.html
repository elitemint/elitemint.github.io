Digger Dame goes on a solo quick dig and stops at a cellar hole that he passed by many times and finds old stuff. With the Fisher F19 and small 5 inch coil to help swing through all the iron and trash Dame was able to find some old things buried in the ground. Some cool old iron relics , a beautiful key hole escutcheon and buckles. These old cellar holes out in the woods can be surprising and you never know what you may find.
