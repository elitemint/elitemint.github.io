Today we are in Ushuaia, Argentina enjoying the sites and testing VR again!

The intention of this video is to benchmark the type of performance I am getting in more remote regions of the world. I don’t want to make these tests all about numbers, so I will focus on the overall experience in VR. I have purposely chosen this location to show what my PC and this sim are capable of in terms of performance and graphics in VR.

As we are all anticipating sim update 6, I thought it would be useful to make a few new videos which can be seen as pre-release demos. These can be useful to reference and compare against update 6 once it has been released. I will make some more videos in similar areas to try and illustrate how the sim is performing in VR. I hope this video helps you dial in your own system, and see you in the sky soon!
