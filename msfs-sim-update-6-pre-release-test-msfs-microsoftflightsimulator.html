Today we are in Ushuaia, Argentina enjoying the sites and testing VR again!

The intention of this video is to benchmark the type of performance I am getting in more remote regions of the world. I don’t want to make these tests all about numbers, so I will focus on the overall experience in VR. I hope this video helps you dial in your own system, and see you in the sky soon!
