Metal detecting a cellar hole in New England that goes back to the colonial 1700s days with the Fisher F19 metal detector. Old relics and coins from the 1700s and 1800s are usually found around these old foundations and you just have to take your time. This is how to find old places to go metal detecting and work them to there full possibility.
