Nestled in an exclusive, sought-after area, in absolute calm, the Mas du Figuier is 5 minutes from the village of Saint-Tropez, the Place des Lices, the port and the beaches of Pampelonne.

With the architecture designed to blend the elegant interior with the landscaped exteriors planted with vines, olive trees and other fruit trees, the Mas du Figuier is composed of a main residence, two guest houses and a guardian house for a total living area of over 800 sqm.
