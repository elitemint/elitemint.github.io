Training your new puppy to be comfortable on a raised bed can benefit your training in many ways. Trained similar to the touch pad, the raised bed can help with the environmental conditioning and future place bed work. Introducing your puppy to the raised bed in a positive way makes training the place easier as your dog gets older.
