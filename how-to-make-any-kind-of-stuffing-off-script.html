The holidays are nearly upon us and Sohla El-Waylly is here to show you how to make any kind of stuffing you want. She teaches easy techniques to make crisp croutons, flavorful mix-ins, and picking the right broth for moisture. Learn two recipes: a taco-inspired cornbread stuffing and a vegan version with savory mushrooms and nori. What kind of stuffing would you make? Let us know in the comments!
