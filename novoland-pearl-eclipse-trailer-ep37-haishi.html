During Hai Shi's most crucial moment, she met Fang Zhu, the imperial court's first official, and Hai Shi became Fang Zhu's disciple. Soon after, the imperial court of the Zheng dynasty devolves into anarchy, and wars with various countries erupt. But the persistent support from Fang Zhu allows the nation to see a new time of peace. Hai Shi then becomes the personal guard of the emperor and she starts to be interested in him, and some misunderstandings rise between them.
