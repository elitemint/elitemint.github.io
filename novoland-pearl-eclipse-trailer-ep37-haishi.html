[Synopsis] During the Zheng dynasty, the Merfolk were slaughtered in the wide ocean of Novoland in search of a mystical pearl that could only be gained through the tears of a young Naga girl. During Hai Shi's most crucial moment, she met Fang Zhu, the imperial court's first official, and Hai Shi became Fang Zhu's disciple. Soon after, the imperial court of the Zheng dynasty devolves into anarchy, and wars with various countries erupt. Meanwhile, Emperor Chi Zhong Xu appears unable of maintaining order in his court and appears to be a self-indulgent monarch. But the persistent support from Fang Zhu allows the nation to see a new time of peace. Hai Shi then becomes the personal guard of the emperor and she starts to be interested in him, and some misunderstandings rise between them.
