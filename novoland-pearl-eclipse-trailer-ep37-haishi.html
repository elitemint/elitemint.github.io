During Hai Shi's most crucial moment, she met Fang Zhu, the imperial court's first official, and Hai Shi became Fang Zhu's disciple. Soon after, the imperial court of the Zheng dynasty devolves into anarchy, and wars with various countries erupt. Meanwhile, Emperor Chi Zhong Xu appears unable of maintaining order in his court and appears to be a self-indulgent monarch. But the persistent support from Fang Zhu allows the nation to see a new time of peace.
