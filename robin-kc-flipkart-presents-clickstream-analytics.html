Robin KC presents "Clickstream Analytics with Apache Druid" via Bangalore Hadoop Meetups on Nov 26, 2021.

Clickstream Analytics @ Flipkart is supported by our homegrown platform Euclid. This talk explores the functional and non-functional challenges of solving clickstream analytics at scale and how Druid fits into the architecture as a keystone infrastructure piece. We also discuss certain challenges we faced and how we have worked around them in our end-to-end architecture.

Speaker: Robin KC

Bio: Robin KC is working as Senior Architect @ Flipkart with the Flipkart Data Platform team. He is deeply plugged into Stream Processing architectures at scale along with reporting and analytics. He works mostly on open source tech stacks in the Big Data domain like Spark, Flink, Kafka, Hbase, Druid, and ElasticSearch.
