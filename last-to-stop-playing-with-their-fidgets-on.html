Today the Krew are playing with fidgets and the last to stop playing with their fidgets on the couch wins. The Krew has a minute to get all the fidgets they need for the challenge. One of the bad things is that you have to leave the challenge. Do you think someone landed on that? You can also land on switching your fidget on the cartwheel or even giving one or two up. A few of the Krew landed on that and they were not very happy about it.
