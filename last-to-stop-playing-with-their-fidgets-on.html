Today the Krew are playing with fidgets and the last to stop playing with their fidgets on the couch wins. The Krew has a minute to get all the fidgets they need for the challenge. Everyone wants to win this challenge and they are all trying their hardest to stay on the couch with their fidget and playing with it. You can also land on switching your fidget on the cartwheel or even giving one or two up. A few of the Krew landed on that and they were not very happy about it.
