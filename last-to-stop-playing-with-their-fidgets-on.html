Today the Krew are playing with fidgets and the last to stop playing with their fidgets on the couch wins. The Krew has a minute to get all the fidgets they need for the challenge. They can get as many as they can carry. While on the couch they can win certain things such as a break or an extra fidget, but there are also bad things you can win on the spinning wheel. One of the bad things is that you have to leave the challenge. Do you think someone landed on that? On the couch, they had to play with their fidget the whole time, if they stop playing with the fidget they are out of this challenge. Everyone wants to win this challenge and they are all trying their hardest to stay on the couch with their fidget and playing with it. You can also land on switching your fidget on the cartwheel or even giving one or two up. A few of the Krew landed on that and they were not very happy about it.
