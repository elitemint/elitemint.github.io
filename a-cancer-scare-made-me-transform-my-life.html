DILAY, 26, from Australia, gained weight after she had her children. After giving birth, Dilay was stressed and tired so had fast food to curb her hunger while she cared for her babies. She wouldn’t eat for the whole day and then binge on everything and anything she could get her hands on. Dilay decided to change her binge eating lifestyle after having the shock of her life - a cancer scare, after the doctor noticed a lump in her neck. The mum decided to get weight loss surgery and credits it for helping her take the first step into a healthier lifestyle and losing 98lbs.
