Who’s ready for a Toyota off-road head-to-head?!

We’ve got one of the most popular Toyota SUVs around, the Land Cruiser, and it’s going up against one of the most recognisable pick-ups in the world, the Hilux! The question is, which will prove itself to be the must-have Toyota off-roader?

For starters, let’s look at the stats. The Land Cruiser comes equipped with a 2.8-litre turbo-diesel that can put down 204hp and 420Nm of torque. It’s the commercial edition, so it’s pretty much devoid of kit - it’s even only got two seats!

As for the Hilux, we’ve got our hands on the Invincible X edition, and once you look under the bonnet you’ll find… the same 2.8-litre turbo diesel as found in the Land Cruiser! However, while the power output is the same 204hp, the torque has been upped to 500Nm!

Will the increased torque prove the difference? There’s only one way to find out… LET’S GO OFF-ROAD!
