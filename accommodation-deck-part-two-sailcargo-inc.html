Separating comfort and propulsion power, the accommodation deck holds the cabins for the captain, officers and passengers above the engine room, where we store and generate power for Ceiba, really exciting achievement!
Ben and Charlie explain how the deck was built, from deck beams to hatches and other structural details, Xavier explains what's going on under this deck, and gives a preview into how Ceiba will be powered, and last but not least Sophie and Jessi share about their experience living and working at the shipyard, a beautiful multicultural experience in the tropics of Costa Rica.
