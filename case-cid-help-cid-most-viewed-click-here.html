Today, the first thrilling investigative series on Indian Television is one of the most popular shows on Sony Entertainment Television. The series consists of hard-core police procedural stories dealing with investigation, detection, and suspense. While the stories are plausible, there is an emphasis on dramatic plotting and technical complexities faced by the police.
