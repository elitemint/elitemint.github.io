Don’t you love me?” My mom couldn’t stand seeing her favorite daughter sad. “Honey, how can you say that? Of course, we love you. We love you so much!” she said as she turned to look at me. “Lucy, why don’t you accommodate your little sister? Let her have the room she wants!”
