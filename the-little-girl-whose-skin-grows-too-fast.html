NYLA is a five-year-old fashionista with a rare condition called ichthyosis - this means her skin grows too fast. Nyla, who’s from LA, cannot sweat which means she’s at risk of overheating and it’s important that she bathes several times per day to clear away excess skin. Fortunately she loves bath time and her condition certainly doesn’t stop her from being a fashion icon. If she isn’t painting her nails or shopping for clothes she’s probably modelling her trendy outfits in front of the camera.
