Made for the OU Business School in 1995, this film looks at the trend towards convergence of telecoms, television and computing and examines the strategy of major players in these sectors in the USA. The film features several extract of an interview with Alan Cawson who sets the context for the issues discussed in the film. In 1995 the huge significance of the internet was barely imaginable; Google, for example, was not launched until January 1996
