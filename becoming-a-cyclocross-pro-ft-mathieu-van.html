I don't have to explain to you who Mathieu van der Poel is, but i will anyways: he's a multiple cyclocross world champion, multiple tour de france etape winner, european mountainbike champion, etc... In this video we visit the world of cyclocross: a mix of moutainbiking and road cycling.
