Creative Metaphor



The darkness enveloping Tyco, DJ Fateh and Roman VIP as they perform the song embodies the hardship faced by Malaysians during the spread of the Covid-19 pandemic including the economic pressures throughout the Movement Control Order period.

However, the rays of light symbolize hope: red - bravery, yellow – national sovereignty, blue – unity, each giving strength to face challenges and rise again.…culminating with rallygoers from all the states in Malaysia gathering to show support to the national leader… to move forward together.




Metafora Kreatif



Kegelapan semasa Tyco, DJ Fateh dan Roman VIP menyampaikan lagu melambangkan kesusahan yang diharungi rakyat Malaysia semasa berdepan dengan penularan wabak pandemik Covid-19 termasuk tekanan ekonomi sepanjang tempoh Perintah Kawalan Pergerakan.

Walaubagaimanapun, sinaran lampu melambangkan harapan: merah - keberanian, kuning - kedaulatan negara, biru - perpaduan; memberi kekuatan untuk berdepan apa jua rintangan dan bangkit semula… dengan kemuncaknya penunjuk perasaan dari kesemua negeri di Malaysia berhimpun memberi sokongan kepada pemimpin negara… untuk maju ke depan bersama.
