We have reached the front-line of point of no return in producing contents to this groundbreaking interactive TV concept when this even more groundbreaking statement from venture capital investor, entrepreneur and author of several bestsellers, Mr. Lars Tvede is being released.
In a world increasingly aware of and affected by global warming, Tvede advocates for definite solutions to the huge climate challenges.
