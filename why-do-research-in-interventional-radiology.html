“Why do Research in Interventional Radiology” by Professor Mark Little, presented at the national Interventional Radiology Research Day 2021.

The Interventional Radiology Research Day (IRRD) 2021 was the first UK research conference dedicated to academic interventional radiology, and took place on Friday 15th October. The event was organised by the British Society of Interventional Radiology (BSIR) Trainee committee and the IR Juniors team, and is supported by the Royal College of Radiologists (RCR) and the National Institute for Health Research (NIHR) Surgical MedTech Co-Operative.
