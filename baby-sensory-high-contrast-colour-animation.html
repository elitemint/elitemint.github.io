High-contrast color animation of shapes, flowers, trains and insects with classical music that will captivate your baby's attention. This video will encourage visual and auditory development and can help stimulate, soothe and entertain your baby.

Newborn babies can see from birth, but just not as clearly as an older person can. In the first weeks and months, their vision rapidly develop. They start to see black, white, red, and other bright colors. With proper stimulation, it can help develop your child's brain and eye coordination and help increase your child's curiosity, focus, and memory.
