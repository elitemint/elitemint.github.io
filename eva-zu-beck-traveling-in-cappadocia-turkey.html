Back for you: Accompany Eva zu Beck in the Turkish region of Cappadocia. The tufa stone caves in Turkey's Göreme National Park, called fairy chimneys, attract many Instagrammers. In Avanos, Eva meets potters in their workshops, and in Ürgüp, she meets a women's cooperative which serves traditional food. After riding a horse over the plateau, her tour ends at a cool rooftop bar in Göreme. Have fun and enjoy Eva zu Beck's adventures in Turkey!

12:47 Rooftop Bar in Göreme
