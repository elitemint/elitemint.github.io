SINCE the birth of her son Power, Rahki G has been working hard on getting her body back into the shape she was known for. Rahki has always wanted to show the world her baby boy but has not been ready... Until now! Rahki Told Truly: "I decided to keep Power's face a secret for a long time but now I'm ready to show the whole entire world."
