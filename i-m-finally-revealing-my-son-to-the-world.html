SINCE the birth of her son Power, Rahki G has been working hard on getting her body back into the shape she was known for. Rahki has always wanted to show the world her baby boy but has not been ready... Until now! Rahki Told Truly: "I decided to keep Power's face a secret for a long time but now I'm ready to show the whole entire world." Today is incredibly important to Rahki, as she will be having her very first mother-and-baby photoshoot where the world will finally meet Power. Rahki knows that today is a huge deal and this has to go perfectly, she said: "This is gonna be the first time that he's in front of the camera, that the world is gonna see him, he really does look like his dad... So try to figure it out!" But will the photoshoot go to plan?
