In today's video, a customer is made fun of by rude shop owner. When the customer enters the clothing store he starts browsing through the women's section. He tells the woman that he likes the clothes in this section but the shop owner doesn't want to understand and is very rude to him. Thankfully, another saleswoman joins the conversation and explains that clothes are for everyone. Her words mean a lot to the customer so he opens up to her...
