In today's video, a customer is made fun of by rude shop owner. When the customer enters the clothing store he starts browsing through the women's section. The shop owner first assumes he must be shopping for his girlfriend. But when he tries on one of the jackets, she walks over to him alerting him that he should check out the men's section instead. He tells the woman that he likes the clothes in this section but the shop owner doesn't want to understand and is very rude to him. Thankfully, another saleswoman joins the conversation and explains that clothes are for everyone. Her words mean a lot to the customer so he opens up to her...
