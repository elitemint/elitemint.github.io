Perla Nera's pool is like no other: made of lava stone, it is a thing of beauty and is bound to be the focal point of your holiday in Sicily. Surrounded by towering palm trees, it really is gorgeous! Perla Nera has many other gorgeous corners to discover. The gardens are stunning and beautifully maintained, whilst indoors there is a truly traditional and aristocratic feeling. For an unforgettable holiday in Sicily, full of Sicilian style, choose Perla Nera!
