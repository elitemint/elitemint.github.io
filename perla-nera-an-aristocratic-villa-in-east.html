Perla Nera's pool is like no other: made of lava stone, it is a thing of beauty and is bound to be the focal point of your holiday in Sicily. Surrounded by towering palm trees, it really is gorgeous! For an unforgettable holiday in Sicily, full of Sicilian style, choose Perla Nera!
