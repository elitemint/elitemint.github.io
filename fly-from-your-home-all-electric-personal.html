Jetson one is the future of transportation and it is here Now. Well sort of. The Swedish company that builds this personal aircraft first designed a prototype 3 years ago. Now they have a production model that you can buy as a kit today.

Jetson aircraft falls under the ultralight category which means you don't need a pilot's license to fly one. Although proper training is highly recommended. This aircraft is also just for recreational use as it only has about 20 minutes of flight time. Powered by 88KW lithium-ion batteries, the Jetson can gain speeds of up to 65 mph.

Here are some of the specs of Jetson ONE
aircraft gross wight = 190 lbs
pilot max weight = 210 lbs
Power = electric
Speed = 65 mph
endurance = 20 minutes
Price = $92,000
