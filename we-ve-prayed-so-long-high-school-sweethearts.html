Cue the tears and pass the tissues because Micah and Jacob are sure to make you cry. We definitely shed some tears throughout their beautiful wedding day at The Baumberhof. From their sweet moment back-to-back before the ceremony to Jacob's powerful reaction as Micah walked down the aisle, it was an emotion-packed weekend. These two met on the worship team in junior high and it wasn't long before they became an inseparable duo. We loved listening to the sweet stories at their rehearsal dinner shared by family and friends. This was clearly a day everyone had been waiting for for a long time. And let's not forget the stunning details planned by Lauren Corp. The growing vines and terra-cotta pots brought Micah's garden-chic vision to life. It truly was a magical day.
