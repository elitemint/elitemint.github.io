Cue the tears and pass the tissues because Micah and Jacob are sure to make you cry. We definitely shed some tears throughout their beautiful wedding day at The Baumberhof. From their sweet moment back-to-back before the ceremony to Jacob's powerful reaction as Micah walked down the aisle, it was an emotion-packed weekend.
