Designing an eLearning course may be a challenge (especially if you’re not a designer!) If you want to forever prevent your designs from being outdated and better engage the learner, check out this expert webinar on eLearning course design! The session covers both design theory basics and real-time practice in PowerPoint, and will be of good use for beginners.

As a speaker, we have Andraya Goodwin, an instructional designer and blogger. She’ll provide you with 10 awesome design tips and tricks and reveal how to:

- transform your courses to be modern and more relatable to today's learner
- bring instructional design and graphic design principles together
- use shapes, icons, and images to evoke emotion
- better engage the learner and make a lasting impact!

About the presenter:
Andraya Goodwin is an instructional designer with a passion for creating fun and engaging training materials. From microlearning videos, PowerPoint presentations, printed guides, and manuals, Andraya has vast experience is refreshing learning materials to fit the needs of the modern learner. She has also mastered iSpring and even made a video series on how to design a course in iSpring and PPT from scratch.
