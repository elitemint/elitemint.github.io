So, the interviewer is asking you the question, why are you interested in this position, to assess your motivations, and whether or not you have researched the role, and their company.

3 THINGS YOU SHOULD INCLUDE IN YOUR ANSWER TO THE INTERVIEW QUESTION, WHY ARE YOU INTERESTED IN THIS POSITION?
#1. Mention the fact that you are seeking a position that enables you to grow and develop both personally and professionally.

#2. Mention that you feel this position is one that you can ADD VALUE in due to the fact your skills, qualities and experience are a match for the job description.

#3. Mention that, having researched their company, you are attracted to their values, their previous track record of success, and their ambitious plans for the future.
