🇨🇿 In the time of the Black Death as disease spread across Europe, thousands of men and women sought salvation in a small church located in Bohemia in what is now Czechia. The bodies were buried en masse until there was no room left for any more. So what did the monks at the church do in order to create more space? They decorated the church with the bones of the plague victims.

Join me as I make my away across Central Europe to the plague church and get offered a lift by a young lady from Belarus.
