Brisk walking is extremely beneficial for your health, but people often don't fully understand how fast is brisk walk or how to do brisk walking at home the right way? Ideally, a 30 minute brisk walk daily can offer you a list of health benefits. It can also play a key role in improving your heart health, helping you with weight loss, boosting your overall mood, and more. So, how to do brisk walking at home for better heart health and weight loss?
