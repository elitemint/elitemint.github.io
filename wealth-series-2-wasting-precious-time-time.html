Are You Wasting Precious Time? Time Management Tips for Wealth Seekers

In pursuing your goal to attain wealth or Financial Freedom, are really using your time productively? If your picture of Financial Freedom does not speak to your Time, it is not true Financial Freedom. It is definitely not freedom, and I don’t know if I am ready to believe such a status without taking into consideration ‘time’ is any to write home about even from a financial perspective. Can you be financially free without being time rich?

More than this, what does the use of your time look like while you are still on your journey towards financial freedom? How do you spend your time today while in pursuit of this goal? Are you using your time in a way that is keeping you from achieving this goal? Yes, you are very busy, I get it, but are you being productive, not just productive in your vocation, but in your overall goal to one day be financially free? Are you using your time efficiently?
