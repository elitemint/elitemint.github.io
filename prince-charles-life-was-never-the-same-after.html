Prince Charles and Princess Diana notoriously had a romance for the ages — and that doesn't mean it was a good one. The couple's doomed marriage captivated royal watchers and the media worldwide, casting a spotlight on the royal family in ways they weren't always happy with.

After being divorced from Diana, Charles returned to the love of his life, Camilla, Duchess of Cornwall, with the two officially marrying in 2005. But despite his long relationship with Camilla, Charles' may always be in some way defined by his notorious relationship with his first wife.
