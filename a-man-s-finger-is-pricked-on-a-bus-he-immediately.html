The bus on the bridge came to a sudden stop. Sam lurched forward from the momentum, pricking his hand on a wire in the seat in front of him. Sam suddenly got a bad feeling. Right at that moment, the portion of the bridge in front of the bus suddenly began to crack. Scared, Sam quickly told his girlfriend Molly to get off the bus. The other people on the bus followed them off.
