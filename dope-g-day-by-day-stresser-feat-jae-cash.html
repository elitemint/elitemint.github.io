Day By Day is a story giving viewers and listeners an in depth look into the struggles of young Zambian men on the come up who are hustling to make ends meet while chasing their dreams. The video was shot and Directed by the talented filmmaker Esther Beukes who has worked on numerous film sets across the continent.
