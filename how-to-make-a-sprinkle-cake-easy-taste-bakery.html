I love sprinkle cakes - they are so colorful, festive and easy to achieve. If you are new to frosting cakes this can be a great option while you are still perfecting your frosting skills. They also add a fun taste and crunch to your cake and your cake slices will look just as festive as the cake. In this video I will show you how to sprinkle an entire cake. This is an easy technique but there are some helpful tips and tricks and mistakes to avoid. You will also learn the difference between sprinkles and quins.
