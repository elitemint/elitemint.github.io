BREAKING NEWS.
Greek-Turkish relations.
GREECE. TURKEY. USA.
According to a report on the news website Proto Thema, US President Joe Biden will meet tomorrow with Turkish President Tayyip Erdogan in Rome, where they are both, for the G20 summit.
This meeting will be the first between the two presidents since US President Joe Biden was elected, and will take place in the wake of the Turkish president's decision to expel the ambassadors of ten countries, including the US one.
Thus, this meeting is expected with great interest, since the already tense atmosphere between the two countries, and for the Russian S-400 anti-missile system, which Turkey refuses to withdraw, has drive their relations, in the worst possible status than ever.
However, the information mentioned by the website says that there will be strict tones from the US president but also that the Turkish proposal for the purchase of new F-16 fighter jets will be discussed, something which, of course, the Greek side does not want to happen.
