Here's the commission I was filming/working on in New York!: https://www.domestika.org/en/courses/1755-the-art-of-mandala-drawing-create-geometric-patterns/fortyonehundred

Recently I was flown from Canada to New York by Domestika to film an online course. It was an incredible experience! Thank you to Domestika and their whole team. Also a huge thank you to Apple for sponsoring this vlog series - I filmed my journey with the newest iPhone Pro and part of my process in my art and in the online course is using the newest iPad Pro.

This is a 5-part vlog series showing you the filming of the art commission, as well as scenes around New York City! (including...a lot of dogs).

Vlog 1
Arriving in NYC, Empire State, Bagel hunting, 5th ave Cathedral, Times Square, the MET museum, showing you around the film studio for the commission!

Vlog 2
Commission filming in the studio + Brooklyn Bridge, vegan dim sum, grand central station, trying to figure out the subway, visiting the MOMA, went on a date!

Vlog 3
Headed out to film in Madison Square Park, drawing and looking at dogs with the film crew following! + Walked the Highline, NYC harbour cruise to see the statue of liberty, citi bike cruising, The Edge observatory, the Whitney art gallery, Bryant Park sunbathing, Getting lost on the subway!

Vlog 4
Commission filming + Washington Square Park, Soho, Central Park, vegan pastries at ABCv, Guggenheim art gallery

VLOG 5
Last day of filming for the commission, with a set change and outfit change! + Drawing tour around New York, 'Sex and the City' apartment, 'Friends' apartment, Top of the Rock observatory, Times Square at Night, getting my 2nd shot, flying to New Zealand!

ARTIST BIO
"Lizzie Snow is a visual artist, working under her artist name 'fortyonehundred'. Inspired by fractals in nature, she creates contemporary explorations of the mandala and free-flowing artworks.

The mediums Lizzie engages in include original artworks, internationally commissioned murals, limited edition prints & collectables, and collaborations with select brands and artists worldwide.

Lizzie has a Master of Fine Arts degree, a Bachelor of Design degree and over 150,000 followers online. Lizzie has collaborated with brands and artists including Apple, Lululemon, Converse, Peugeot, Blunt Umbrellas, TopShop, Roy Henry Vickers, AllBirds, Whittaker’s and more.

Lizzie is a New Zealander, now based between NZ & Canada."
