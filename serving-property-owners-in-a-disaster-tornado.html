Serving Property Owners in a Disaster

On Friday December 10, 2021 the US experienced one of the most deadly tornado outbreaks in its history, with over 80 lives lost.

Tornados touched down from near St. Louis all the way across to Kentucky and Tennessee.

When storms like this strike, there is a team of highly skilled catastrophe experts and contractors that mobilize to the affected area offering support, guidance and leadership.

In this multi part series we explore what it’s like to travel with teams like this and what they do to help communities recover.

In Part 1 we met with TW Mock from TAD Recovery Services, his son Andre and Matt Behrens from C3 Group to document their journey assisting affected property owner
