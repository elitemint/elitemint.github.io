Today in this rustic cooking, we decided to cook a meal for you called Bozbash. A dish common in the Caucasus in the form of a filling soup based on lamb broth. It differs from other filling soups in the obligatory ingredients in the form of chickpeas (aka chickpeas, mutton peas, nohut, nat) and chestnuts (the latter can be replaced with potatoes). You can also note such a characteristic feature of bozbash preparation as additional frying of already boiled and washed lamb.

Name of dish: juicy Meatballs.
Ingredients: Cow meat, rice, onions, turmeric, black pepper, salt, butter, potatoes, chickpeas, pickles, water, tomatoes.
Fruit syrup ingredients: Feijoa, tangerine, sugar powder, water.
