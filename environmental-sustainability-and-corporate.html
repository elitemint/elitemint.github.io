Resolving social issues through business is vital for ensuring a sustainable future. When we leverage the power of business we can — and do — make incredible strides to advance the greater good. This mindset is nothing new at Ricoh. We believe it's simply the right thing to do.

Ricoh supports the United Nation's Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) – a global partnership to address the challenges our world faces. The SDGs are the blueprint to achieve a better and more sustainable future for all. Our building blocks for these initiatives include people (society), planet (environment) and prosperity (economic activities). Learn more: https://www.ricoh-usa.com/en/about-us/corporate-responsibility-and-environmental-sustainability
