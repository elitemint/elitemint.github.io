Europa Oil & Gas (Holdings) PLC (AIM: EOG) Simon Oddie joins Proactive London to talk about the milestones achieved during the past year across its portfolio, including the key ramp up of production at the Wressle field.

Achievements such as the delivery of Wressle as a 500 barrel per day operation have laid the foundations for an exciting new phase in the company’s growth trajectory, said chief executive Simon Oddie.

He says Wressle is showing capacity to produce at significantly higher rates than the previous target and further major development potential exists in other reservoirs.
