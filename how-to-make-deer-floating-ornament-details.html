Here is a quick tutorial to show you how I make my design - "deer in snow" floating Christmas ornaments. I created it with images i found in Cricut Design Space.

I have had an amazing response to this video and wanted to update in here to include the answers to the questions I have been getting most often.

ORNAMENTS - from Michaels, Canada - 25pk - plastic - 3 inch.

SNOW - Crafters Square (brand) 01.48oz (42g bag) - from Dollerama, I think.

RIBBON - Micheals, Canada

TREE IMAGE - # M8FD812B - search in images - Cricut Design Space

TREE - Cricut Premium Permanent Vinyl

DEER - # MF37BO22 - woodland winter scene - CDS

DEER - Cricut Adhesive Foil - Stainless. Colour not on it but its kinda teal. I make this ornament with many different colours or deer and ribbon.

ACETATE CENTRE - I created this myself from a circle and welding a small square to the top to create the tab. The tab sits in the neck so that it doesn't move once inside.
Dimensions - .66x372.88x3.2 Thickness of acetate is unknown, sorry, not on the packaging and too thin to measure.
Acetate sheets are from Amazon or Michaels. 12x12 pack of 12 sheets.
No brand name on them.
Recollections and Cricut also sell acetate.

If you have any more questions , please add them in the comments below and I will be happy to answer them.
Thank you for watching and Happy Crafting!
