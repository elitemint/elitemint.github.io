Robert Bauval is specifically known for the Orion Correlation Theory (OCT), which proposes a relationship between the fourth dynasty Egyptian pyramids of the Giza Plateau and the alignment of certain stars in the constellation of Orion. Robert Bauval describes some of these further mysteries that leave the great pyramid the most strangest construct ever built on planet Earth.
