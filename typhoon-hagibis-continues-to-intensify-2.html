Typhoon Hagibis continues to put on a show of rapid intensification reaching category 4 by F-13 analysis and still has room to continue to intensify as it gets ready to bear down on the Mariana Islands

There are Typhoon warnings up for most of the Mariana Islands Tropical Storm Warnings for Rota and Guam is under a Tropical Storm Watch
