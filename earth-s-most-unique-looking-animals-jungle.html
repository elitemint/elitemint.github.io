While the animal kingdom is full of some beautiful and elegant marvels, not every animal necessarily fits that bill. In this episode of Worlds Weirdest Animals Faces, we take a look at some of Earth's funnier looking creatures that reside in the jungle, whose looks indeed serve a purpose for their survival. Like the Proboscis monkey, whose large nose acts as an echo chamber to amplify his calls, or the Three-Toed sloth, who's toes act like hooks to help them hold onto branches securely while they sleep.
