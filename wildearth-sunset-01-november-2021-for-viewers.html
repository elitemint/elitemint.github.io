November 2021 Times

The sunrise safari starts at 05:30 local time, which means start times of 23:30 EST, 20:30 PST, 03:30 in the UK [GMT], 05:30 in Central Europe, and 13:30 Sydney time.

The sunset safari starts at 15:30 CAT local time, 09:30 EST, 06:30 PST, 13:30 in the UK [GMT], 15:30 in Central Europe, and 23:30 Sydney time.

When our safari isn't live, you can view the feed from the Djuma Waterhole camera, from the heart of Djuma Private Game Reserve in the Sabi Sand/greater Kruger National Park area.
