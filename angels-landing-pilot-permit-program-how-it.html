Planning to visit Zion National Park this spring? Make sure to Plan Like a Park Ranger by understanding the new Angels Landing Pilot Permit Program.

In response to concerns about crowding and congestion on the trail, on and after April 1, 2022, everyone who hikes Angels Landing needs to have a permit. To apply for a permit, interested hikers will need to enter the Seasonal Lottery on recreation.gov following the instructions in this video.

For more information, including the program's background, details on how it works, and an audio described version of this video, visit go.nps.gov/AngelsLanding
