More than a decade ago, P&G scientists developed a powdered cleaning technology that transforms dirty water into clean drinking water. The non-profit P&G Children’s Safe Drinking Water (CSDW) Program shares the power of clean water with children and families around the world. With the help of more than 150 partners around the world, we've provided billions of liters of clean water through the CSDW Program.
