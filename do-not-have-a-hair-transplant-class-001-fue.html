Hair transplant surgery is always a gamble. Why? Because no surgery in the world can actually be guaranteed of a particular final outcome. The only guarantee you can get, if you get one at all, is that the clinic or doctor you choose for your FUT or FUE hair transplant procedure for hairloss will give you their best effort.
