Unsafe is a co-op horror survival game for up to 4 players. You and your team members wake up from your cryostasis in the “Black Monolith” station. Although you were the ones who set up the station equipment and devices, you don’t know what happened next. Your untimely awakening means one thing: everything went wrong. Investigate the area to learn more about what transpired while you were in stasis. Bypass security systems, gather data, face dangers. Stay together. Stay sane. Stay alive.
