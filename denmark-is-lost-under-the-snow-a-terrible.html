Record snowfalls hit Denmark on the first day of winter. The city of Aalborg suffered the most, where in a short time the monthly norm of precipitation fell, in the form of snow, which in some places reached 70 centimeters. Also, the snowfall was accompanied by a hurricane wind, which in some places reached a speed of 70 km / h. Traffic on the roads was severely restricted, there were hundreds of road accidents resulting in heavy traffic congestion, public transport and the airport were temporarily out of service. Utilities have been put on an emergency mode, are actively fighting the consequences of the snowfall, but so far they have not been able to cope. No information on the victims has been reported.
