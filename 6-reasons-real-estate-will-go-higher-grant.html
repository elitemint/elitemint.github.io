A lot of people are predicting another housing crash or Real Estate bubble, but I feel confident the value of properties will only go higher and here's why.
1) INTEREST RATES ARE AT AN ALL TIME LOW
Investors are investing in single family homes as a rental stock to take advantage of record low interest rates.
2) SUPPLY ISSUES
Builders can build apartments faster and make more money than a lot of single family homes.
3) THE RETURN OF THE INTERNATIONAL BUYER
When travel is more open and accommodating again, you'll see investors come in to do their due diligence.
4) MASSIVE AMOUNTS OF MIGRATION
In Florida for example, we have record numbers of people migrating from California and other States.
5) INFLATION
Since there has been global printing of money, surpassing $17 trillion, Real Estate will be affected by the inflation.
6) DEMAND FOR YIELD
With interest rates at an all time low, people are looking for a place to put their money that yields returns other than the bank.
