Scaler 2.5 New Features Complete Overview.
Davide Carbone walks us through the new features in a major update free for all registered Scaler 2 users.

This video covers all of the new Guitar Features including Chord Charts, Strummed Sequences, FRET views and new guitar sounds. Plus all the new chord sets and other content is covered.
