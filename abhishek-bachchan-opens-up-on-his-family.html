Today, on the show, we have with us Abhishek Bachchan in the house. An actor, a film producer, an entrepreneur, and someone who loves future technology. This conversation was absolutely fantastic, the energy he brings to the table is extremely humble.

He has done some amazing movies in his career. From Refugee to Dhoom series to Dostana, Abhishek has shown a different side in every character that he played.
