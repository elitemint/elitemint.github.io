The shocking death of a main character in 'And Just Like That...,' the 'Sex and the City' reboot, has many people wondering how someone who exercises regularly can still have a heart attack.

Dr. Neal Barnard joins “The Weight Loss Champion” Chuck Carroll to explain why riding a Peloton, or exercising every day, isn’t enough to keep your heart healthy. They will discuss the important role proper nutrition has in keeping your heart pumping in an optimal fashion.
