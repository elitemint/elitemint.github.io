Written by Mitch Leigh and Andrew Scott. Produced by Steve Barri.

From the 1967 album, “The Disadvantages Of You”.

Formed in New York City, the Brass Ring were led by Phil Bodner and began their chart run in 1966, when the single, “The Phoenix Love Theme” reached #32 on the Hot-100. Three other 45s followed until ““The Disadvantages Of You” returned them to the Hot-100 (#36) in 1967.

“The Disadvantages Of You” album was the Brass Ring’s fourth and reached #193 on the album chart.
