An awesome sculpture to celebrate the hopefully soon to be released Avatar Live-Action Netflix Adaptation! Here's hoping they don't mess it up like The Last Airbender movie! This is a really cool sculpture by Mag-Net over on Cults3D. This whole print turned out a little wonky but it looks good enough from a from view angle that I didn't mind! Here are some details about the print! Watch the video for a more in-depth description!

Printer: Creality LD-002R SLA Printer
Resin Brand: Elegoo
Resin Colors: ABS-Like Cool Blue, Black, and Translucent

(The base and little Aang's resin was created by mixing SLA resins, more specifically doing a 70/30 mix of Elegoo Translucent Resin and Elegoo Black Resin.)
