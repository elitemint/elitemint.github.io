Here are some details about the print! Watch the video for a more in-depth description!

Printer: Creality LD-002R SLA Printer
Resin Brand: Elegoo
Resin Colors: ABS-Like Cool Blue, Black, and Translucent

(The base and little Aang's resin was created by mixing SLA resins, more specifically doing a 70/30 mix of Elegoo Translucent Resin and Elegoo Black Resin.)
