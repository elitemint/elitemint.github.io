2021 Volkswagen Taigun launched in India! 2021 VW Taigun is the all new SUV from VW India and it rivals the MG Astor, Kia Seltos, Hyundai Creta and Nissan Kicks. 2021 Volkswagen Taigun is built on the same MQB A0 IN platform as the Skoda Kushaq. Infact, Taigun shares its platform, few body panels, engines, transmission, etc from Kushaq. Though, both cars looks very different thanks to clever design. 2021 Volkswagen Taigun available in 1.0L 3 Cyl petrol engine with MT/AT transmission. This engine is available in Taigun Comfortline Base model, Taigun Highline mid variant and Taigun Topline top model. Another engine on offer is 1.5L TSI EVO petrol engine with MT/DSG AT transmission. This performance line is available in Taigun GT MT and Taigun GT Plus DSG variant.
This 2021 VW Taigun Highline variant gets features like halogen headlamps and foglamps, chrome garnish all around, 16 inch silver alloy wheels, roof rails, rear wiper & defogger, lightbar tail lamps, rear camera and sensors, etc. 2021 Volkswagen Taigun Highline Interior gets dual tone interior theme. 10 inch touchscreen supports wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto. Connected car tech also on offer. Auto AC is present & wireless charger. All seats gets adjustable head restraints with fabric upholstery. Taigun Highline sunroof is not available.
