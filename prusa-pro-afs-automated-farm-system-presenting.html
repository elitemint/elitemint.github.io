Introducing the future of manufacturing! Prusa PRO AFS is an automated 3D print farm featuring new CoreXY Original Prusa 3D printers. It's our next-generation solution for rapid prototyping and small-batch production with smart task scheduling, Prusa Connect farm management software, automatic print collection & delivery mechanisms, hot-swapping capabilities and simple maintenance. Prusa PRO AFS live demonstration is currently on display at EXPO 2020 Dubai (until March 31st, 2022).

Learn more about Prusa PRO AFS at https://expo.prusa3d.com/
