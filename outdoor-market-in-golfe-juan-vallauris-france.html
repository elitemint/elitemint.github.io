Outdoor market in Golfe-Juan every Friday... It is similar to a flea market. You can find a lot of different stuff like clothes, shoes, bags, accessories, house wear, food & more...
I love going to the market because you can bargain & negotiate the price....
The market in the village of Golfe-Juan in Vallauris, regularly on Fridays, open at 9:00 am & close at around 12:30 pm.
