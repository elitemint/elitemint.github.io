Authorities said one person was injured in a shooting Friday evening at the Tacoma Mall food court.

Police were called to the Tacoma Mall just after 7 p.m. Friday for reports of a shooting. Officers said one victim was found with a gunshot wound, but no suspects are in custody.

More than 60 units from multiple law enforcement agencies are at the mall to investigate.

Officers urge the public to avoid going to the mall while police are at the scene.
