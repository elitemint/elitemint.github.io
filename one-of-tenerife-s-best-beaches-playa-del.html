Monika and I headed off on a ride and within two minutes I’d spotted a sight so rare, it’s an almost non existent sight in the UK- an Autobianchi A112 Abarth! This little Italian car was Autobianchi’s answer to the Mini and it really is a special sight to see it on the road as an every day car!

We then headed off for brunch at Pistacho in Los Cristianos. We highly recommend this spot- great food, very healthy and with a great atmosphere!

Over a coffee, I chat about an app that I’m a huge fan of- Readly. It allows me to keep reading all of my favourite motorcycle magazines, and magazines in general, while in Tenerife- all from my phone.

After that, we fire up the Triumph Bonneville and head over to the Playa El Duque beach. This is my favourite beach in Tenerife because of its white sand, turquoise water, very safe and lots of amenities. After that, we ended the day with a beer in Plaza el Duque shopping centre.
