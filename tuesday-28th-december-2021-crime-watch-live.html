• 100 PERSONS WERE ARRESTED AND PROCESSED BY WESTERN DIVISION POLICE OFFICERS ON BOARD THE VESSEL ON SUNDAY 26TH DECEMBER IN THE WATERS IN CHAGUARAMAS.

• POLICE ARE AWAITING ADVICE FROM THE OFFICE OF THE DPP TO DETERMINE WHAT, IF ANY, CHARGES SHOULD BE LAID AGAINST SCOON AND THOSE ON BOARD THE VESSEL.

• SCOON OBTAINED A LICENSE TO OPERATE A RESTAURANT SITUATED AT #25 QUEENS PARK WEST, PORT OF SPAIN.
