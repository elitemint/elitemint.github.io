Around the middle of each month we try to give you a short update on the latest in Cardano development, with the core architects, engineers and delivery folks delivering it!

Plus network architect, John Woods, (who joined Duncan Coutts and the architecture team a few months ago) explains some of the optimization and tuning we'll be doing as projects start to roll out on mainnet (and other fun stuff coming down the pipe). Plus Kevin talks Plutus.
