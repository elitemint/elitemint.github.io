Around the middle of each month we try to give you a short update on the latest in Cardano development, with the core architects, engineers and delivery folks delivering it!

Last week saw the release of the beta version of the PAB, integrated in to the wallet backend. in this video you get an update from Nigel on progress and what's next in the process. Plus network architect, John Woods, (who joined Duncan Coutts and the architecture team a few months ago) explains some of the optimization and tuning we'll be doing as projects start to roll out on mainnet (and other fun stuff coming down the pipe). Plus Kevin talks Plutus.
