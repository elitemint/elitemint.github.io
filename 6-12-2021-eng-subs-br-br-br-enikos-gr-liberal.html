Greek-Turkish relations.
GREECE. TURKEY.
According to the news websites Enikos.gr and Liberal.gr, and with information from the newspaper Kathimerini, the Turkish authorities arrested and detained a Greek couple, who had gone on a trip to Adrianople, while the man is a police officer.
More specifically, the couple had crossed into Turkey yesterday, from the Kipoi of Evros and the woman, according to the information, is from Feres.
The unconfirmed information says that they were arrested because they were taking photos, while the Greek side has requested official information, for the reasons of the arrest and detention.
