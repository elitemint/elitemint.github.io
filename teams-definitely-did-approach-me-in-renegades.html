Justin "jks" Savage, former star of Renegades/100T and recent player for compLexity, is the star of the 196th Episode of 'Reflections'. jks was Oceania's best player for the majority of CS:GO and was able to make a semi-finals run at the Starladder Berlin major in 2019.

In part one, jks discusses the early days of competing abroad, the team's break-out at Gfinity, other destinations he could have gone to and some of the key personnel changes in the team over the years.

This interview was recorded on the 11th of November 2021.
