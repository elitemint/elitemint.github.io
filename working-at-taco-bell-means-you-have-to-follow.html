In the world of fast food, there are no rules. Do you want to dunk your fries in your milkshake? Go ahead! Do you want to create a Frankenstein sandwich from multiple menu items? Why not! Hungry for six burritos? Go for it!

Considering how often the chain is thinking “outside the bun,” you might imagine there’s quite a bit of flexibility behind the counter. Believe it or not, these are the bizarre rules Taco Bell employees are forced to follow.
