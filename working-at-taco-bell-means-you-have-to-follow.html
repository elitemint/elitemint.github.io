In the world of fast food, there are no rules. Do you want to dunk your fries in your milkshake? Go ahead! Do you want to create a Frankenstein sandwich from multiple menu items? Why not! Hungry for six burritos? Go for it!

When it comes to Taco Bell, its menu tends to break the rules for you. Take the nacho fries, the quesarito, or the chicken sandwich taco, for example. Considering how often the chain is thinking “outside the bun,” you might imagine there’s quite a bit of flexibility behind the counter. Believe it or not, these are the bizarre rules Taco Bell employees are forced to follow.
