Within each of the evidence sessions there may be images, audio and videos of the fire. Warnings will be given before they are shown and there will be short breaks at appropriate times so that anyone who wishes to leave the hearing room or stop watching the live stream can do so.

If you are affected by these hearings and need support, please contact the Grenfell Health & Wellbeing service helpline on 0208 637 6279 (0800 0234 650 for out-of-hours support) or Hestia Grenfell Support on 0800 246 5617.

Throughout the hearings there will be short breaks during which time the Grenfell Tower Inquiry logo will be displayed.
