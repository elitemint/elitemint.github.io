KIA RIO is classified as a supermini, but it’s about the size of compact cars from the late 1990s and early noughties. The 2021 facelifted KIA Rio comes with a 1.2 normally aspirated petrol 84 hp 4-cylinder motor, and 1-liter turbocharged 3-cylinder engines producing 100 or 120 hp. The latter is a mild hybrid.
