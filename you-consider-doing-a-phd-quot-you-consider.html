"You consider doing a PhD? You should not take this decision light-heartedly! The reason is because doing a PhD is a defining moment in your academic Career and it is a personal transformation. We are a highly reputed research intensive University, we have modern infrastructure and we provide an intellectually inspiring environment. Our new Doctoral Schools provide support in many respects to make sure you can carry out research at the highest international level!"
