Located in the small hawker center, this Hokkien Mee is still sold at RM4 per bowl until today. With an additional of 50 cents you can get a large bowl and an additional of RM1 you can enjoy additional prawns or pork ribs. Selling over 10 years passing down from the mother to her daughter, this stall serves a good Hokkien Mee with a reasonable price.

Although it's priced at just RM4 per bowl, the noodles and ingredients given are generous. The taste of the soup is classic with a savoury touch. The soup is not too thick but it's flavourful with a balanced taste of prawn and pork ribs.
