Interviewees include current and former ITN figures: Andrew Gardner, Sandy Gall, Gerald Seymour, Sir Geoffrey Cox, Julia Somerville, Jon Snow, Sir David Nicholas, Trevor McDonald, Michael Nicholson, Anna Ford, Michael Brunson, Paul Carleton, Paul Davies, and Stewart Purvis.{% endraw %}
