#EN
Feel a formative experience literally with the product decorating unit (PDU) of the IMPRESSA bread. It is a completely new solution on the market and enables pressed as well as cut decoration patterns into the surface of your bakery products.

Special benefits for you are:

1. High-quality products by gentle dough sheet production
2. Processing very soft doughs with a high dough yields
3. Very precise operation thanks to a camera system
4. Highest flexibility regarding product shape & weight
5. Little floor space required
6. High performance – up to 60 cycles/minute
