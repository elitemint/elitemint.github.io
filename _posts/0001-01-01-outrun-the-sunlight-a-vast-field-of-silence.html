'A Vast Field of Silence' is the fourth LP by Chicago, Illinois (USA) based instrumental post-progressive band, Outrun The Sunlight. The album will be released on November 12, 2021 across all major platforms.

Outrun the Sunlight is
Austin Peters | guitar
Cody McCarty | guitar
Phil Kalas | bass
Kyle Kunkler | synths & keys
Luke Angle | drums
