Campus Reform reporter Addison Smith went to the University of Northern Iowa to see if students viewed these politically correct speech codes favorably.

Smith then went through several words to see if students could guess the terms that schools and organizations are suggesting as alternatives. Most students were unable to guess the alternatives.
