HaShem prepares everything well in advance to be ready for us and when it sprouts from seemingly nowhere we think that it just ‘happened’. We must attempt to look for the Hashgacha and look for the Yad HaShem in each situation. If we look for it we have a chance to see it.{% endraw %}
