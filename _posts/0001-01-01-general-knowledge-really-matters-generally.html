In an era where core academics is often taking precedence over soft skill development and general knowledge (especially in South Asia), it has become crucial to focus on enhancing GK and general awareness among students. Anansh talks about why this is important and how it can be achieved in this enlightening talk. Anansh is the Founder of SkillSphere Education - a pan Asia 21st century skill development organization focusing on developing essential soft skills and general awareness among K12 schools for over 200000 students in 400+ schools and 7+ countries.

A public speaker, public speaking coach, quizmaster and educationist, he is an alumnus of the prestigious Cornell University.

Since the past 10 years, he has been focusing on making students the best versions of themselves by developing critical soft skills. He has personally conducted workshops for over 30000+ students and teachers since 2013.
