FACE DIET : 9. BABY FACE How to Improve Color Complexion สร้างสีผิวสม่ำเสมอ
1. Use fingers to massage the spiral loop. From the middle of the forehead to the temples. Repeat 5 times.
2. Use fingertips to massage in a circle on the surface of the eye socket. Repeat 5 times.
3. Use fingers press the chin skin. Repeat 10 times.
4. Use fingers to massage in circle on cheekbones, cheek and chin. Repeat 5 times.
5. The thumb and index finger, pull and twist gently across the face skin. Repeat 10 times.
6. Rub hands palm to keep warm, apply gently on the face from inside to outside. Repeat 3 times.
