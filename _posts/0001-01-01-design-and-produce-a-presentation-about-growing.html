--- title: "Design and produce a presentation about growing food, fibre or shelter" image: "https:\/\/i.ytimg.com\/vi\/_zOvR3wSWsU\/hqdefault.jpg" vid_id: "_zOvR3wSWsU" categories: "Education" tags: ["Design","produce","presentation"] date: "2021-11-09T13:50:04+03:00" vid_date: "2015-10-22T23:57:02Z" duration: "PT4M36S" viewcount: "1464" likeCount: "9" dislikeCount: "0" channel: "Primary Industries Education Foundation Australia" --- {% raw %}This is a unit with five inquiry teaching sequences about growing our food, fibre and shelter.
This unit encourages students to investigate a primary industry and the production practices it uses to produce a food or fibre.
After identifying a primary industry to research, students gather facts and information about where their chosen primary industry grows food, fibre or shelter and students design and produce a product of their chosen item from the beginning through to the end product.
Students explore citations like ‘From seed to salad’ ‘From grass to glass’ ‘From paddock to plate’; ‘From dirt to shirt’; ‘From farm to fork’; ‘From field to fabric’; ‘From boat to belly’ and ‘From pond to plate’ and pick their own production process to summarise using visual representations.{% endraw %}
