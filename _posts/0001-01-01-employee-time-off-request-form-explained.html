If you’re hoping to secure time off from your employer in the event of medical leave, vacation, jury duty, or other reasons, it’s best to present them with a formal Employee Time-Off Request Form.

Time-off requests should be submitted with ample notice during favorable times. Employers should approve or deny requests as quickly as possible so the employee may adequately plan for occurrences surrounding their requested time off.
