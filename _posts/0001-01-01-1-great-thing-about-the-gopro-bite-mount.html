This is wave #17 of “A Wave A Day” for 2019.

The world believes all Australians are born surfers. I wanted to put this to the test, so for the entire month of January I am going to surf a wave every day, no matter the conditions, no matter the weather and no matter how I feel! I’m not a great surfer but I have access to a beach right out my bedroom door so I feel compelled to use it!
