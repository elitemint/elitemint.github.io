Wooden ships have been built and rebuilt here for a thousand years and, to some, this scruffy wharf is a magical vision of living history.
A centre for ancient maritime crafts, the quay is a haven for the few dozen surviving Thames sailing barges. But Standard Quay's latest owner, a property developer, plans to turn it into a tourist trap with shops, restaurants and luxury houses. The Quay is the story of a traditional working shipyard's final year.{% endraw %}
