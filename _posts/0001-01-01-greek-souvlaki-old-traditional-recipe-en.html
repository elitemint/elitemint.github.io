An almost forgotten taste that hasn’t lost its value. It’s the traditional Greek souvlaki of our childhood, like we, old folks now, used to enjoy it back in the day when a small skewer and a thin slice of bread used to be our snack. Tasty and juicy, freshly grilled, with a lovely taste. This is exactly how this souvlaki tasted like, it’s a forgotten taste that we chose to show you today, to bring back memories to the old folks and hopefully inspiring the younger ones to pass it to the next generation. A simple but delicious recipe! Let’s see it in every detail, step-by-step.
