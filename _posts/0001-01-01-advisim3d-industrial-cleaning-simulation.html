THE COMPANY


ADVISIM3D is a product and brand of ADVITEC Informatik GmbH, which has been managing and consulting complex IT projects for about 25 years.

The application is based on the SIMKOR research project for the development of a software for carrying out virtual cleanability tests and simulating complex spray cleaning systems. It was funded by the Federal Ministry of Education and Research and successfully implemented as a cooperative project with the partners Fraunhofer Institute for Process Engineering and Packaging (IVV) Dresden, the Innovation and Simulation Service Festenberg (ISF) and the consortium leader ADVITEC Informatik GmbH.{% endraw %}
