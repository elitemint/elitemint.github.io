THE PROSPECTIVE OF PRESENT AND FUTURE OF CANADA WIDE #Yiakil MOVEMENT.

On Sunday, 13 November 2019 voice of Eritrea presenting a show on: The prospective, of present and future by the eyes of Canada Wide movement of yiakil, with Ms Rahel Sium and Mr Samuel Habtom from Vancuber, Mr. Abrham Tekie from Manitoba and Mr Ghebrehiwet Gherezghiher from Saskatoon. The panel discussion will also focuses to the current issue in Eritrea and Ethiopia.
