Jack heads to Munich to take a look around the remarkable Mercedes Vision EQXX: a radical, experimental new electric concept car that serves as a testbed for various sustainability and efficiency-focused technologies you may one day see featured on your own EV. From glass-fibre springs to lab-grown silk, the EQXX may seem more sci-fi than road car, but this concept is no showroom queen: it’s a genuine test mule and the best clue yet about what we can expect from future e-Mercs.
