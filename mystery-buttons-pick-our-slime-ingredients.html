Today Cilla and Maddy are making slime, but the mystery buttons will pick our slime ingredients. We haven’t made slime in a while, because we are in the middle of moving, but today we are finally making some slime again and it was so satisfying. How this fun slime challenge works is that there are twenty mystery buttons and each of them will say an ingredient. These mystery buttons will say bad ingredients and good ingredients. Cilla and Maddy will get to press ten mystery buttons each. How this slime will turn out is a mystery, which you need to watch. Maddy didn’t have much luck in this challenge and didn’t really get the ingredients she wanted. It was driving her crazy. Cilla however was very happy with the mystery buttons she picked. You will see a little drama in this funny video. Maddy takes making slime very seriously and when she picked bad ingredients, she wasn’t happy at all. Cilla was teasing her with getting all the ingredients Maddy wanted. If you want to know who won this challenge, you must watch this video until the end. We hope you enjoy this video as much as we enjoyed filming it.
