This bonus weeks song is called Too Slow from the V.S Sonic.EXE Mod. The Sonic.EXE character is the main mascot or horror creepypasta that is based off on in the making of this mod. The song itself as stated in the name has a very slow begin along with the first two turns but then speeds up or picks up the pace. This song is pretty popular or well liked in the FNF community and I'm glad to finally make a video on it since I came across so many skin mods while working with the other songs. Once again I still want people to know that I did use a bot to keep things well sync because I don't intend on hiding that fact since I wanted to make this video as a fun viewing experience while delivering people exposure of new skin mods out there. Overall, I love the spooky atmosphere that this song holds. I also ran into a few issues while working with this mod which forced me to use "Botplay" option to fully play it out. Hope it looks good despite the lack of "Sick" words.
