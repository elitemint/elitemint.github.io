The day before the big game at Camp Nou, Koeman and players work out at the Ciutat Esportiva.
The team trained on Saturday morning for the first of three huge games in the same week, the one on Sunday at 9.00pm CEST against Valencia.
The Tito Vilanova field at the Ciutat Esportiva was the venue, and Ronald Koeman had the services of all the uninjured players plus Nico, Gavi and Balde of Barça B.
President Joan Laporta, vice-president for sport Rafa Yuste and director of football Mateu Alemany were in attendance.
