A charming Upper West Side co-op nestled between Riverside Park and Central Park, this pristine 2-bedroom, 2-bathroom boasts modern finishes and lovely original details. Features of this beautiful home include gorgeous oak flooring, arched doorways, oversized sash windows with noise reduction glass, airy 10-ft ceilings with custom crown molding, custom closets and built-ins throughout, and a cozy wood-burning fireplace.

Residents enter into a large foyer adorned with a walk-in coat closet. From there, the home flows into an open living, dining, and kitchen area saturated with southern light. The kitchen is equipped with custom floor-to-ceiling cabinets, sleek quartzite countertops and backsplashes, a hidden washer/dryer, and a suite of high-end stainless steel appliances.

The primary bedroom possesses a trio of outfitted closets and a private en-suite bathroom with an Onyx vanity and a step-in shower. The pin-drop quiet second bedroom overlooks a lush courtyard and has a wall of built-in storage and home office solutions. The second bathroom has a deep soaking tub.
