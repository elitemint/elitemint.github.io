A charming Upper West Side co-op nestled between Riverside Park and Central Park, this pristine 2-bedroom, 2-bathroom boasts modern finishes and lovely original details. Residents enter into a large foyer adorned with a walk-in coat closet. From there, the home flows into an open living, dining, and kitchen area saturated with southern light. The kitchen is equipped with custom floor-to-ceiling cabinets, sleek quartzite countertops and backsplashes, a hidden washer/dryer, and a suite of high-end stainless steel appliances.
