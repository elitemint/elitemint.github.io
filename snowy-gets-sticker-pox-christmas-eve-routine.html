Guys! Its almost Christmas!! Christmas eve means one more day till Santa comes with all his presents and gifts. It also means one more day till Snowy our Elf on the Shelf goes back to the North Pole. Niko Bear instantly recognizes it as a trap, but not a tape trap, a string trap. Adley and Niko find that Snowy has made a mess and she also has STiCKER POX! She must have been watching Adley videos or our newest Spacestation animation, cause how could she now that eating a sticker gave you sticker pox?!! The elf did bring a pop it book for Navey to read and she loves it.

We also invite over my family, so we could spend the day with them and Adley and Niko could hang out with their cousins.
