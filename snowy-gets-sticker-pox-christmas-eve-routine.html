Guys! Its almost Christmas!! Christmas eve means one more day till Santa comes with all his presents and gifts. It also means one more day till Snowy our Elf on the Shelf goes back to the North Pole. So Jenny and I wake up the kiddos and we head downstairs to see if we can find Snowy. Halfway down the steps we notice that something is going on. Niko Bear instantly recognizes it as a trap, but not a tape trap, a string trap. There is a string attached to some of the kiddos chocolate and it goes through the entire kitchen, around our Christmas tree and down to the honeycomb room.

Adley and Niko find that Snowy has made a mess and she also has STiCKER POX! She must have been watching Adley videos or our newest Spacestation animation, cause how could she now that eating a sticker gave you sticker pox?!! The elf did bring a pop it book for Navey to read and she loves it.

Now that we have found Snowy, its time for our breakfast routine. We are already going to have french toast, but i decided to make it all Christmas-y, by putting eggnog in them. We also invite over my family, so we could spend the day with them and Adley and Niko could hang out with their cousins. After breakfast we have dance party in our basement before heading to my moms house.

There, we decorate cookies, Navy is on a computer tracking Santa has he flies around the world, and Niko is eating the frosting. Adley and my sister Sara play all sorts of board games like mystery date and twister.After the cookies are decorated, we have on more big family fun dance party before its time to head home and get milk and cookies out for Santa Claus and Christmas!
