A huge thanks to SKYFLY for letting us come out and film the newest attraction at The Island in Pigeon Forge. Surrounded by the Mountains of East Tennessee and some of the best restaurants in Pigeon Forge including Paula Deen’s, Dick’s Last Resort, Timberwood Grill and Margaritaville this attraction will sweep you off your feet and fly you around the world. Fly over Bristol Motor Speedway and sail right down to the track while a race is happening! See Mount Rushmore, Rain Forrest, Polar caps and more.
SKYFLY is one amazing adventure in Pigeon Forge, Tennessee that may be just a little better than Epcot’s Soarnin’ at Walt Disney World!
If you are planning a trip to East Tennessee, Pigeon Forge, Gatlinburg or Knoxville…. This has to be on your to do list.
