TL;DW
- Season 24 end on 5th December
- Most likely start date (not confirmend yet) 10th of December
- If you still need to find Ethereals, use a lvl 5-10 character in Torment 6 Puzzle Rings (craft them with upgrade rare or gamble at lvl 31 with DH/Monk/WD, 1500 blood shards on average)
- I have some leftover puzzle rings and will do a little community event for people that still need them in the next days
- Make sure that you salvage all your Ethereals once you are completely done with S24 - there are lots of valuable materials inside and the weapons will disappear on season end
