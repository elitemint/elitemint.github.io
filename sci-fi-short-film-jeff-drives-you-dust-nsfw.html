More about "Jeff Drives You":
David is a sad, lonely gay man plagued by relationships past. In a random turn of events, he wins a spot in the "Jeff Fleet Beta Test," an self-driving car program meant for long journeys. At first, Jeff, the bumbling AI can't get David's attention. But once David's phone dies, the two realize they have a lot more in common than they thought. Jeff Drives You is a strange strange film about the cyclical nature of relationships, and the baggage we carry with us from one to the next.
