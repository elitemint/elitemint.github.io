Yes, that's right. They choose to behave that way because they aren't into the whole feelings thing. It's different for every person, but for example, most Sigma Males like to care less about what others think, and more about their own things. That's the way they choose to be, and it isn't worth getting mad at them for that.

Other than that, we can see Sigma males as aloof and uninterested in other people’s pursuits, which is also not true. The truth is that Sigma men do connect with people sometimes (although this might take a while), and they show interest, but only on their own terms.
