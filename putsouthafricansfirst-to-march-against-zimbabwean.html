A movement calling itself #PutSouthAfricansFirst is planning a march to the Union Buildings on the 24th of November. They plan to demonstrate against extension of Zimbabwe Exemption Permit. Zimbabwean Exemption Permit Holders Association approached the courts to declare them permanent residents as they have stayed in the country for more than ten years.
