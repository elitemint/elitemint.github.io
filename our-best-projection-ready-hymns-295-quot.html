I.
Hear ye the Master's Call
Give Me thy best
For be it great or small
That is His test
Do then the best you can
Not for reward

II.
Wait not for men to laud
Heed not their slight
Winning the smile of God
Brings its delight
Aiding the good and true
Ne'er goes unblest
All that we think or do,
Be it the best

III.
Night soon comes on a pace
Day hastens by
Workman and work must face
Testing on high
Oh, may we in that day
Find rest, sweet rest
Which God has promised those
Who do their best

Chorus
Ever work for Jesus will be blest
But He asks from everyone His best
Our talents may be few
These may be small
But unto Him is due our best, our all
