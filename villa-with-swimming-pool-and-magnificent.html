The villa, immersed in the Mediterranean scrub and located 500 meters from the sea, enjoys total privacy.

The sleeping area consists of 4 double bedrooms and 4 bathrooms.

The property is completed by the 2,000 sq m park which is characterized by the presence of a wooded part and a part of the garden, in this splendid setting there is the magnificent panoramic infinity pool with solarium.
