ABOUT SWITCH MUSIC
Switch Music is the musical expression of Switch, the student ministry of Life.Church. We are a collective of worship pastors who are passionate about creating songs that combine a modern sound with the truth of the Gospel.

As pastors and songwriters, we really believe our purpose through Switch is to shine light on God’s truth in the middle of life’s difficulties. Anxiety and depression are real-life issues, and we shouldn’t downplay or cover it up. It’s exactly what Christ came to save us from. So, we’re not going to dance around the hard issues. We are committed to writing songs of prayer to cling to, especially when life gets tough.

ABOUT LIFE.CHURCH
Life.Church is a multi-site Christian church that meets in 34 locations in 10 states and globally at Church Online. Our Senior Pastor Craig Groeschel founded Life.Church 24 years ago with the mission of leading people to become fully devoted followers of Christ.
