When it comes to coffee with a view, you cannot beat Vintage Paris in Branson, Missouri! But pulling off I had no idea what I was in store for as I learned they also have seasonal drinks for each holiday including Halloween (I visited in October). Come check out what makes this shop a bit more than your average coffee shop and how its view easily trumps any other coffee shop you will likely ever visit!! Come for the coffee, stay for the view!
