DUPLICATE is a twisty, mind-bending Freudian-fuelled science fiction story about a man who shares his body with his twin brother, stabilized by an experimental deep-brain stimulation device. FIGHT CLUB meets ETERNAL SUNSHINE OF A SPOTLESS MIND meets BLACK MIRROR, Duplicate is an engaging and pacey physiological thriller staring Ansel Elgort, Patricia Clarkson, Matt Bomer and Suki Waterhouse.
