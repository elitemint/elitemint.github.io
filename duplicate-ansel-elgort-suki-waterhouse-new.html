DUPLICATE is a twisty, mind-bending Freudian-fuelled science fiction story about a man who shares his body with his twin brother, stabilized by an experimental deep-brain stimulation device. Living a comfortable balanced life with each other, they manage to keep their condition a secret from everyone. At least until one of them wants more and starts an undisclosed relationship with a woman.

FIGHT CLUB meets ETERNAL SUNSHINE OF A SPOTLESS MIND meets BLACK MIRROR, Duplicate is an engaging and pacey physiological thriller staring Ansel Elgort, Patricia Clarkson, Matt Bomer and Suki Waterhouse.

Coming to UK screens on January 21 2019.
