Meanwhile I was able to create a video on the most talked about topic in Switzerland grocery shopping! As eating out is incredibly expensive most Swiss and foreign immigrants prefer to cook. Hence, having a basic idea on availability of food and price differences helps you budget and plan ahead of your trip. I decided to shoot a video comparing Aldi and Lidl which are considered low end grocery stores in Switzerland. The two big ones coop and migros will be uploaded on another video.
