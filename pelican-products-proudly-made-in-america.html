At Pelican, we strive to create durable, reliable products that last a lifetime, built by American workers. Here are just a few of the reasons that producing our products within America leads to direct and indirect benefits for our company and our customers.

More Jobs and A Stronger Economy: Buying American-made goods doesn‚Äôt just support the workers who make those goods, but ripples out through the entire economy. Lower Carbon Footprint: Products made overseas have a higher carbon footprint than U.S.-made goods. Goods made in China or India have to be shipped across the ocean to reach American stores, burning fossil fuel and emitting greenhouse gases with every mile they travel.
