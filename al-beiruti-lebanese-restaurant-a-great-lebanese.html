Welcome to Al Beiruti, a new chapter redefining the world of
Lebanese Restau-Cafés, bringing bits of Beirut everywhere it goes.
Beyond its thrilling energy and its urban flair that is unmistakably Lebanese, Beirut will forever be known for its scenes of endless tablescapes of mezzes and cafés fragrant with that alluring mist from an afternoon well-spent with friends that are , over a perfectly-crafted shisha.
Honoring its namesake, Al Beiruti embodies all things Beirut and brings the city’s undeniable soulfulness everywhere it goes, serving memories and aspirations while mixing the old with the new.
Setting its own culture by combining those elements one looks for in both a perfect restaurant and a homey café, the Al Beiruti Restau-Café experience sets the tone once you take your first steps through the door: the chatter of a crowd while they indulge in impeccably-executed Lebanese dishes and international favorites, a Show Kitchen buzzing with spirit where the chefs blend into the dining experience, and, last but certainly not least, that world-famous Lebanese hospitality with an unparalleled attention to detail making you feel right at home.
Al Beiruti is an all-day dining destination with something for every part of every day. Bright mornings over freshly-made Mankoushé and that perfect cup of coffee, long lunches with the family, afternoons with friends animated by the sounds of Tawlé games, and loungy evenings well spent over a Shisha crafted to perfection by Al Beiruti’s very own Shisha Master.
