Borneo is the third-largest island in the world, and it is located at the centre of Maritime Southeast Asia. The island is divided between Indonesia, Malaysia and Brunei. The capital city, Kuching, is the largest in Sarawak, the economic centre of the state, and the seat of the Sarawak state government. Sarawak is home to 27 ethnic groups.
