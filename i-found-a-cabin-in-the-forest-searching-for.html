He has always known I’d love to have my own home. It is such an exciting prospect, though nothing is set in stone. When my mother moved to this area she got a fantastic deal on a little house (which made me quite hopeful), and shortly after the prices went crazy and we started to think that owning a home wouldn’t happen, so we are incredibly grateful for the chance to renovate this barn.
