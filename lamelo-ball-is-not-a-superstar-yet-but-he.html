LaMelo Ball and the Charlotte Hornets were down 23 points in the second half before mounting a huge comeback, eventually beating the Indiana Pacers 123-122 last night. Ball finished with 31 points and made 7-of-9 three-pointers. After the game, Hornets' Head Coach James Borrego said quote: 'He takes his game to another level when he’s making shots.' Skip Bayless and Shannon Sharpe discuss how big of a star LaMelo will become after leading his team to a big comeback win.
