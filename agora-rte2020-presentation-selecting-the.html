Description:

Knowing the performance characteristics of the H.264, HEVC, and AV1 video codecs are critical to achieving interactivity at scale for your users. In this presentation and panel discussion, we'll cover each video codec's functional differences and provide examples of ideal use-cases and applications. Attendees will gain actionable insights into how to tune an encoder for the best performance. Join this session to hear from some of the foremost experts in live video streaming and video conferencing and discover how you can improve your video interactivity by choosing the right video codec.
