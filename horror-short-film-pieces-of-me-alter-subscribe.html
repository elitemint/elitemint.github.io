More About "Pieces of Me":
A struggling young starlet discovers that the best way to obtain the perfect Hollywood body is by consuming other people's body parts. After a terrible audition, Natalie's agent tells her that this time it's her acting chops that aren't cutting it. In order to land the role of her dreams, Natalie must dig deep into the character and understand the anguish of a grieving mother. Without any experiences of her own to draw from, Natalie visits the grieving mother of one of her victims in order to eat her brain and steal her memories.
