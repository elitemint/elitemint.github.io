Tayfields is an elegant collection of 1, 2, 3 & 4 bedroom apartments and houses in a beautifully landscaped setting.

All new homes are finished to the very highest standards with contemporary open-plan living areas, superb bespoke designer kitchens and luxurious bathrooms.

Tayfields offers the best of both worlds, with a desirable, well connected location and a leafy, village style setting. Attractive parkland is at the heart of the development, with a large water feature and adjacent play area, a network of waterways, and extensive planting and green space.

The charming town centre of Bury St Edmunds, with its picturesque medieval streets and squares, is a short stroll from the development and offers an excellent choice of independent and boutique shops alongside high street brands. The station is only a 7 minute walk which offers direct links to Cambridge and Peterborough.
