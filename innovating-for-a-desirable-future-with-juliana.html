Designing futures can be a tricky endeavour. Too often future creations are jeopardised because they are either following an unconscious bias, replicating parameters of the past, or even just following technological evolution. These flaws in future design can lead us to unwanted systems.  This session explored how to design desirable futures and influence ecosystems. In this session Juliana discussed:

- How to design a possible and preferred future.
- How to practice speculative design

Juliana Proserpio is an entrepreneur and educator. She is the Co-Founder and Chief Design Officer of ECHOS, an innovation lab and its business units: Echos - School of Design Thinking – a school that puts innovation in practice, and Echos – innovation projects.
