In the final episode of the series, Era is joined by Premier League winner Joleon Lescott. Growing up in a footballing household Joelon started his career at Wolves, before moving to Everton and eventually Man City where he would be part of their first ever Premier League title capped off by that legendary Aguero goal.

Now a pundit and England under-21's coach, Joleon opens up about the abuse he has received and his tumultuous time at his boyhood club Aston Villa and more.
