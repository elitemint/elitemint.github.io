Fishing offshore of Jupiter Florida, the deep sea fish can't resist our colorful metal jigs! In today's video myself and @Landshark Outdoors go in search of pelagic fish and bottom fish that will eat a variety of jigs. This Catch Clean and cook I create a delicious dinner from one of the pelagic fish we catch.
