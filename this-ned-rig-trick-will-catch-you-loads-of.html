You already know the phrase... #NedRig4Life

I have made numerous videos on Ned Rig retrieve techniques & tips but this one is a bit different. On this fall fishing trip, I was planning to power fish with larger gear but ended up stumbling on this pattern (which I have done several times). Let the Ned Rig settle into the vegetation a bit, rip it out, and pause... BAM - the bass can't resist when you rip it out and pause it, especially in the fall with a baitfish imitating Ned Rig.
