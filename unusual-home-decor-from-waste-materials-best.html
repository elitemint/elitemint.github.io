Unusual home decor from waste materials. Best out of waste. Today I will show you how to make an amphora from waste material. Junk material is essentially garbage, but don't rush to throw it away, you can make wonderful crafts out of it!

From waste material with their own hands, at times, real masterpieces are created. And we will make a decorative amphora that will decorate the room.

To make an amphora you need:
• Newspapers;
• Masking tape;
• Empty reel of scotch tape;
• Cardboard tube;
• Papier - mache;
• Acrylic paints and varnish.

In a few hours of work, you get a wonderful craft from junk material!
