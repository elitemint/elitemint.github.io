Today Cilla and Maddy are going shopping, but this challenge will have a twist they have to guess each other’s budget. Mom pulled Cilla and Maddy aside separately and asked them for their budget, but she told them not to share their budget with each other. Maddy and Cilla had to guess each other’s budget while going shopping. If one of the girls went over the budget, they had to return the things they bought, but if they were under the budget or right on the budget, they were able to keep the things they bought. Maddy was trying to cheat. Woojin knew the budget and was shopping with Maddy and mom. Maddy tried to run away from mom so she could ask Woojin what budget she has. Woojin didn’t budge at all. Mom eventually caught up to Woojin and Maddy. Maddy knew Cilla would not pick a high budget because she doesn’t like to spend money. However, Cilla also knew that Maddy loves to spend money. Maddy had a very hard time picking the things she wants to buy knowing she probably doesn’t have a high budget. Do you think Cilla and Maddy guessed the budget, or did they go over the budget? You must watch and see until the end. We hope you enjoy this video as much as we enjoyed shopping.
