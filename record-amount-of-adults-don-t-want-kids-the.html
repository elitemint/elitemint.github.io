The Pew Research Center just published a study concluding that birthrates have again fallen this year, continuing the recent six-year trend and drawing attention to the possible long-term effects on the economy and government policy if this trend continues. Cenk Uygur and Ana Kasparian discuss on The Young Turks. Watch LIVE weekdays 6-8 pm ET. http://youtube.com/theyoungturks/live

Read more HERE:
https://www.washingtonpost.com/nation/2021/11/21/americans-childless-pew-us-population/

"More U.S. adults who do not already have children are saying they are unlikely to ever have them, a new Pew Research Center survey finds — findings that could draw renewed attention to the risks of declining birthrates for industrialized nations.

Experts are concerned that the U.S. birthrate, which has declined for the sixth straight year, may not fuel enough population growth on its own to keep the future economy afloat and fund social programs."
