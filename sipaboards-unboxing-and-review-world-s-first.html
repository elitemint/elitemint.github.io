The SipaBoards Tourer Drive is a board built for more. Faster, lighter and longer than other Drive Collection models, this spear-headed SUP will glide through streams wherever you take it. Sleekly cut for easy maneuverability and low drag, the Tourer Drive is still a well balanced and considerably stiff board. A perfect choice for experienced paddlers and those who cannot stop exploring the coastline.
