Today we are unboxing and testing SIPABOARDS - World's First Self-inflating Electric Powered SUP board. SIPABOARDS propulsion unit allows you to ride up to 3 hours with a top speed of 5 km/h - a fun ride for your whole family!

The Tourer Drive Super Pack is an all-in-one limited edition SUP set that will take you further.

The SipaBoards Tourer Drive is a board built for more. Faster, lighter and longer than other Drive Collection models, this spear-headed SUP will glide through streams wherever you take it. The extended waterline allows the SipaDrive to develop up to 7 km/h and add some power so that your rides can become full size explorations.

Sleekly cut for easy maneuverability and low drag, the Tourer Drive is still a well balanced and considerably stiff board. A perfect choice for experienced paddlers and those who cannot stop exploring the coastline.

The SipaBoards Tourer Drive Set includes:

12′ MSL drop-stitch inflatable standup paddleboard
3 pc ultralight carbon fibre paddle (749 g)
Fully integrated jet engine and built-in compressor – the SipaDrive®
144 Wh Battery pack
Wireless paddle-mount remote
Battery pack for the SipaDrive and charging cable
Deluxe carrying backpack
Board carrying strap
Front and rear carry handles
10’ Coiled Safety leash
Free Mobile App

more info on https://sipaboards.com/
