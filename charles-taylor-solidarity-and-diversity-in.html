In October 2011 at the University of Winnipeg, renowned author and philosopher Dr. Charles Taylor presented "Solidarity and Diversity in a Secular Age: Managing Belief and Unbelief in the Public Square," a lecture based on his 2007 Magnum Opus "A Secular Age"



The lecture was presented by the Knowles-Woodsworth Centre for Theology and Public Policy, the U of W Politics Department and The Uniter's Mouseland Press Speaker Series.
