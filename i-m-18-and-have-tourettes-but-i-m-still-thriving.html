CECE Jaye, from Evesham in the UK, has Tourette Syndrome and constantly suffers from symptoms, which include motor and vocal tics, meaning she’s prone to sudden jolts of movement and outbursts. To combat misinformation on the condition, Cece began uploading educational and humorous videos to her social media accounts, with the idea that she could educate people about her condition and how to behave around someone with Tourettes. Despite being in regular pain because of the constant movement, Cece’s learned how to live with the condition and isn’t letting it deter her from accomplishing her future ambitions, which include getting to a million followers on TikTok.
