Tentart is a 3-day arts festival which incorporates a number of activities and installations following El Greco’s motto: “art is everywhere you look for it”.
It provides a collective experience in a festival which reinforces participation, interaction with the audience and includes happenings, movie viewings, theatre, live concerts, yoga, games, workshops, with an attitude towards extroversion and hospitality.
Tentart Festival takes place in the park of Agia Varvara springs in the city of Drama, an area of exquisite natural beauty, which has been declared a ‘historical site’ within the historic city centre by the Ministry of Culture.
It is sponsored by private funds, and the Ministry of Culture of Greece, organised and produced by ART'n ROLL a non profit cultural organisation.
