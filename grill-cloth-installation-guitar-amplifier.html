Grill Cloth Installation - Guitar Amplifier - Cabinet Building - DIY Stretching Speaker Baffle

If you've got a ratty, saggy, grill cloth or are building an amp cabinet from scratch this video will show you how it's done. Straight lines and tight cloth are the jam. It doesn't take many tools or much time at all to get perfect grill cloth.

You'll need:

Speaker baffle
Grill cloth
Straight edge ruler
Boxcutter
Stapler
Heat gun
Sturdy work surface
Eye protection

Have fun and if you mess it up just take out the staples and try again!
