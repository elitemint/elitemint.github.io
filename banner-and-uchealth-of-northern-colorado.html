Leaders from Banner Health and UCHealth northern Colorado provide a virtual town hall (Thursday, Nov. 18, 2021), to learn more about how COVID-19 is impacting Larimer and Weld County hospitals.

Regional presidents Margo Karsten from Banner Health and Kevin Unger from UCHealth, and physicians from both systems update the community on how they are addressing the COVID-19 pandemic. Topics include trends in hospitalizations, ICU admissions, vaccination and its impact in reducing the severity of illness, and what the community can do to help.
