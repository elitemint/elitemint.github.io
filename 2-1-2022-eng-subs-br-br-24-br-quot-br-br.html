Greek-Turkish relations.
GREECE. TURKEY. CYPRUS.
As reported on Friday, the correspondent of the SKAI TV station in Turkey, Manolis Kostidis, the Turkish Foreign Minister, Mevlut Cavusoglou, during his interview, on the Turkish channel 24, stated that if Greece does not demilitarize the islands, their sovereignty will be discussed, unfolding now clearly, what are the real goals of Turkey, all these years.
And these are none other than seizing our islands, as well as the expression "Their sovereignty will be discussed", it is understood that Turkey will not "discuss" with anyone, but on the pretext that they are not respecting the Lausanne agreement and Paris, will move with unilateral action.

Let no be mistaken. The Turks want first half of the Aegean and the islands enclosed in it and then the rest of it.
Thrace, half of Crete, that is, whatever they can grab.
