Lexus has always been committed to the spirit of innovation and following the announcement earlier this year that 20 new products will be launched in the next five years, today we would like to share the All-New Lexus LX.

The LX is a one and only SUV that combines reliability, durability and rough-road drive all whilst maintaining high level performance and comfort for the rider. We have maintained the traditional body-on-frame structure whilst renewing its characteristics by adopting the new GA-F platform and achieving a digitally developed high-rigidity body.

We have also extended the lineup of various grades, including a new VIP grade and improved security with our first fingerprint-authentication push-start switch.

All changes present in the new LX are a bold next step into the future of Lexus.
