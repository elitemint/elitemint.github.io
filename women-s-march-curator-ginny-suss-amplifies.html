Ginny Suss is a producer. She produces concerts, movies and shows all highlighting the talents of worthy individuals. She is the consummate champion of others, eternally behind-the-scenes. Until now. This episode of MAVENS brings Ginny center-stage to discuss her role as The Roots tour manager, producing headlining acts all over the world, and most recently, curating the music for the historic 2017 Women’s March on Washington.
