The League of Women Voters of the Morristown Area and the Randolph PTO Council co-hosted a debate on Oct 25, 2021 for candidates running for seats on the Randolph Twp, NJ School Board. Six candidates are vying for three seats: Thomas Duffy, Peggy Kurtz, Michael LoCascio, Allison Manfred, and Yong Wang.
