Recent reads and new acquisitions for the month of May included a slew of European comics: The Moomin Deluxe Anniversary Edition; Hugo Pratt's The Man From the Great North; the first volume of Jerome K Jerome Bloche adventures; and an anthology of Spanish comics all feature in this video.
