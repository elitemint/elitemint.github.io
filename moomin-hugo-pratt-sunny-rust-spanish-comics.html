Recent reads and new acquisitions for the month of May included a slew of European comics: The Moomin Deluxe Anniversary Edition; Hugo Pratt's The Man From the Great North; the first volume of Jerome K Jerome Bloche adventures; and an anthology of Spanish comics all feature in this video. But Americans Royden Lepp (Rust Vol 4) and Noah Van Sciver (Fante Bukowski) also appear, as do the rest of of the volumes of Taiyo Matsumoto's excellent Sunny, from Japan.
