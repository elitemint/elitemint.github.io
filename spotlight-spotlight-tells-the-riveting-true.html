Spotlight tells the riveting true story of the Pulitzer Prize–winning Boston Globe investigation that would rock the city and cause a crisis in one of the world’s oldest and most trusted institutions. Directed by Academy Award® nominee Tom McCarthy (Best Original Screenplay, Up, 2009), Spotlight is a tense investigative thriller, tracing the steps to one of the biggest crime stories in modern times.
