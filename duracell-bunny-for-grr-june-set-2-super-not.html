GRR radio returned once again, for another Friday night of madness, but, with social distancing rules relaxing slightly, we were able to put together a bit more of a party event.

I was last on the lineup this time round so was able to extend my half hour slot into an hour using some of my extra time. However, I like the choons and it was so much fun, I couldn't not upload!
