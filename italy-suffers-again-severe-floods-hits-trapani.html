A cyclone impacts on Italy with floods in Sicily and Calabria.

Four people were also saved from floods in Trapani. In the provincial capital, the violent rains caused various damage and flooding. Several buildings were evacuated.

Schools have been closed in many regions in eastern Sicily, from Messina to Catania and Acireale.
The same provision is in many regions of Calabria, as a Catanzaro and Reggio Calabria.

Rains, thunderstorms and gale force winds will continue to hit the Ionian slopes. Meteorological agency reported more than 300 mm of rain fell in 24 hours. Red Alert is also for Basilicata and Puglia.
