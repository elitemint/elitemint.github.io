Chiropractic is useful in that we can set and align the boney structures of the spine (subluxations) and provide relief for this type of patient. I often advise not try and attempt to adjust the structures of the the neck while I'm treating them as this doesn't allow me to properly evaluate my work when bones are being manipulated constantly by the patient.
