Because of the escalating fishing dispute between the UK and France, Boris Johnson has been encouraged to look into enacting a blanket ban on all EU vessels entering British seas. New polling data reveals that Britons disagree of Jersey issuing new fishing licences, and that they would prefer restrict all of France's fishing vessels from UK waters in order to deescalate post-Brexit tensions.
