In 2021, the tokenization industry start growing exponentially, with the number of tokenization platforms and projects exploding.

Learn about the reasons for this growth and the future of the market from the new Stobox Interview with Mohsin Masud — Founder & CEO at AKRU, experienced real estate investment executive who recognized the benefits of blockchain and launched a platform for fractionalized real estate investment.

Tokenization is mainly associated with the process of converting some form of assets into a token that can be issued, transferred, recorded, or stored on a blockchain system.

What are the benefits of real estate tokenization for issuers and investors?

🔸 Better liquidity
🔸 Lower entry cost
🔸 Standardization of transactions via smart contracts
🔸 Greater transparency
🔸 Top security

The tokenization of real estate property, one of the principal use cases of blockchain in real estate, has yielded tremendous results. It helps asset and fund owners raise their capital more efficiently and provides investors with almost unlimited access to private real estate investments, along with better liquidity, greater transparency, and other eye-catching benefits.

By leveraging Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT), it increases trust through greater transparency. Blockchain also expedites contract processes, saves time, and reduces costs.

As tokenization in real estate gathers pace and becomes an ever more popular use of blockchain, plenty of companies and startups around the world getting involved in the issuance and selling of real estate-backed tokens
