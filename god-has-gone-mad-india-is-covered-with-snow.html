Fresh snow fell in many parts of Himachal Pradesh and Uttarakhand as weather conditions in northern India began to change in preparation for the cold winter. The Manali-Leh highway was closed to normal traffic due to fresh snow at Baralacha Pass in the Lahaul and Spiti region of Himachal Pradesh. At least 80 passengers were stuck in this area. Extreme weather events against the backdrop of a delayed end to the monsoons over the past several weeks have raised the alarm about the extremely cold conditions in India's northern belt. Experts warned that due to the La Nini effect, the temperature of the mercury would drop to 3 degrees Celsius. India's Meteorological Department has already predicted this week heavy rainfall, thunderstorm snowfalls, gusty winds and lightning in isolated locations over Jammu and Kashmir, Ladakh, Gilgit-Baltistan, Muzaffarabad, Himachal Pradesh and Punjab.
