Fresh snow fell in many parts of Himachal Pradesh and Uttarakhand as weather conditions in northern India began to change in preparation for the cold winter. Many people were stuck in the Himachal Lahaul and Spiti area due to inclement weather as the roads remained blocked. Extreme weather events against the backdrop of a delayed end to the monsoons over the past several weeks have raised the alarm about the extremely cold conditions in India's northern belt. Experts warned that due to the La Nini effect, the temperature of the mercury would drop to 3 degrees Celsius. India's Meteorological Department has already predicted this week heavy rainfall, thunderstorm snowfalls, gusty winds and lightning in isolated locations over Jammu and Kashmir, Ladakh, Gilgit-Baltistan, Muzaffarabad, Himachal Pradesh and Punjab.
