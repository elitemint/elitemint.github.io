When levelling an object or marking out, poor lighting conditions can slow work down. Now the 196-2 LED with its illuminated vials lets you take accurate readings and keep working – the job gets done. Because the purpose of our levels is to be accurate and stay accurate. Suitable for all craftsmen who often need to work in the dark or in poor visibility conditions, especially when installing heating, in reinforced concrete construction or electrical installations.

Product advantages
- Ultra-rigid aluminium profile with reinforced ribs for high stability and finger-grip opening for easy use
- Two integrated LED light sources ensure intense, balanced illumination for optimum readability of horizontal and vertical vials, even under poor visibility conditions.

- End caps with an anti-slip stopper – for a firm seat when marking.
