When levelling an object or marking out, poor lighting conditions can slow work down. Because the purpose of our levels is to be accurate and stay accurate. Suitable for all craftsmen who often need to work in the dark or in poor visibility conditions, especially when installing heating, in reinforced concrete construction or electrical installations.

- Ultra-rigid aluminium profile with reinforced ribs for high stability and finger-grip opening for easy use
