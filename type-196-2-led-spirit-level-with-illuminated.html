When levelling an object or marking out, poor lighting conditions can slow work down. Now the 196-2 LED with its illuminated vials lets you take accurate readings and keep working – the job gets done. The tough reinforced aluminum profile together with the STABILA locked vial system is the ideal combination on any harsh building site. Because the purpose of our levels is to be accurate and stay accurate. Suitable for all craftsmen who often need to work in the dark or in poor visibility conditions, especially when installing heating, in reinforced concrete construction or electrical installations.

Product advantages
- Ultra-rigid aluminium profile with reinforced ribs for high stability and finger-grip opening for easy use
- Two integrated LED light sources ensure intense, balanced illumination for optimum readability of horizontal and vertical vials, even under poor visibility conditions.
- Automatic switch-off to protect battery life.
- End caps with an anti-slip stopper – for a firm seat when marking.
- Shock-absorbing end caps.
- Removable end caps – exact positioning and marking right up into the corners.
- Spirit level with two precision-milled measuring surfaces – no discolorations on the surface.

- Equipment: 1 illuminated horizontal vial, 1 illuminated vertical vial, second vertical vial is not illuminated, 2 milled measuring surfaces, finger-grip opening.
